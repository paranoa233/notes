#### 9.1.6 练习题

1. 计算下列不定积分:

(1) \( \int \frac{x}{1 + {x}^{4}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) (2) \( \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{x\left( {1 - x}\right) }} \) ;

(3) \( \int \ln \left( {1 + {x}^{2}}\right) \mathrm{d}x \) ; (4) \( \int \frac{\arctan \sqrt{x}}{\sqrt{x}\left( {1 + x}\right) }\mathrm{d}x \)

(5) \( \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{{\mathrm{e}}^{x} - 1} \) ; (6) \( \int \frac{x\ln \left( {x + \sqrt{1 + {x}^{2}}}\right) }{{\left( 1 + {x}^{2}\right) }^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) ;

(7) \( \int \frac{x{\mathrm{e}}^{x}}{{\left( 1 + x\right) }^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) (8) \( \int \frac{1 + x}{x\left( {1 + x{\mathrm{e}}^{x}}\right) }\mathrm{d}x \)

(9) \( \int {\ln }^{2}\left( {x + \sqrt{1 + {x}^{2}}}\right) \mathrm{d}x \) ; (10) \( \int \frac{{\mathrm{e}}^{\arctan x}}{{\left( 1 + {x}^{2}\right) }^{\frac{3}{2}}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) .

2. 用配对积分法计算下列不定积分:

(1) \( \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1 + {x}^{2} + {x}^{4}} \) ; (2) \( \int \frac{{\mathrm{e}}^{x}\mathrm{\;d}x}{{\mathrm{e}}^{x} + {\mathrm{e}}^{-x}} \) ;

(3) \( \int \frac{b\sin x + a\cos x}{a\sin x + b\cos x}\mathrm{\;d}x\;\left( {a \neq  b}\right) \) ； (4) \( \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1 + {x}^{3}} \) .

3. 通过计算不定积分

\[
\int \left( {{\cos }^{4}x - {\sin }^{4}x}\right) \mathrm{d}x\text{ 与 }\int \left( {{\cos }^{4}x + {\sin }^{4}x}\right) \mathrm{d}x,
\]

进而求出不定积分

\[
\int {\cos }^{4}x\mathrm{\;d}x\;\text{ 和 }\;\int {\sin }^{4}x\mathrm{\;d}x.
\]

4. 导出计算下列不定积分的递推公式:

(1) \( \int {\sin }^{n}x\mathrm{\;d}x \) (2) \( \int {\tan }^{n}x\mathrm{\;d}x \)

(3) \( \int {\sec }^{n}x\mathrm{\;d}x \) (4) \( \int \frac{1}{{x}^{n}\sqrt{1 + {x}^{2}}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) .

5. 试求: (1) \( \int x{f}^{\prime \prime }\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \) ; (2) \( \int {f}^{\prime }\left( {2x}\right) \mathrm{d}x \) .

6. 设对任意正整数 \( m, n \) ,定义 \( I\left( {m, n}\right)  = \int {\cos }^{m}x{\sin }^{n}x\mathrm{\;d}x \) ,证明:

(1) \( I\left( {m, n}\right)  =  - \frac{{\sin }^{n - 1}x{\cos }^{m + 1}x}{m + n} + \frac{n - 1}{m + n} \cdot  I\left( {m, n - 2}\right) \) ;

(2) \( I\left( {n, n}\right)  =  - \frac{\cos {2x}{\sin }^{n - 1}{2x}}{n \cdot  {2}^{n + 1}} + \frac{n - 1}{4n} \cdot  I\left( {n - 2, n - 2}\right) \) .
