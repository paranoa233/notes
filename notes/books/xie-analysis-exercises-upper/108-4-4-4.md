#### 4.4.4 练习题

1. 确定下列无穷小量的阶:

(1) \( \sqrt{1 + \tan x} - \sqrt{1 - \tan x}\left( {x \rightarrow  0}\right) \) ; (2) \( \ln x - \ln a\left( {x \rightarrow  a}\right) , a > 0 \) ;

(3) \( {a}^{x} - 1\left( {x \rightarrow  0}\right) \) ,其中 \( a > 0 \) ; (4) \( {a}^{{x}^{2}} - {b}^{{x}^{2}}\left( {x \rightarrow  0}\right) \) ,其中 \( a, b > 0 \) ;

(5) \( \ln \left( {x + \cos \frac{\pi }{2}x}\right) \left( {x \rightarrow  1}\right) \) ; (6) \( \ln x\ln \left( {x - 1}\right) \left( {x \rightarrow  {1}^{ + }}\right) \) .

2. 设存在极限 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}\frac{f\left( x\right) }{x} \) ,又有 \( f\left( x\right)  - f\left( \frac{x}{2}\right)  = o\left( x\right) \left( {x \rightarrow  0}\right) \) ,证明: \( f\left( x\right)  = \; o\left( x\right) \left( {x \rightarrow  0}\right) \) .

3. 与数列中的几个常见的无穷大量之间的关系 \( \ln n \ll  {n}^{\varepsilon } \ll  {a}^{n} \ll  n! \ll  {n}^{n}(a > \; 1,\varepsilon  > 0) \) 相类似,证明当 \( x \rightarrow   + \infty \) 时,有

\[
\ln x \ll  {x}^{\varepsilon } \ll  {a}^{x} \ll  {x}^{x}\left( {a > 1,\varepsilon  > 0}\right) ,
\]

其中 \( u \ll  v \) 的定义是 \( \lim \frac{u}{v} = 0 \) .

4. 用等价量代换方法计算下列极限:

(1) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}\frac{\ln \left( {{\sin }^{2}x + {\mathrm{e}}^{x}}\right)  - x}{\ln \left( {{x}^{2} + {\mathrm{e}}^{2x}}\right)  - {2x}} \) ; (2) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow   + \infty }}\left( {x + 1}\right) \left\lbrack  {\ln \left( {{x}^{2} + x}\right)  - 2\ln \left( {x + 1}\right) }\right\rbrack \)

(3) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}\frac{\sqrt{1 + x} - \sqrt[6]{1 + x}}{\sqrt[3]{1 + x} - 1} \) ; (4) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}\frac{\sqrt{\cos x} - \sqrt[3]{\cos x}}{{\sin }^{2}x} \) ;

(5) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}\frac{{\left( 3 + 2\sin x\right) }^{x} - {3}^{x}}{{\tan }^{2}x} \) ; (6) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  0}}{\left( \frac{\arcsin t}{t}\right) }^{\frac{1}{{t}^{2}}} \) .
