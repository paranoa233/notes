#### 2.1.5 练习题

1. 按极限定义证明:

(1) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\frac{3{n}^{2}}{{n}^{2} - 4} = 3 \) (2) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\frac{\sin n}{n} = 0 \)

(3) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{\left( 1 + n\right) }^{\frac{1}{n}} = 1 \) (4) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\frac{{a}^{n}}{n!} = 0\;\left( {a > 0}\right) \) .

2. 设 \( {a}_{n} \geq  0, n \in  {\mathbf{N}}_{ + } \) ,数列 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 收敛于 \( a \) ,证明 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\sqrt{{a}_{n}} = \sqrt{a} \) .

3. 若 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{a}_{n} = a \) ,证明 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\left| {a}_{n}\right|  = \left| a\right| \) . 反之如何?

4. 下面一组题在本章的许多极限计算中有用 (并与第五章的连续性概念有关):

(1) 设 \( p\left( x\right) \) 是 \( x \) 的多项式. 若 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{a}_{n} = a \) ,证明 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}p\left( {a}_{n}\right)  = p\left( a\right) \) ;

(2) 设 \( b > 0,\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{a}_{n} = a \) ,证明 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{b}^{{a}_{n}} = {b}^{a} \) ;

(3) 设 \( b > 0,\left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 为正数列, \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{a}_{n} = a, a > 0 \) ,证明 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{\log }_{b}{a}_{n} = {\log }_{b}a \) ;

(4) 设 \( b \) 为实数， \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 为正数列， \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{a}_{n} = a, a > 0 \) ，证明 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{a}_{n}^{b} = {a}^{b} \) ；

(5) 设 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{a}_{n} = a \) ,证明 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\sin {a}_{n} = \sin a \) .

(例如上面提到过的题: 若 \( b > 0 \) ,则 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{b}^{\frac{1}{n}} = 1 \) . 它是第 \( \left( 2\right) \) 题的特例.)

5. 设 \( a > 1 \) ,证明 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\frac{{\log }_{a}n}{n} = 0 \) .

(可以利用已知极限 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\sqrt[n]{n} = 1 \) .)
