#### 10.4.6 练习题

1. Cauchy 曾经用下面的例子说明用 Newton-Leibniz 公式时必须验证条件. 请指出以下计算中的错误并作更正:

\[
{\int }_{0}^{{3\pi }/4}\frac{\sin x}{1 + {\cos }^{2}x}\mathrm{\;d}x = {\left. \arctan \left( \sec x\right) \right| }_{0}^{{3\pi }/4} =  - \arctan \sqrt{2} - \frac{\pi }{4}.
\]

2. 计算下列各题:

(1) \( {\int }_{0}^{2}\left| {1 - x}\right| \mathrm{d}x \) (2) \( {\int }_{-2}^{2}\min \left\{  {\frac{1}{\left| x\right| },{x}^{2}}\right\}  \mathrm{d}x \) (3) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow   + \infty }}\sqrt{x}{\int }_{x}^{x + 1}\frac{\mathrm{d}t}{\sqrt{t + \cos t}} \) (4) \( {\int }_{-\pi /4}^{\pi /4}\frac{{\cos }^{2}x}{1 + {\mathrm{e}}^{-x}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) ; (5) \( {\int }_{0}^{\pi }\left( {{\int }_{0}^{x}\frac{\sin t}{\pi  - t}\mathrm{\;d}t}\right) \mathrm{d}x \) ; (6) \( {\int }_{0}^{\pi }\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{{a}^{2}{\sin }^{2}x + {b}^{2}{\cos }^{2}x}\left( {{ab} \neq  0}\right) \) .

3. 利用对称性, 计算下列各题:

(1) \( {\int }_{0}^{\pi }\frac{x\mathrm{\;d}x}{1 + {\cos }^{2}x} \) (2) \( {\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{x}{{\mathrm{e}}^{x} + {\mathrm{e}}^{1 - x}}\mathrm{\;d}x \)

(3) \( {\int }_{-2}^{2}x\ln \left( {1 + {\mathrm{e}}^{x}}\right) \mathrm{d}x \) (4) \( {\int }_{0}^{\pi /4}\ln \left( {1 + \tan x}\right) \mathrm{d}x \)

(5) \( {\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\frac{{\sin }^{n}x}{{\sin }^{n}x + {\cos }^{n}x}\mathrm{\;d}x \) (6) \( {\int }_{0}^{\pi }\frac{{a}^{n}{\sin }^{2}x + {b}^{n}{\cos }^{2}x}{{a}^{2n}{\sin }^{2}x + {b}^{2n}{\cos }^{2}x}\mathrm{\;d}x \) .

4. 设 \( f \in  C\left\lbrack  {0, a}\right\rbrack  , a > 0 \) .

(1) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {0, a}\right\rbrack \) 上 \( f\left( x\right)  + f\left( {a - x}\right)  \neq  0 \) ,计算 \( I = {\int }_{0}^{a}\frac{f\left( x\right) }{f\left( x\right)  + f\left( {a - x}\right) }\mathrm{d}x \) ;

(2)在 \( \left\lbrack  {0, a}\right\rbrack \) 上 \( f\left( x\right) f\left( {a - x}\right)  \equiv  1 \) ,计算 \( I = {\int }_{0}^{a}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1 + f\left( x\right) } \) .

5. 设 \( f \) 为连续函数,证明下列等式:

(1) \( {\int }_{0}^{\pi }{xf}\left( {\sin x}\right) \mathrm{d}x = \frac{\pi }{2}{\int }_{0}^{\pi }f\left( {\sin x}\right) \mathrm{d}x \)

(2) \( {\int }_{1}^{a}f\left( {{x}^{2} + \frac{{a}^{2}}{{x}^{2}}}\right) \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x} = {\int }_{1}^{a}f\left( {x + \frac{{a}^{2}}{x}}\right) \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x} \) ;

(3) \( {\int }_{0}^{2\pi }f\left( {a\cos x + b\sin x}\right) \mathrm{d}x = 2{\int }_{0}^{\pi }f\left( {\sqrt{{a}^{2} + {b}^{2}}\cos x}\right) \mathrm{d}x \) .

6. 设 \( n \in  {\mathbf{N}}_{ + } \) ,计算 \( {\int }_{0}^{\pi }{\sin }^{{2n} - 1}x\cos \left( {{2n} + 1}\right) x\mathrm{\;d}x \) 与 \( {\int }_{0}^{\pi }{\cos }^{{2n} - 1}x\sin \left( {{2n} + 1}\right) x\mathrm{\;d}x \) .

7. 计算 \( I\left( {m, n}\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{1}{x}^{m}{\ln }^{n}x\mathrm{\;d}x \) ,其中 \( m, n \) 是正整数.

8. 计算 \( J\left( {m, n}\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}{\sin }^{m}x{\cos }^{n}x\mathrm{\;d}x \) ,其中 \( m, n \) 是正整数.

9. 求 \( {F}^{\prime }\left( 0\right) \) ,其中 \( F\left( x\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{x}\cos \frac{1}{t}\mathrm{\;d}t \) .

10. (Fejér (费耶尔) 积分) 证明: \( {\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}{\left( \frac{\sin {nx}}{\sin x}\right) }^{2}\mathrm{\;d}x = \frac{n\pi }{2} \) .

11. 定义 \( f\left( x\right)  = {\int }_{x}^{x + \pi /2}\left| {\sin t}\right| \mathrm{d}t, x \in  \left( {-\infty , + \infty }\right) \) .

(1)证明: \( f \) 是周期为 \( \pi \) 的周期函数；

(2)求 \( f \) 的最大值与最小值.
