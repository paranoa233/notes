#### 12.2.3 练习题

1. 讨论下列广义积分的敛散性, 若是收敛, 还要讨论是条件收敛还是绝对收敛:

(1) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }x{\sin }^{4}x\mathrm{\;d}x \) (2) \( {\int }_{3}^{+\infty }\frac{\ln \ln x}{\ln x}\sin x\mathrm{\;d}x \)

(3) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{1}{x}{\mathrm{e}}^{\cos x}\sin \left( {\sin x}\right) \mathrm{d}x \) (4) \( {\int }_{1}^{+\infty }\ln \frac{{x}^{2}}{{x}^{2} - 1}\mathrm{\;d}x \)

(5) \( {\int }_{1}^{+\infty }\ln \left( {\cos \frac{1}{x} + \sin \frac{1}{x}}\right) \mathrm{d}x \) ; (6) \( {\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{1}{x}\ln \frac{1 + x}{1 - x}\mathrm{\;d}x \) ;

(7) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\left\lbrack  {\frac{1}{\sqrt{x}} - \sqrt{\ln \left( {1 + \frac{1}{x}}\right) }}\right\rbrack  \mathrm{d}x \) ; (8) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{\sin x}{\sqrt{x + \cos x}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) .

2. 对于以下含有参数的广义积分，确定出使积分绝对收敛、条件收敛和发散的参数范围:

(1) \( {\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{{\sin }^{p}x{\cos }^{q}x} \) ; (2) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{\cos x}{1 + {x}^{p}}\mathrm{\;d}x\left( {p > 0}\right) \) ;

(3) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{\sin x}{x}{\left| \ln x\right| }^{p}\mathrm{\;d}x \) (4) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{\ln \left( {1 + x}\right) }{{x}^{p}}\mathrm{\;d}x \)

(5) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{\sin {x}^{2}}{1 + {x}^{p}}\mathrm{\;d}x\left( {p \geq  0}\right) \) ; (6) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{{x}^{p}\sin x}{1 + {x}^{q}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) ;

(7) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{{\mathrm{e}}^{\sin x}\sin {2x}}{{x}^{p}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) (8) \( {\int }_{\mathrm{e}}^{+\infty }\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{{\left( x - \mathrm{e}\right) }^{p}{\left( \ln \ln x\right) }^{q}} \) .

3. 设 \( {a}_{1},\cdots ,{a}_{n} \) 为互不相同的实数, \( {p}_{1},\cdots ,{p}_{n} > 0 \) ,讨论广义积分

\[
{\int }_{-\infty }^{+\infty }\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{{\left| x - {a}_{1}\right| }^{{p}_{1}}{\left| x - {a}_{2}\right| }^{{p}_{2}}\cdots {\left| x - {a}_{n}\right| }^{{p}_{n}}}
\]

的敛散性.

4. 讨论广义积分 \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\left\lbrack  {\ln \left( {1 + \frac{1}{x}}\right)  - \frac{1}{1 + x}}\right\rbrack  \mathrm{d}x \) 的敛散性.

5. 判别广义积分 \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{x\mathrm{\;d}x}{1 + {x}^{4}{\sin }^{2}x} \) 的敛散性.

6. 求函数 \( F\left( x\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }{\left| {t}^{2} - \frac{1}{{t}^{2}}\right| }^{x}\mathrm{\;d}t \) 的定义域.

7. 设 \( {f}^{\prime } \in  C\left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 且 \( {f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) \) 处处大于 0,证明: 广义积分

\[
{\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{f\left( x\right)  - f\left( 0\right) }{{x}^{p}}\mathrm{\;d}x
\]

在 \( p < 2 \) 时收敛， \( p \geq  2 \) 时发散.

8. 设 \( {\int }_{a}^{+\infty }f \) 为条件收敛,证明:

(1)广义积分 \( {\int }_{a}^{+\infty }\left( {\left| f\right|  \pm  f}\right) \) 发散;

(2) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow   + \infty }}\frac{{\int }_{a}^{x}\left( {\left| f\right|  + f}\right) }{{\int }_{a}^{x}\left( {\left| f\right|  - f}\right) } = 1 \) .

9. 设 \( f \in  {C}^{1}\lbrack a, + \infty ) \) 单调,且 \( f\left( {+\infty }\right)  = 0 \) ,证明: \( {\int }_{a}^{+\infty }{f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) {\sin }^{2}x\mathrm{\;d}x \) 收敛.

10. 设 \( f, g \in  {C}^{1}\lbrack a, + \infty ),{f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) \) 非负, \( f\left( {+\infty }\right)  = 0 \) ,且 \( g\left( x\right) \) 在 \( \lbrack a, + \infty ) \) 上有界,证明: \( {\int }_{a}^{+\infty }f\left( x\right) {g}^{\prime }\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \) 收敛.
