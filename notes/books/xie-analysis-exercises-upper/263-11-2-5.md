#### 11.2.5 练习题

1. 设 \( f \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 上单调增加,证明: 对每个 \( c \in  \left( {a, b}\right) \) ,函数 \( F\left( x\right)  = {\int }_{c}^{x}f\left( t\right) \mathrm{d}t \) 为 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 上的下凸函数.

2. 设 \( f \) 在 \( \lbrack 0, + \infty ) \) 上是下凸函数,证明: 函数 \( F\left( x\right)  = \frac{1}{x}{\int }_{0}^{x}f\left( t\right) \mathrm{d}t \) 是 \( \left( {0, + \infty }\right) \) 上的下凸函数.

3. 设 \( f \) 于 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 上为非负的上凸函数,证明: \( {\int }_{0}^{1}{2f}\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \geq  \mathop{\max }\limits_{{x \in  \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack  }}\{ f\left( x\right) \} \) .

4. 设 \( f \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 上为上凸可微函数, \( f\left( a\right)  = f\left( b\right)  = 0,{f}^{\prime }\left( a\right)  = \alpha  > 0,{f}^{\prime }\left( b\right)  = \beta  < 0 \) , 证明:

\[
0 \leq  {\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \leq  \frac{1}{2}{\alpha \beta } \cdot  \frac{{\left( b - a\right) }^{2}}{\beta  - \alpha }.
\]

5. 设 \( f \in  {C}^{1}\left\lbrack  {0,2}\right\rbrack  , f\left( 0\right)  = f\left( 2\right)  = 1,\left| {{f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) }\right|  \leq  1 \) ,证明: \( \left| {{\int }_{0}^{2}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x}\right|  \geq  1 \) .

6. 已知函数 \( f \in  C\left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) ,且 \( f\left( x\right) \) 处处大于 0,证明:

\[
{\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x{\int }_{a}^{b}\frac{1}{f\left( x\right) }\mathrm{d}x \geq  {\left( b - a\right) }^{2}.
\]

7. 已知非负函数 \( f \in  R\left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack  ,{\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = 1, k \) 为实数,证明:

\[
{\left( {\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( x\right) \cos kx\mathrm{\;d}x\right) }^{2} + {\left( {\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( x\right) \sin kx\mathrm{\;d}x\right) }^{2} \leq  1.
\]

8. 设 \( f \in  {C}^{1}\left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 且 \( f\left( a\right)  = 0 \) ,证明比例题 11.2.3 更强的不等式:

\[
{\int }_{a}^{b}{f}^{2}\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \leq  \frac{{\left( b - a\right) }^{2}}{2}{\int }_{a}^{b}{\left( {f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) \right) }^{2}\mathrm{\;d}x - \frac{1}{2}{\int }_{a}^{b}{\left( {f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) \right) }^{2}{\left( x - a\right) }^{2}\mathrm{\;d}x.
\]

9. 设 \( f \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 上可微且当 \( x \in  \left( {0,1}\right) \) 时, \( 0 \leq  {f}^{\prime }\left( x\right)  \leq  1, f\left( 0\right)  = 0 \) . 证明:

\[
{\left( {\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x\right) }^{2} \geq  {\int }_{0}^{1}{f}^{3}\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x
\]

且仅当 \( f\left( x\right)  \equiv  0 \) 或 \( f\left( x\right)  = x \) 时成立等号.

10. (1) 试用 Young 不等式证明: 当 \( a, b \geq  1 \) 时成立 \( {ab} \leq  {\mathrm{e}}^{a - 1} + b\ln b \) ;

(2)设函数 \( f \in  R\left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) ，试用 Minkowski 不等式证明下面两个不等式不能同时成立:

\[
{\int }_{0}^{\pi }{\left| f\left( x\right)  - \sin x\right| }^{2}\mathrm{\;d}x \leq  \frac{3}{4}\text{ 和 }{\int }_{0}^{\pi }{\left| f\left( x\right)  - \cos x\right| }^{2}\mathrm{\;d}x \leq  \frac{3}{4}.
\]
