#### 9.2.4 练习题

1. 用观察法将被积函数拆开后计算不定积分:

(1) \( \int \frac{x}{\left( {x + 1}\right) \left( {x + 2}\right) }\mathrm{d}x \)

(3) \( \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{{x}^{4}\left( {{x}^{2} + 1}\right) } \)

(2) \( \int \frac{{x}^{2} + x + 1}{x\left( {1 + {x}^{2}}\right) }\mathrm{d}x \) ；

(4) \( \int \frac{4{x}^{2} + 3}{\left( {{x}^{2} + 1}\right) \left( {{x}^{2} + 2}\right) }\mathrm{d}x \) .

2. 计算下列有理函数的不定积分:

(1) \( \int \frac{{x}^{5} - x}{1 + {x}^{8}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) ;

(3) \( \int \frac{x\mathrm{\;d}x}{\left( {x + 1}\right) \left( {{x}^{2} + 3}\right) } \) ;

(5) \( \int \frac{1 + x + {x}^{2}}{{\left( x - 2\right) }^{10}}\mathrm{\;d}x \)

(7) \( \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1 + {x}^{6}} \) ;

(2) \( \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x\left( {{x}^{n} + a}\right) }\left( {a \neq  0}\right) \) ;

(4) \( \int \frac{x - 1}{{\left( {x}^{2} + 2x + 3\right) }^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) ;

(6) \( \int \frac{{x}^{3} + 1}{{x}^{3} - 5{x}^{2} + {6x}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) ;

(8) \( \int \frac{{x}^{2} - x + 3}{\left( {{x}^{2} + x + 1}\right) {\left( x - 1\right) }^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) .

3. 计算下列三角函数有理式的不定积分:

(1) \( \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1 + \cos x} \) ;

(3) \( \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{2 + {\tan }^{2}x} \)

(5) \( \int \frac{\sec x}{{\left( 1 + \sec x\right) }^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x \)

(7) \( \int \frac{\sin x\cos x}{1 + {\sin }^{4}x}\mathrm{\;d}x \) ;

4. 计算 Poisson (泊松) 积分 \( \int \frac{1 - {r}^{2}}{1 - {2r}\cos x + {r}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x\;\left( {-1 < r < 1}\right) \) .

(2) \( \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin x{\cos }^{4}x} \)

(4) \( \int {\tan }^{5}x{\sec }^{3}x\mathrm{\;d}x \) ;

(6) \( \int \frac{{\sin }^{2}x\cos x}{\sin x + \cos x}\mathrm{\;d}x \) ;

(8) \( \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{{\sin }^{4}x{\cos }^{2}x} \) .

5. 计算下列无理函数的不定积分:

(1) \( \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{\sin x{\cos }^{7}x}} \) ;

(3) \( \int \sqrt{{\tan }^{2}x + 2}\mathrm{\;d}x \)

(5) \( \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{{\left( x - 1\right) }^{3}\left( {x - 2}\right) }} \) ;

(2) \( \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x\sqrt{2{x}^{2} + 3}} \) ;

(4) \( \int \frac{x{\mathrm{e}}^{x}\mathrm{\;d}x}{\sqrt{1 + {\mathrm{e}}^{x}}} \) ;

(6) \( \int \frac{\sqrt{x} - 1}{\sqrt[3]{x} + 1}\mathrm{\;d}x \) .
