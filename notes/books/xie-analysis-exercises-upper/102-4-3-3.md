#### 4.3.3 例题

下面的 3 个例题表明, 利用复合函数的极限方法, 即变量代换法 (参见命题 4.1.1), 可以扩大以上两个重要极限的适用范围 (证明从略).

例题 4.3.1 若 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  {t}_{0}}}g\left( t\right)  = 0 \) ,且当 \( t \neq  {t}_{0} \) 时 \( g\left( t\right)  \neq  0 \) ,则 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  {t}_{0}}}\frac{\sin g\left( t\right) }{g\left( t\right) } = 1 \) .

例题 4.3.2 若 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  {t}_{0}}}g\left( t\right)  =  + \infty \left( {-\infty }\right) \) ,则 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  {t}_{0}}}{\left( 1 + \frac{1}{g\left( t\right) }\right) }^{g\left( t\right) } = \mathrm{e} \) .

例题 4.3.3 设 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  {t}_{0}}}g\left( t\right)  = 0 \) ,且 \( t \neq  {t}_{0} \) 时 \( g\left( t\right)  \neq  0 \) ,则 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  {t}_{0}}}\frac{\ln \left( {1 + g\left( t\right) }\right) }{g\left( t\right) } = 1 \) .

例题 4.3.4 求 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}{\left( \cos x\right) }^{\frac{1}{{x}^{2}}} \) .

解 首先看出这个问题是 \( {1}^{\infty } \) 型的不定式,因此可以试用上述第二个重要极限. 改写原问题如下:

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}{\left( \cos x\right) }^{\frac{1}{{x}^{2}}} = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}{\left( 1 - 2{\sin }^{2}\frac{x}{2}\right) }^{\frac{1}{{x}^{2}}}
\]

\[
= \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}{\left( 1 - 2{\sin }^{2}\frac{x}{2}\right) }^{\frac{1}{-2{\sin }^{2}\frac{x}{2}} \cdot  \frac{-2{\sin }^{2}\frac{x}{2}}{{x}^{2}}}.
\]

由于 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}\frac{2{\sin }^{2}\frac{x}{2}}{{x}^{2}} = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}{\left( \frac{\sin \frac{x}{2}}{\frac{x}{2}}\right) }^{2} \cdot  \frac{1}{2} = \frac{1}{2} \) ,因此答案是 \( {\mathrm{e}}^{-\frac{1}{2}} \) .

例题 4.3.5 求 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  \infty }}{\left( \sin \frac{1}{x} + \cos \frac{1}{x}\right) }^{x} \) .

解 与上一题类似, 可将原式改写如下:

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  \infty }}{\left\lbrack  1 + \left( \sin \frac{1}{x} + \cos \frac{1}{x} - 1\right) \right\rbrack  }^{x}
\]

\[
= \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  \infty }}{\left\lbrack  1 + \left( \sin \frac{1}{x} + \cos \frac{1}{x} - 1\right) \right\rbrack  }^{\frac{x}{\sin \frac{1}{x} + \cos \frac{1}{x} - 1} \cdot  \left( {\sin \frac{1}{x} + \cos \frac{1}{x} - 1}\right) },
\]

可见只要计算出

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  \infty }}x\left( {\sin \frac{1}{x} + \cos \frac{1}{x} - 1}\right)  = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  \infty }}\left( {\frac{\sin \frac{1}{x}}{\frac{1}{x}} - \frac{2{\sin }^{2}\frac{1}{2x}}{\frac{1}{2x} \cdot  2}}\right)  = 1,
\]

即知原问题的答案为 e.

注 以上两个例题的求解在写法上可以利用 \( {u}^{v} = {\mathrm{e}}^{v\ln u} \) 和例题 4.3.3 改写如下 (只写出后一例题):

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  \infty }}x\ln \left( {\sin \frac{1}{x} + \cos \frac{1}{x}}\right)  = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  \infty }}x\ln \left\lbrack  {1 + \left( {\sin \frac{1}{x} + \cos \frac{1}{x} - 1}\right) }\right\rbrack
\]

\[
= \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  \infty }}x\left( {\sin \frac{1}{x} + \cos \frac{1}{x} - 1}\right) ,
\]

以下同上. 又如果一开始作代换 \( y = 1/x \) ,则在书写上更方便一些.
