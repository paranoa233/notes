## 第一组参考题

1. 设 \( \left\{  {a}_{{2k} - 1}\right\}  ,\left\{  {a}_{2k}\right\} \) 和 \( \left\{  {a}_{3k}\right\} \) 都收敛,证明: \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 收敛.

2. 设 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 有界,且满足条件 \( {a}_{n} \leq  {a}_{n + 2},{a}_{n} \leq  {a}_{n + 3}, n \in  {\mathbf{N}}_{ + } \) ,证明: \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 收敛.

3. 设 \( \left\{  {{a}_{n} + {a}_{n + 1}}\right\} \) 和 \( \left\{  {{a}_{n} + {a}_{n + 2}}\right\} \) 都收敛,证明: \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 收敛.

4. 设数列 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 收敛于 0,又存在极限 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\left| \frac{{a}_{n + 1}}{{a}_{n}}\right|  = a \) . 证明: \( a \leq  1 \) .

5. 设 \( {a}_{n} = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{k = 1}}^{n}\left( {\sqrt{1 + \frac{k}{{n}^{2}}} - 1}\right) , n \in  {\mathbf{N}}_{ + } \) ,计算 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{a}_{n} \) .

6. 用 \( p\left( n\right) \) 表示能整除 \( n \) 的素数的个数,证明: \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\frac{p\left( n\right) }{n} = 0 \) .

7. 设 \( {a}_{0},{a}_{1},\cdots ,{a}_{p} \) 是 \( p + 1 \) 个给定的数,且满足条件 \( {a}_{0} + {a}_{1} + \cdots  + {a}_{p} = 0 \) . 求 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\left( {{a}_{0}\sqrt{n} + {a}_{1}\sqrt{n + 1} + \cdots  + {a}_{p}\sqrt{n + p}}\right) . \)

8. 证明: 当 \( 0 < k < 1 \) 时, \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\left\lbrack  {{\left( 1 + n\right) }^{k} - {n}^{k}}\right\rbrack   = 0 \) .

9. (1) 设 \( \left\{  {x}_{n}\right\} \) 收敛. 令 \( {y}_{n} = n\left( {{x}_{n} - {x}_{n - 1}}\right) , n \in  {\mathbf{N}}_{ + } \) ,问 \( \left\{  {y}_{n}\right\} \) 是否收敛？

(2)在上一小题中，若 \( \left\{  {y}_{n}\right\} \) 也收敛，证明: \( \left\{  {y}_{n}\right\} \) 收敛于 0 .

10. (1) 设正数列 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 满足条件 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\frac{{a}_{n}}{{a}_{n + 1}} = 0 \) ,证明: \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 是正无穷大量.

(2)设正数列 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 满足条件 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\frac{{a}_{n}}{{a}_{n + 1} + {a}_{n + 2}} = 0 \) ,证明: \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 无界.

11. 证明: \( {\left( \frac{n}{3}\right) }^{n} < n! < {\left( \frac{n}{2}\right) }^{n} \) ,其中右边的不等式当 \( n \geq  6 \) 时成立.

12. 证明: \( {\left( \frac{n}{\mathrm{e}}\right) }^{n} < n! < \mathrm{e}{\left( \frac{n}{2}\right) }^{n} \) .

13. (对于命题 2.5.4 的改进) 证明:

(1) \( n \geq  2 \) 时成立

\[
1 + 1 + \frac{1}{2!} + \cdots  + \frac{1}{n!} + \frac{1}{n!n} = 3 - \frac{1}{2!1 \cdot  2} - \cdots  - \frac{1}{n!\left( {n - 1}\right) n};
\]

(2) \( \mathrm{e} = 3 - \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{k = 0}}^{n}\frac{1}{\left( {k + 2}\right) !\left( {k + 1}\right) \left( {k + 2}\right) } \) ;

(3) 用 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{k = 0}}^{n}\frac{1}{k!} + \frac{1}{n!n} \) 计算 \( \mathrm{e} \) 要比不加上最后一项好得多.

14. 设 \( {a}_{n} = 1 + \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} + \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} + \cdots  + \frac{1}{\sqrt{n}} - 2\sqrt{n}, n \in  {\mathbf{N}}_{ + } \) ,证明: \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 收敛.

15. 设已知存在极限 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\frac{{a}_{1} + {a}_{2} + \cdots  + {a}_{n}}{n} \) ,证明: \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\frac{{a}_{n}}{n} = 0 \) .

16. 证明: \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{\left( n!\right) }^{1/{n}^{2}} = 1 \) .

17. 设对每个 \( n \) 有 \( {x}_{n} < 1 \) 和 \( \left( {1 - {x}_{n}}\right) {x}_{n + 1} \geq  \frac{1}{4} \) ,证明 \( \left\{  {x}_{n}\right\} \) 收敛,并求其极限.

18. 设 \( {a}_{1} = b,{a}_{2} = c \) ,在 \( n \geq  3 \) 时, \( {a}_{n} = \frac{{a}_{n - 1} + {a}_{n - 2}}{2} \) ,证明 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 收敛,并求其极限.

19. 设 \( a, b, c \) 是三个给定的实数. 令 \( {a}_{1} = a,{b}_{1} = b,{c}_{1} = c \) ,并以递推公式定义

\[
{a}_{n + 1} = \frac{{b}_{n} + {c}_{n}}{2},{b}_{n + 1} = \frac{{c}_{n} + {a}_{n}}{2},{c}_{n + 1} = \frac{{a}_{n} + {b}_{n}}{2}, n \in  {\mathbf{N}}_{ + }.
\]

求这三个数列的极限.

20. (1) 设 \( {a}_{1} > {b}_{1} > 0,{a}_{n + 1} = \frac{2{a}_{n}{b}_{n}}{{a}_{n} + {b}_{n}},{b}_{n + 1} = \sqrt{{a}_{n + 1}{b}_{n}}, n \in  {\mathbf{N}}_{ + } \) ,证明: \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 和 \( \left\{  {b}_{n}\right\} \) 收敛于同一极限.

(2)在 \( {a}_{1} = 2\sqrt{3},{b}_{1} = 3 \) 时，证明上述极限等于单位圆的半周长 \( \pi \) . (这里可以利用极限 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}n\sin \frac{\pi }{n} = \pi \) .)

注 本题与例题 2.3.5 完全不同. 实际上这就是计算圆周率的 Archimedes (阿基米德) - 刘徽方法的迭代形式 (参见 \( \left\lbrack  {4,{60}}\right\rbrack \) ). 在 (2) 中的两个数列 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 和 \( \left\{  {b}_{n}\right\} \) 就是单位圆的外切和内接正多边形的半周长 (请求出边数与 \( n \) 的关系).
