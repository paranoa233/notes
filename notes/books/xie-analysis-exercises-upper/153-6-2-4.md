#### 6.2.4 练习题

1. 对 \( y = \arctan x \) 计算 \( {y}^{\left( n\right) }\left( 0\right) \) .

2. 对 \( y = {\left( \arctan x\right) }^{2} \) 计算 \( {y}^{\left( n\right) }\left( 0\right) \) .

3. 对 \( y = {\left( \arcsin x\right) }^{2} \) 计算 \( {y}^{\left( n\right) }\left( 0\right) \) .

4. 设 \( y = {x}^{n - 1}{\mathrm{e}}^{\frac{1}{x}} \) ,证明: \( {y}^{\left( n\right) } = \frac{{\left( -1\right) }^{n}}{{x}^{n + 1}}{\mathrm{e}}^{\frac{1}{x}} \) .

5. 求下列函数的 \( n \) 阶导函数:

(1) \( y = {\sin }^{3}x \) ; (2) \( y = {\mathrm{e}}^{x}\sin x \)

(3) \( y = {x}^{n - 1}\ln x \) (4) \( y = {x}^{3}{\mathrm{e}}^{x} \) ;

(5) \( y = \frac{{x}^{n}}{1 - x} \) (6) \( y = \frac{{x}^{n}}{{\left( x + 1\right) }^{2}} \)

(7) \( y = \sin {ax}\sin {bx} \) .

6. 证明: \( y = {C}_{1}{\mathrm{e}}^{{\lambda }_{1}x} + {C}_{2}{\mathrm{e}}^{{\lambda }_{2}x} \) 满足微分方程 \( {y}^{\prime \prime } - \left( {{\lambda }_{1} + {\lambda }_{2}}\right) {y}^{\prime } + {\lambda }_{1}{\lambda }_{2}y = 0 \) .

7. 证明: \( y = {C}_{1}\sin \left( {{\omega t} + \varphi }\right)  + {C}_{2}\cos \left( {{\omega t} + \varphi }\right) \) 满足微分方程 \( {y}^{\prime \prime } + {\omega }^{2}y = 0 \) .

8. 若曲线由极坐标方程 \( \rho  = f\left( \theta \right) \) 表示，则可得到参数方程 \( x = f\left( \theta \right) \cos \theta , y = \; f\left( \theta \right) \sin \theta \) ,求 \( {y}^{\prime }\left( x\right) \) .

9. 证明: Archimedes 螺线 \( \rho  = {a\theta } \) 与双曲螺线 \( \rho  = a{\theta }^{-1} \) 在相交处的切线正交.

10. 对下列参数方程求 \( {y}^{\prime }\left( x\right) \) 和 \( {y}^{\prime \prime }\left( x\right) \) (在 (4) 中假定 \( f \) 二阶可导):

(1) \( \left\{  \begin{array}{l} x = \sqrt{1 + t} \\  y = \sqrt{1 - t} \end{array}\right. \) (2) \( \left\{  \begin{array}{l} x = {at}\cos t \\  y = {at}\sin t \end{array}\right. \)

(3) \( \left\{  \begin{array}{l} x = a{\cos }^{3}t \\  y = a{\sin }^{3}t \end{array}\right. \) (4) \( \left\{  \begin{array}{l} x = {f}^{\prime }\left( t\right) , \\  y = t{f}^{\prime }\left( t\right)  - f\left( t\right) . \end{array}\right. \)

11. 求出由方程 \( {x}^{3} + {y}^{3} - {3xy} = 0 \) 确定的曲线上有水平切线的点.

12. 是否可以利用函数 \( y = \sin x\sin {3x}\sin {5x} \) 的奇偶性计算 \( {y}^{\prime \prime }\left( 0\right) \) ?

13. 是否可以不展开乘积 \( y = \left( {6 + {5x}}\right) {\left( 4 + 3x\right) }^{2}{\left( 2 + x\right) }^{3} \) 就求出 \( {y}^{\left( 5\right) }\left( 0\right) \) ?

14. 设 \( f \) 为 7 次多项式,若 \( f\left( x\right)  + 1 \) 能被 \( {\left( x - 1\right) }^{4} \) 整除, \( f\left( x\right)  - 1 \) 能被 \( {\left( x + 1\right) }^{4} \) 整除,能否利用导数工具求出 \( f \) ?
