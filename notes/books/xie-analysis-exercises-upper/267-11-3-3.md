#### 11.3.3 练习题

1. 证明:

(1) \( {\int }_{0}^{\sqrt{2\pi }}\sin {x}^{2}\mathrm{\;d}x > 0 \) (2) \( \frac{1}{{20}\sqrt[3]{2}} < {\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{{x}^{19}}{\sqrt[3]{1 + {x}^{6}}}\mathrm{\;d}x < \frac{1}{20} \) ;

(3) \( {\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}x{\left( \frac{\sin {nx}}{\sin x}\right) }^{4}\mathrm{\;d}x < \frac{{n}^{2}{\pi }^{2}}{4} \) ; (4) \( 0 < \frac{\pi }{2} - {\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\frac{\sin x}{x}\mathrm{\;d}x < \frac{{\pi }^{3}}{144} \) ;

(5) \( {0.005} < {\int }_{0}^{100}\frac{{\mathrm{e}}^{-x}}{x + {100}}\mathrm{\;d}x < {0.01} \) ; (6) \( \frac{2}{9}{\pi }^{2} < {\int }_{\pi /6}^{\pi /2}\frac{2x}{\sin x}\mathrm{\;d}x < \frac{1}{3}{\pi }^{2} \) .

2. 设 \( f \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {0, a}\right\rbrack  \left( {a > 0}\right) \) 上有可积的导函数,证明:

\[
\left| {f\left( 0\right) }\right|  \leq  \frac{1}{a}{\int }_{0}^{a}\left| {f\left( x\right) }\right| \mathrm{d}x + {\int }_{0}^{a}\left| {{f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) }\right| \mathrm{d}x.
\]

3. 设 \( f \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 上有可积的导函数,证明:

\[
{\int }_{0}^{1}\left| {f\left( x\right) }\right| \mathrm{d}x \leq  \max \left\{  {{\int }_{0}^{1}\left| {{f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) }\right| \mathrm{d}x,\left| {{\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x}\right| }\right\}  .
\]

4. 设函数 \( f \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 上可微, \( \left| {{f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) }\right|  \leq  M \) ,且 \( {\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = 0 \) . 对于函数 \( F\left( x\right)  = \; {\int }_{a}^{x}f\left( t\right) \mathrm{d}t \)

(1)证明: \( \left| {F\left( x\right) }\right|  \leq  \frac{M{\left( b - a\right) }^{2}}{8} \) ；

(2)在增加条件 \( f\left( a\right)  = f\left( b\right)  = 0 \) 时证明: \( \left| {F\left( x\right) }\right|  \leq  \frac{M{\left( b - a\right) }^{2}}{16} \) .

5. 证明: 对每个正整数 \( n \) ,成立

\[
\frac{2}{3}n\sqrt{n} < 1 + \sqrt{2} + \cdots  + \sqrt{n} < \frac{{4n} + 3}{6}\sqrt{n}.
\]

6. 设 \( f \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 上可微, \( f\left( a\right)  = f\left( b\right)  = 0,\left| {{f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) }\right|  \leq  M \) ,证明:

\[
\left| {{\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x}\right|  \leq  \frac{M}{4}{\left( b - a\right) }^{2}.
\]

7. 设 \( f \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 上二阶可微, \( f\left( a\right)  = f\left( b\right)  = 0,\left| {{f}^{\prime \prime }\left( x\right) }\right|  \leq  M \) ,证明:

\[
\left| {{\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x}\right|  \leq  \frac{M}{12}{\left( b - a\right) }^{3}.
\]

8. (矩形公式) 设 \( f \in  {C}^{2}\left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) ,证明: 存在 \( \xi  \in  \left( {a, b}\right) \) ,使成立

\[
{\int }_{a}^{b}f - \left( {b - a}\right) f\left( \frac{a + b}{2}\right)  = \frac{{f}^{\prime \prime }\left( \xi \right) {\left( b - a\right) }^{3}}{24}.
\]

9. 设 \( f \) 于 \( \left\lbrack  {-1,1}\right\rbrack \) 上可微,且有 \( a \in  \left( {0,1}\right) \) ,使得 \( {\int }_{-a}^{a}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = 0 \) ,证明:

\[
\left| {{\int }_{-1}^{1}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x}\right|  \leq  M\left( {1 - {a}^{2}}\right) .
\]

10. 设 \( f \) 于 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 上可微, \( \left| {{f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) }\right|  \leq  M \) ,证明:

\[
\left| {{\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x - \frac{1}{n}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{k = 1}}^{n}f\left( \frac{k}{n}\right) }\right|  \leq  \frac{M}{2n}.
\]

11. 设 \( f \) 在区间 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 上可微,且 \( {f}^{\prime } \in  R\left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) ,对正整数 \( n \) 定义

\[
{A}_{n} = {\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x - \frac{1}{n}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{k = 1}}^{n}f\left( \frac{k}{n}\right) ,
\]

证明: \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}n{A}_{n} = \frac{1}{2}\left( {f\left( 0\right)  - f\left( 1\right) }\right) \) .

12. 设 \( f \) 在区间 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 上二阶可微,且 \( {f}^{\prime \prime } \in  R\left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) ,对正整数 \( n \) 定义

\[
{B}_{n} = {\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x - \frac{1}{n}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{k = 1}}^{n}f\left( \frac{{2k} - 1}{2n}\right) ,
\]

证明: \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{n}^{2}{B}_{n} = \frac{1}{24}\left\lbrack  {{f}^{\prime }\left( 1\right)  - {f}^{\prime }\left( 0\right) }\right\rbrack \) .
