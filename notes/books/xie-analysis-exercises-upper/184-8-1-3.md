#### 8.1.3 练习题

1. 以下几个函数极限均不宜用 L'Hospital 法则, 为什么?

(1) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  1}}\frac{x}{x + 1} \) (2) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow   + \infty }}\frac{x + \sin x}{x - \sin x} \)

(3) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow   + \infty }}\frac{{\mathrm{e}}^{x} + {\mathrm{e}}^{-x}}{{\mathrm{e}}^{x} - {\mathrm{e}}^{-x}} \) ; (4) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow   + \infty }}\frac{x}{\sqrt{1 + {x}^{2}}} \) .

2. 求下列极限:

(1) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}\frac{{a}^{{x}^{2}} - {b}^{{x}^{2}}}{{\left( {a}^{x} - {b}^{x}\right) }^{2}} \) (2) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow   + \infty }}\left\lbrack  {\left( {{x}^{3} - {x}^{2} + \frac{x}{2}}\right) {\mathrm{e}}^{\frac{1}{x}} - \sqrt{1 + {x}^{6}}}\right\rbrack \) ;

(3) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}{\left( \ln \frac{1}{x}\right) }^{x} \) (4) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow   + \infty }}{\left( \frac{\pi }{2} - \arctan x\right) }^{\frac{1}{\ln x}} \) .

3. 确定 \( a, b \) ,使得当 \( x \rightarrow  0 \) 时,下列函数为尽可能高阶的无穷小量:

(1) \( f\left( x\right)  = x - \left( {a + b\cos x}\right) \sin x \) ; (2) \( f\left( x\right)  = {\mathrm{e}}^{x} - \frac{1 + {ax}}{1 + {bx}} \) ;

(3) \( f\left( x\right)  = \cot x - \frac{1 + a{x}^{2}}{x + b{x}^{3}} \) ; (4) \( f\left( x\right)  = \cos x - \frac{1 + a{x}^{2}}{1 + b{x}^{2}} \) .

4. 应用 Taylor 公式求下列极限:

(1) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}\left( {\frac{1}{x} - \frac{1}{\sin x}}\right) \) ; (2) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}\frac{{\mathrm{e}}^{{x}^{3}} - 1 - {x}^{3}}{{\sin }^{6}{2x}} \) ;

(3) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{n}^{2}\ln \left( {n\sin \frac{1}{n}}\right) \) ; (4) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{\left( -1\right) }^{n}n\sin \left( {\sqrt{{n}^{2} + 2}\pi }\right) \) ;

(5) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}\frac{\sin \left( {\tan x}\right)  - \tan \left( {\sin x}\right) }{{x}^{7}} \) ; (6) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  0}}\frac{x\sin \left( {\sin x}\right)  - {\sin }^{2}x}{{x}^{6}} \) .

5. 设存在 \( {f}^{\prime \prime }\left( a\right) \) ,证明: \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{h \rightarrow  0}}\frac{f\left( {a + {2h}}\right)  - {2f}\left( {a + h}\right)  + f\left( a\right) }{{h}^{2}} = {f}^{\prime \prime }\left( a\right) \) .

6. 设 \( f \) 在某邻域 \( O\left( {x}_{0}\right) \) 上二阶连续可微, \( {f}^{\prime }\left( {x}_{0}\right)  \neq  0, f\left( x\right)  \neq  f\left( {x}_{0}\right) ,\forall x \neq  {x}_{0} \) . 求

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  {x}_{0}}}\left\lbrack  {\frac{1}{f\left( x\right)  - f\left( {x}_{0}\right) } - \frac{1}{{f}^{\prime }\left( {x}_{0}\right) \left( {x - {x}_{0}}\right) }}\right\rbrack  .
\]

7. 设 \( f \) 在 \( \lbrack 0, + \infty ) \) 上二阶连续可微,且 \( {f}^{\prime \prime }\left( x\right)  > 0, f\left( 0\right)  = {f}^{\prime }\left( 0\right)  = 0 \) . 试求极限 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}\frac{{xf}\left( u\right) }{{uf}\left( x\right) } \) ,其中 \( u \) 是函数 \( f \) 的图像在点 \( \left( {x, f\left( x\right) }\right) \) 处的切线在 \( x \) 轴上的截距.

8. 令 \( f\left( x\right)  = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{n}^{x}\left\lbrack  {{\left( 1 + \frac{1}{n + 1}\right) }^{n + 1} - {\left( 1 + \frac{1}{n}\right) }^{n}}\right\rbrack \) ,确定其定义域和值域.

9. 设 \( {x}_{1} > 0,{x}_{n + 1} = \ln \left( {1 + {x}_{n}}\right) , n \in  {\mathbf{N}}_{ + } \) ,证明: \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}n{x}_{n} = 2 \) .
