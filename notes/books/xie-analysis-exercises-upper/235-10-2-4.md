#### 10.2.4 练习题

1. 设 \( f \in  C\left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) ,且对满足条件 \( g\left( a\right)  = g\left( b\right)  = 0 \) 的每个函数 \( g \in  C\left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) ,都有 \( {\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( x\right) g\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = 0 \) ,证明: \( f \equiv  0 \) .

2. 设非负函数 \( f \in  R\left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) ,且 \( {\int }_{a}^{b}f > 0 \) ,若有多项式 \( P \) 使 \( {\int }_{a}^{b}{P}^{2}\left( x\right) f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = 0 \) ,证明: \( P \equiv  0 \) .

3. 设 \( f \in  C\left\lbrack  {-1,1}\right\rbrack \) ,且对 \( \left\lbrack  {-1,1}\right\rbrack \) 上的每个可积偶函数 \( g \) 都有 \( {\int }_{-1}^{1}f\left( x\right) g\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = 0 \) , 证明: \( f \) 是 \( \left\lbrack  {-1,1}\right\rbrack \) 上的奇函数.

4. 计算极限 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{\int }_{0}^{1}{\left( 1 - {x}^{2}\right) }^{n}\mathrm{\;d}x \) .

5. 分析例题 10.2.4 的条件和证明过程, 试写出它的可能推广, 并作出证明. (这是一道开放题,要求设计出一定的条件,使 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{\int }_{a}^{b}{f}^{n}\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = 0 \) 成立.)

6. 已知 \( {x}_{n} \in  \left\lbrack  {0,\frac{\pi }{2}}\right\rbrack  , n = 1,2,\cdots \) ,且满足 \( \frac{2}{\pi }{\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}{\sin }^{n}x\mathrm{\;d}x = {\sin }^{n}{x}_{n} \) ,计算极限 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{x}_{n} \)

7. 设 \( f \in  C\left\lbrack  {-1,1}\right\rbrack \) ,证明: \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{h \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}{\int }_{-1}^{1}\frac{h}{{h}^{2} + {x}^{2}}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = {\pi f}\left( 0\right) \) .

8. 设 \( f \in  C\left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) ,计算:

(1) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{\int }_{0}^{1}{x}^{n}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \) (2) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{\int }_{0}^{1}n{x}^{n}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \) .

9. 设正数列 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 满足 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{\int }_{0}^{{a}_{n}}{x}^{n}\mathrm{\;d}x = 2 \) ,计算极限 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}{a}_{n} \) .

10. 设 \( n \in  {\mathbf{N}}_{ + },{I}_{n} = {\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\frac{{\sin }^{2}{nt}}{\sin t}\mathrm{\;d}t \) ,计算极限 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\frac{{I}_{n}}{\ln n} \) .
