#### 11.5.2 参考题

第一组参考题

1. 设 \( f \in  {C}^{1}\left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) ,且 \( f\left( 0\right)  = 0, f\left( 1\right)  = 1 \) ,证明:

\[
{\int }_{0}^{1}\left| {f\left( x\right)  - {f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) }\right| \mathrm{d}x \geq  \frac{1}{\mathrm{e}}.
\]

2. 设 \( a > 0 \) ,证明:

\[
{\int }_{0}^{\pi }x{a}^{\sin x}\mathrm{\;d}x{\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}{a}^{-\cos x}\mathrm{\;d}x \geq  \frac{{\pi }^{3}}{4}.
\]

3. (Tchebycheff (切比雪夫) 不等式) (1) 设 \( F \in  C\left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) ,处处大于 0,且单调减少, 证明:

\[
{\int }_{a}^{b}F\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x{\int }_{a}^{b}x{F}^{2}\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \leq  {\int }_{a}^{b}{F}^{2}\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x{\int }_{a}^{b}{xF}\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x;
\]

(2) 设 \( f, g \) 在区间 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 上可积,且对于任何 \( x < y \) 具有性质 \( \left( {f\left( x\right)  - f\left( y\right) }\right) (g\left( x\right)  - \; g\left( y\right) ) \geq  0 \) ,又设 \( p \in  R\left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) ,且处处大于 0,证明:

\[
{\int }_{a}^{b}p\left( x\right) f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x{\int }_{a}^{b}p\left( x\right) g\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \leq  {\int }_{a}^{b}p\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x{\int }_{a}^{b}p\left( x\right) f\left( x\right) g\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x.
\]

4. 设 \( f \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 上连续,且 \( 0 \leq  f\left( x\right)  < 1 \) ,证明:

\[
{\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{f\left( x\right) }{1 - f\left( x\right) }\mathrm{d}x \geq  \frac{{\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x}{1 - {\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x}.
\]

5. 证明不等式:

\[
{\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{\cos x}{\sqrt{1 - {x}^{2}}}\mathrm{\;d}x \geq  {\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{\sin x}{\sqrt{1 - {x}^{2}}}\mathrm{\;d}x
\]

6. 设 \( {f}^{\left( 2n\right) } \in  C\left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) ,且 \( {f}^{\left( k\right) }\left( a\right)  = {f}^{\left( k\right) }\left( b\right)  = 0, k = 0,1,2,\cdots , n - 1 \) ,证明:

\[
\left| {{\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x}\right|  \leq  \frac{{\left( n!\right) }^{2}{\left( b - a\right) }^{{2n} + 1}}{\left( {2n}\right) !\left( {{2n} + 1}\right) !} \cdot  \mathop{\max }\limits_{{a \leq  x \leq  b}}\left\{  \left| {{f}^{\left( 2n\right) }\left( x\right) }\right| \right\}  .
\]

7. 设函数 \( f, g \in  C\left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) ,且 \( f\left( x\right)  \text{ ≢ } 0, g\left( x\right) \) 处处大于 0 . 记

\[
{d}_{n} = {\int }_{a}^{b}{\left| f\left( x\right) \right| }^{n}g\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x, n = 1,2,\cdots ,
\]

证明: 数列 \( \left\{  \frac{{d}_{n + 1}}{{d}_{n}}\right\} \) 收敛,并求出其极限.

8. 计算 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\left\lbrack  {\frac{\sin \left( {\pi /n}\right) }{n + 1} + \frac{\sin \left( {{2\pi }/n}\right) }{n + 1/2} + \cdots  + \frac{\sin \pi }{n + 1/n}}\right\rbrack \) .

9. 对任意实数 \( a \) ,证明: \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\mathop{\prod }\limits_{{k = 1}}^{{n + 1}}\cos \left( {\frac{\sqrt{{2k} - 1}}{n}{a}^{2}}\right)  = {\mathrm{e}}^{-\frac{{a}^{4}}{2}} \) .

10. 设 \( f \) 是 \( \lbrack 1, + \infty ) \) 上的非负单调减少函数,令

\[
{a}_{n} = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{k = 1}}^{n}f\left( k\right)  - {\int }_{1}^{n}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x, n \in  {\mathbf{N}}_{ + },
\]

证明: 数列 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 收敛.

(这是 “面积原理” 的一种简单情况 (参见 [26]). 取 \( f\left( x\right)  = \frac{1}{x} \) ,就得到关于 Euler 常数的命题 2.5.6; 取 \( f\left( x\right)  = \frac{1}{\sqrt{x}} \) ,就是第二章第一组参考题 14.)

11. 计算积分 \( {\int }_{0}^{1}\sin {x}^{2}\mathrm{\;d}x \) ,使得误差不超过 0.001 .

12. 证明: 函数 \( F\left( x\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{x}\sin \frac{1}{t}\mathrm{\;d}t \) 在区间 \( (0,1\rbrack \) 上有无穷多个零点.

13. 设 \( f \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 上可导, \( {f}^{\prime } \) 单调增加且有 \( {f}^{\prime }\left( x\right)  \geq  m > 0 \) ,证明:

\[
\left| {{\int }_{a}^{b}\cos f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x}\right|  \leq  \frac{2}{m}.
\]

14. 设 \( f \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 上连续可微,且满足 \( {f}^{\prime }\left( x\right)  \geq  m > 0,\left| {f\left( x\right) }\right|  \leq  \pi \) ,证明:

\[
\left| {{\int }_{a}^{b}\sin f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x}\right|  \leq  \frac{2}{m}.
\]

15. 对 \( n \in  {\mathbf{N}}_{ + } \) ,定义

\[
{S}_{n} = 1 + \frac{n - 1}{n + 2} + \frac{n - 1}{n + 2} \cdot  \frac{n - 2}{n + 3} + \cdots  + \frac{n - 1}{n + 2} \cdot  \frac{n - 2}{n + 3}\cdots \cdots \frac{1}{2n},
\]

证明: \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\frac{{S}_{n}}{\sqrt{n}} = \frac{\sqrt{\pi }}{2} \) .
