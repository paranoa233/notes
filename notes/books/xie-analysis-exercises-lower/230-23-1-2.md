#### 23.1.2 几种常用的求参变量积分的方法

如果直接求 \( \varphi \left( t\right)  = {\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( {x, t}\right) \mathrm{d}x \) 有困难,常采用以下两种方法.

1. 先求 \( {\varphi }^{\prime }\left( t\right) \) ,即先求 \( {\int }_{a}^{b}{f}_{t}\left( {x, t}\right) \mathrm{d}x \) ,然后再对 \( t \) 积分求出 \( \varphi \left( t\right) \) .

2. 把 \( f\left( {x, t}\right) \) 表示为积分形式,再用积分号下求积分的方法. 此时通常要交换积分次序.

例题 23.1.3 计算

\[
I\left( x\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\ln \left( {{\sin }^{2}\theta  + {x}^{2}{\cos }^{2}\theta }\right) \mathrm{d}\theta ,\;0 < x <  + \infty . \tag{23.1}
\]

解 令 \( f\left( {x,\theta }\right)  = \ln \left( {{\sin }^{2}\theta  + {x}^{2}{\cos }^{2}\theta }\right) .\forall {x}_{0} \in  \left( {0, + \infty }\right) , f\left( {x,\theta }\right) \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {\frac{1}{2}{x}_{0},2{x}_{0}}\right\rbrack   \times \; \left\lbrack  {0,\frac{\pi }{2}}\right\rbrack \) 上连续,且 \( {f}_{x}\left( {x,\theta }\right)  = \frac{{2x}{\cos }^{2}\theta }{{\sin }^{2}\theta  + {x}^{2}{\cos }^{2}\theta } \) 也在 \( \left\lbrack  {\frac{1}{2}{x}_{0},2{x}_{0}}\right\rbrack   \times  \left\lbrack  {0,\frac{\pi }{2}}\right\rbrack \) 上连续. 应用积分号下求导的性质,可得 \( \forall x \in  \left( {0, + \infty }\right) \) ,

\[
{I}^{\prime }\left( x\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\frac{{2x}{\cos }^{2}\theta }{{\sin }^{2}\theta  + {x}^{2}{\cos }^{2}\theta }\mathrm{d}\theta  = {2x}{\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\frac{\mathrm{d}\theta }{{x}^{2} + {\tan }^{2}\theta }\;\left( {\text{ 令 }\tan \theta  = t}\right)
\]

\[
= {2x}{\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{1}{{x}^{2} + {t}^{2}} \cdot  \frac{1}{1 + {t}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}t
\]

\[
= \frac{2x}{{x}^{2} - 1}{\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\left( {\frac{1}{1 + {t}^{2}} - \frac{1}{{x}^{2} + {t}^{2}}}\right) \mathrm{d}t\;\left( {x \neq  1}\right)
\]

\[
= \frac{2x}{{x}^{2} - 1}\left( {\frac{\pi }{2} - \frac{1}{x}\frac{\pi }{2}}\right)  = \frac{\pi }{1 + x}\text{ . }
\]

积分得

\[
I\left( x\right)  = \pi \ln \left( {1 + x}\right)  + C\left( {x \neq  1}\right) .
\]

由 \( I\left( x\right) \) 的连续性知上述表达式对 \( x = 1 \) 也成立. 在 (23.1) 中,令 \( x = 1 \) ,则 \( I\left( 1\right)  = 0 \) , 从而 \( C =  - \pi \ln 2 \) . 最后得到

\[
I\left( x\right)  = \pi \ln \frac{1 + x}{2}.
\]

例题 23.1.4 求

\[
I\left( \alpha \right)  = {\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\ln \frac{1 + \alpha \cos x}{1 - \alpha \cos x} \cdot  \frac{1}{\cos x}\mathrm{\;d}x,\;\left| \alpha \right|  < 1.
\]

解 由于

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  \pi /2}}\ln \frac{1 + \alpha \cos x}{1 - \alpha \cos x} \cdot  \frac{1}{\cos x} = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  \pi /2}}\left\lbrack  {\frac{\ln \left( {1 + \alpha \cos x}\right) }{\cos x} - \frac{\ln \left( {1 - \alpha \cos x}\right) }{\cos x}}\right\rbrack   = {2\alpha },
\]

于是 \( x = \frac{\pi }{2} \) 不是瑕点. 又注意到有

\[
\frac{\ln \left( {1 + \alpha \cos x}\right) }{\cos x} - \frac{\ln \left( {1 - \alpha \cos x}\right) }{\cos x} = {\int }_{-\alpha }^{\alpha }\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{1 + y\cos x},
\]

令 \( f\left( {x, y}\right)  = 1/\left( {1 + y\cos x}\right) ,\forall \alpha  \in  \left( {-1,1}\right) , f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {0,\frac{\pi }{2}}\right\rbrack   \times  \left\lbrack  {-\alpha ,\alpha }\right\rbrack \) 上连续,利用交换积分次序性质，即可得到

\[
I\left( \alpha \right)  = {\int }_{-\alpha }^{\alpha }\mathrm{d}y{\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1 + y\cos x} = {\int }_{-\alpha }^{\alpha }\frac{2}{\sqrt{1 - {y}^{2}}}\arctan {\left. \left( \sqrt{\frac{1 - y}{1 + y}}\tan \frac{x}{2}\right) \right| }_{0}^{\pi /2}\mathrm{\;d}y
\]

\[
= {\int }_{-\alpha }^{\alpha }\frac{2}{\sqrt{1 - {y}^{2}}}\arctan \sqrt{\frac{1 - y}{1 + y}}\mathrm{\;d}y
\]

\[
= {\int }_{0}^{\alpha }\frac{2}{\sqrt{1 - {y}^{2}}}\left( {\arctan \sqrt{\frac{1 - y}{1 + y}} + \arctan \sqrt{\frac{1 + y}{1 - y}}}\right) \mathrm{d}y
\]

\[
= \pi {\int }_{0}^{\alpha }\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\sqrt{1 - {y}^{2}}} = \pi \arcsin \alpha .
\]

注 1 也可以用下面的方法求 \( I\left( \alpha \right) \) :

\[
I\left( \alpha \right)  = {\int }_{-\alpha }^{\alpha }\mathrm{d}y{\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1 + y\cos x} = {\int }_{-\alpha }^{\alpha }\mathrm{d}y{\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\frac{\left( {1 + y\cos x}\right) \mathrm{d}x}{1 - {y}^{2}{\cos }^{2}x}.
\]

由于 \( {\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\frac{y\cos x\mathrm{\;d}x}{1 - {y}^{2}{\cos }^{2}x} \) 是 \( y \) 的奇函数,于是 \( {\int }_{-\alpha }^{\alpha }\mathrm{d}y{\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\frac{y\cos x\mathrm{\;d}x}{1 - {y}^{2}{\cos }^{2}x} = 0 \) ,从而

\[
I\left( \alpha \right)  = {\int }_{-\alpha }^{\alpha }\mathrm{d}y{\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1 - {y}^{2}{\cos }^{2}x} = 2{\int }_{0}^{\alpha }\mathrm{d}y{\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{\mathrm{d}u}{{u}^{2} + \left( {1 - {y}^{2}}\right) }
\]

\[
= 2{\int }_{0}^{\alpha }\frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - {y}^{2}}} \cdot  \frac{\pi }{2}\mathrm{\;d}y = \pi \arcsin \alpha .
\]

注 2 还可以用对参变量 \( \alpha \) 求导的方法求 \( I\left( \alpha \right) \) .

例题 23.1.5 证明:

\[
{\int }_{0}^{2\pi }{\mathrm{e}}^{t\cos \theta }\cos \left( {t\sin \theta }\right) \mathrm{d}\theta  = {2\pi }.
\]

分析 注意上式左端是含参变量 \( t \) 的积分. 设 \( f\left( t\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{2\pi }{\mathrm{e}}^{t\cos \theta }\cos \left( {t\sin \theta }\right) \mathrm{d}\theta \) . 要证明 \( f\left( t\right)  \equiv  {2\pi } \) ,即 \( f\left( t\right) \) 为常函数. 显然有 \( f\left( 0\right)  = {2\pi } \) ,于是只要证明 \( {f}^{\prime }\left( t\right)  \equiv  0 \) .

证 1 由求导性质

\[
{f}^{\prime }\left( t\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{2\pi }{\mathrm{e}}^{t\cos \theta }\cos \theta \cos \left( {t\sin \theta }\right) \mathrm{d}\theta  - {\int }_{0}^{2\pi }{\mathrm{e}}^{t\cos \theta }\sin \left( {t\sin \theta }\right) \sin \theta \mathrm{d}\theta
\]

\[
= {\int }_{0}^{2\pi }{\mathrm{e}}^{t\cos \theta }\cos \left( {t\sin \theta  + \theta }\right) \mathrm{d}\theta
\]

容易归纳证明

\[
{f}^{\left( n\right) }\left( t\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{2\pi }{\mathrm{e}}^{t\cos \theta }\cos \left( {t\sin \theta  + {n\theta }}\right) \mathrm{d}\theta .
\]

于是

\[
{f}^{\left( n\right) }\left( 0\right)  = 0,\;n = 1,2,\cdots .
\]

从而可利用 Taylor 展式

\[
f\left( t\right)  = f\left( 0\right)  + 0 + \cdots  + 0 + \frac{{t}^{n}}{n!}{f}^{\left( n\right) }\left( \xi \right) ,
\]

其中 \( \xi \) 介于 0 与 \( t \) 之间. 因为

\[
\left| {{f}^{\left( n\right) }\left( \xi \right) }\right|  = \left| {{\int }_{0}^{2\pi }{\mathrm{e}}^{\xi \cos \theta }\cos \left( {\xi \sin \theta  + {n\theta }}\right) \mathrm{d}\theta }\right|  \leq  {\int }_{0}^{2\pi }{\mathrm{e}}^{\left| \xi \right| }\mathrm{d}\theta  \leq  {2\pi }{\mathrm{e}}^{\left| t\right| },
\]

所以对每一个固定的 \( t \in  \left( {-\infty , + \infty }\right) \) 有

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\frac{{t}^{n}}{n!}{f}^{\left( n\right) }\left( \xi \right)  = 0
\]

最后得到

\[
f\left( t\right)  \equiv  f\left( 0\right)  = {2\pi }.
\]

证 2 利用下一章的曲线积分知识, 可以计算

\[
{f}^{\prime }\left( t\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{2\pi }{\mathrm{e}}^{t\cos \theta }\cos \theta \cos \left( {t\sin \theta }\right) \mathrm{d}\theta  - {\int }_{0}^{2\pi }{\mathrm{e}}^{t\cos \theta }\sin \left( {t\sin \theta }\right) \sin \theta \mathrm{d}\theta
\]

\[
= {\oint }_{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} = 1}{\mathrm{e}}^{tx}\left\lbrack  {\cos \left( {ty}\right) \mathrm{d}y + \sin \left( {ty}\right) \mathrm{d}x}\right\rbrack
\]

其中单位圆取逆时针方向. 用 Green 公式得

\[
{f}^{\prime }\left( t\right)  = {\iint }_{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} \leq  1}\left\{  {\frac{\partial }{\partial x}\left\lbrack  {{\mathrm{e}}^{tx}\cos \left( {ty}\right) }\right\rbrack   - \frac{\partial }{\partial y}\left\lbrack  {{\mathrm{e}}^{tx}\sin \left( {ty}\right) }\right\rbrack  }\right\}  \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{\;d}y = 0.
\]

利用含参变量积分的性质,我们可以用嵌入法来计算一些定积分 \( I = \; {\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \) . 这些定积分对应的不定积分不是初等函数,故不能用 Newton-Leibniz 公式进行计算. 嵌入法的步骤是先引入参变量 \( y \) ,构造 \( I\left( y\right)  = {\int }_{a}^{b}g\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}x \) ,使得 \( I\left( {y}_{0}\right)  = I\left( {y}_{0}\right. \) 为某定值 \( ) \) ,求出 \( I\left( y\right) \) 后令 \( y = {y}_{0} \) . 在应用含参变量积分性质时要注意条件检验.

例题 23.1.6 求定积分

\[
I = {\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{\ln \left( {1 + x}\right) }{1 + {x}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x.
\]

解 对这个积分在上册 325 页的例题 10.4.7 中已经给出一种解法, 下面的解法是利用含参变量积分的性质. 由于 \( f\left( x\right)  = \frac{\ln \left( {1 + x}\right) }{1 + {x}^{2}} \) 没有有限形式的原函数,直接积分是不可能的. 引入变量 \( \alpha \) ,定义

\[
I\left( \alpha \right)  = {\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{\ln \left( {1 + {\alpha x}}\right) }{1 + {x}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x.
\]

易见 \( I\left( 1\right)  = I \) . 可以验证 \( f\left( {x,\alpha }\right)  = \frac{\ln \left( {1 + {\alpha x}}\right) }{1 + {x}^{2}} \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack   \times  \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 上满足积分号下求导的条件, 于是

\[
{I}^{\prime }\left( \alpha \right)  = {\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{x}{\left( {1 + {x}^{2}}\right) \left( {1 + {\alpha x}}\right) }\mathrm{d}x
\]

\[
= \frac{1}{1 + {\alpha }^{2}}{\int }_{0}^{1}\left( {\frac{\alpha  + x}{1 + {x}^{2}} - \frac{\alpha }{1 + {\alpha x}}}\right) \mathrm{d}x
\]

\[
= {\left. \frac{1}{1 + {\alpha }^{2}}\left\lbrack  \alpha \arctan x + \frac{1}{2}\ln \left( 1 + {x}^{2}\right)  - \ln \left( 1 + \alpha x\right) \right\rbrack  \right| }_{0}^{1}
\]

\[
= \frac{1}{1 + {\alpha }^{2}}\left\lbrack  {\frac{\pi }{4}\alpha  + \frac{1}{2}\ln 2 - \ln \left( {1 + \alpha }\right) }\right\rbrack  .
\]

由此得出

\[
I\left( 1\right)  = I\left( 0\right)  + {\int }_{0}^{1}{I}^{\prime }\left( \alpha \right) \mathrm{d}\alpha  = {\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{1}{1 + {\alpha }^{2}}\left\lbrack  {\frac{\pi }{4}\alpha  + \frac{1}{2}\ln 2 - \ln \left( {1 + \alpha }\right) }\right\rbrack  \mathrm{d}\alpha
\]

\[
= {\left. \left( \frac{\pi }{8}\ln \left( 1 + {\alpha }^{2}\right)  + \frac{1}{2}\ln 2\arctan \alpha \right) \right| }_{0}^{1} - I\left( 1\right) .
\]

移项得

\[
I\left( 1\right)  = \frac{\pi }{8}\ln 2.
\]
