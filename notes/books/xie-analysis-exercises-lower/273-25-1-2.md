#### 25.1.2 第一型曲面积分的应用

1. 求曲面的面积. 在 (25.2) 与 (25.1) 中令 \( f \equiv  1 \) ,则曲面 \( S \) 的面积为

\[
{\iint }_{S}\mathrm{\;d}S = {\iint }_{{D}_{xy}}\sqrt{1 + {z}_{x}^{2} + {z}_{y}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x\mathrm{\;d}y = {\iint }_{{D}_{uv}}\sqrt{{EG} - {F}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}u\mathrm{\;d}v.
\]

2. 求曲面的质量. 设曲面 \( S \) 的面密度为 \( \rho \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) ,则它的质量

\[
m = {\iint }_{S}\rho \left( {x, y, z}\right) \mathrm{d}S
\]

\[
= {\iint }_{D}\rho \left( {x\left( {u, v}\right) , y\left( {u, v}\right) , z\left( {u, v}\right) }\right) \sqrt{{EG} - {F}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}u\mathrm{\;d}v.
\]

3. 求曲面的质心坐标. 曲面 \( S \) 的质心坐标 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right) \) 由下面的公式确定:

\[
{x}_{0} = \frac{1}{m}{\iint }_{S}{x\rho }\left( {x, y, z}\right) \mathrm{d}S
\]

\[
{y}_{0} = \frac{1}{m}{\iint }_{S}{y\rho }\left( {x, y, z}\right) \mathrm{d}S
\]

\[
{z}_{0} = \frac{1}{m}{\iint }_{S}{z\rho }\left( {x, y, z}\right) \mathrm{d}S
\]

例题 25.1.2 求上半球面 \( z = \sqrt{{a}^{2} - {x}^{2} - {y}^{2}} \) 被 \( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} = {ax} \) 截取部分的面积与质心坐标,其中 \( a > 0 \) .

解 见图 22.11, 这就是 Viviani 体的上表面. 由于

\[
{z}_{x} = \frac{-x}{\sqrt{{a}^{2} - {x}^{2} - {y}^{2}}},\;{z}_{y} = \frac{-y}{\sqrt{{a}^{2} - {x}^{2} - {y}^{2}}},
\]

故所求面积

\[
S = {\iint }_{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} \leq  {ax}}\frac{a}{\sqrt{{a}^{2} - {x}^{2} - {y}^{2}}}\mathrm{\;d}x\mathrm{\;d}y
\]

\[
= {\int }_{-\pi /2}^{\pi /2}\mathrm{\;d}\theta {\int }_{0}^{a\cos \theta }\frac{ar}{\sqrt{{a}^{2} - {r}^{2}}}\mathrm{\;d}r
\]

\[
= \left( {\pi  - 2}\right) {a}^{2}\text{ . }
\]

下面求质心坐标，由对称性知 \( {y}_{0} = 0 \) ，且

\[
{x}_{0} = \frac{1}{\left( {\pi  - 2}\right) {a}^{2}}{\iint }_{S}x\mathrm{\;d}S
\]

\[
= \frac{2}{\left( {\pi  - 2}\right) {a}^{2}}{\iint }_{\begin{matrix} {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} \leq  {ax}} \\  {y \geq  0} \end{matrix}}\frac{ax}{\sqrt{{a}^{2} - {x}^{2} - {y}^{2}}}\mathrm{\;d}x\mathrm{\;d}y
\]

\[
= \frac{2}{\left( {\pi  - 2}\right) a}{\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\cos \theta \mathrm{d}\theta {\int }_{0}^{a\cos \theta }\frac{r}{\sqrt{{a}^{2} - {r}^{2}}}r\mathrm{\;d}r\;\left( {r = a\cos t}\right)
\]

\[
= \frac{2}{\left( {\pi  - 2}\right) a}{\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\cos \theta \mathrm{d}\theta {\int }_{\pi /2}^{\theta }\frac{{a}^{2}{\cos }^{2}t}{a\sin t}\left( {-a\sin t}\right) \mathrm{d}t
\]

\[
= \frac{a}{\pi  - 2}{\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\cos \theta \left( {\frac{\pi }{2} - \theta  - \sin \theta \cos \theta }\right) \mathrm{d}\theta
\]

\[
= \frac{2a}{3\left( {\pi  - 2}\right) }
\]

\[
{z}_{0} = \frac{1}{\left( {\pi  - 2}\right) {a}^{2}}{\iint }_{S}z\mathrm{\;d}S
\]

\[
= \frac{1}{\left( {\pi  - 2}\right) {a}^{2}}{\iint }_{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} \leq  {ax}}\frac{a}{\sqrt{{a}^{2} - {x}^{2} - {y}^{2}}}\sqrt{{a}^{2} - {x}^{2} - {y}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x\mathrm{\;d}y
\]

\[
= \frac{\pi a}{4\left( {\pi  - 2}\right) }.
\]
