#### 18.1.6 练习题

1. 求下列极限:

(1) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\left( {x, y}\right)  \rightarrow  \left( {0, a}\right) }}\frac{\sin {xy}}{x} \) ;

(2) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\left( {x, y}\right)  \rightarrow  \left( {0,0}\right) }}\left( {x + y}\right) \ln \left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}}\right) \) ;

(3) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\left( {x, y}\right)  \rightarrow  \left( {0,0}\right) }}f\left( {x, y}\right) \) ,其中

\[
f\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \frac{\ln \left( {1 + {xy}}\right) }{x}, & \text{ 当 }x \neq  0, \\  y, & \text{ 当 }x = 0; \end{array}\right.
\]

(4) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\left( {x, y}\right)  \rightarrow  \left( {\infty ,0}\right) }}{\left( 1 + \frac{1}{x}\right) }^{\frac{{x}^{2}}{x + y}} \) ;

(5) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\left( {x, y}\right)  \rightarrow  \left( {\infty ,\infty }\right) }}\frac{x + y}{{x}^{2} - {xy} + {y}^{2}} \) .

2. 证明: \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\left( {x, y}\right)  \rightarrow  \left( {0,0}\right) }}\frac{{x}^{2}{y}^{2}}{{x}^{3} + {y}^{3}} \) 不存在.

3. 讨论下列函数在点 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 的重极限与累次极限:

(1) \( \frac{{x}^{2}{y}^{2}}{{x}^{2}{y}^{2} + {\left( x - y\right) }^{2}} \) ;

(2) \( \left( {x + y}\right) \sin \frac{1}{x}\sin \frac{1}{y} \) ;

(3) \( f\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \frac{xy}{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}} + y\sin \frac{1}{x}, & \text{ 当 }x \neq  0, \\  0, & \text{ 当 }x = 0. \end{array}\right. \)

4. 设一元函数 \( f\left( t\right) \) 在 \( \mathbf{R} \) 上有连续导数,定义二元函数

\[
g\left( {x, y}\right)  = \frac{f\left( x\right)  - f\left( y\right) }{x - y}\text{ ,当 }x \neq  y,
\]

求 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\left( {x, y}\right)  \rightarrow  \left( {t, t}\right) }}g\left( {x, y}\right) \) .

5. 叙述并证明二元函数极限存在的惟一性定理、局部有界性定理与局部保号性定理.

6. 设 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{y \rightarrow  {y}_{0}}}\varphi \left( y\right)  = A,\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  {x}_{0}}}\psi \left( x\right)  = 0 \) ,且在点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 附近有 \( \left| {f\left( {x, y}\right)  - \varphi \left( y\right) }\right|  \leq  \psi \left( x\right) \) , 证明: \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\left( {x, y}\right)  \rightarrow  \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) }}f\left( {x, y}\right)  = A \) .

7. 证明: 在命题 18.1.2 中若 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 除直线 \( x = {x}_{0}, y = {y}_{0} \) 外有定义,其他条件不变, 则重极限与两个累次极限都存在, 并且相等.
