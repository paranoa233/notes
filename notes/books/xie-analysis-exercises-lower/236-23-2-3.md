#### 23.2.3 练习题

1. 讨论下列广义积分的一致收敛性:

(1) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }{\mathrm{e}}^{-\left( {1 + {a}^{2}}\right) t}\sin t\mathrm{\;d}t, a \in  \left( {-\infty , + \infty }\right) \) ;

(2) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{\cos {xy}}{\sqrt{x + y}}\mathrm{\;d}x, y \in  \left\lbrack  {{y}_{0}, + \infty }\right) \) ,其中 \( {y}_{0} > 0 \) ;

(3) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }{\mathrm{e}}^{-t{x}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x, t \in  \left( {0, + \infty }\right) \) ;

(4) \( {\int }_{1}^{+\infty }{\mathrm{e}}^{-{\alpha x}}\frac{\cos x}{\sqrt{x}}\mathrm{\;d}x,\alpha  \in  \lbrack 0, + \infty ) \) ;

(5) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }{\mathrm{e}}^{-{\left( x - y\right) }^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x, y \in  \left( {-\infty , + \infty }\right) \) ;

(6) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }x\ln x{\mathrm{e}}^{-t\sqrt{x}}\mathrm{\;d}x,\left( 1\right) t \in  \lbrack {t}_{0}, + \infty ) \) ,其中 \( {t}_{0} > 0,\left( 2\right) t \in  \left( {0, + \infty }\right) \)

(7) \( {\int }_{1}^{+\infty }\frac{1 - {\mathrm{e}}^{-{ut}}}{t}\cos t\mathrm{\;d}t, u \in  \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \)

(8) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{\alpha t}{1 + {\alpha }^{2} + {t}^{2}} \cdot  {\mathrm{e}}^{-{\alpha }^{2}{t}^{2}}\cos {\alpha }^{2}{t}^{2}\mathrm{\;d}t,\alpha  \in  \left( {0, + \infty }\right) \) ;

(9) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }{\mathrm{e}}^{-{x}^{2}\left( {1 + {y}^{2}}\right) }\sin y\mathrm{\;d}y, x \in  \left( {0, + \infty }\right) \) ;

(10) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{\alpha \mathrm{d}x}{1 + {\alpha }^{2}{x}^{2}},\alpha  \in  \left( {0,1}\right) \)

(11) \( {\int }_{0}^{2}\frac{{x}^{t}}{\sqrt[3]{\left( {x - 1}\right) \left( {x - 2}\right) }}\mathrm{d}x,\left| t\right|  < \frac{1}{2} \) ;

(12) \( {\int }_{0}^{1}{\left( 1 - x\right) }^{u - 1}\mathrm{\;d}x,\left( 1\right) u \in  \lbrack a, + \infty ) \) ,其中 \( a > 0,\left( 2\right) u \in  \left( {0, + \infty }\right) \) .

2. 设 \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }{x}^{\lambda }f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \) 当 \( \lambda  = a,\lambda  = b \) 时收敛 \( \left( {a < b}\right) \) . 证明: \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }{x}^{\lambda }f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \) 关于 \( \lambda  \in  \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 一致收敛.

3. 证明: 积分 \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }x{\mathrm{e}}^{-{xy}}\mathrm{\;d}y \) 在 \( \left( {0, + \infty }\right) \) 上不一致收敛.
