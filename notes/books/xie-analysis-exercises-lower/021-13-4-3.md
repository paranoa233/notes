#### 13.4.3 练习题

1. 讨论下列无穷乘积的敛散性 \( {}^{ \oplus  } \) :

(1) \( \mathop{\prod }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }\sqrt{\frac{n + 1}{n + 2}} \) (2) \( \mathop{\prod }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }\left( {1 + {x}^{{2n} + 1}}\right) \)

(3) \( \mathop{\prod }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }\sqrt[n]{1 + \frac{1}{n}} \) (4) \( \mathop{\prod }\limits_{{n = 2}}^{\infty }{\left( \frac{{n}^{2} - 1}{{n}^{2} + 1}\right) }^{p} \)

(5) \( \mathop{\prod }\limits_{{n = 2}}^{\infty }\left\lbrack  {1 + {\left( -1\right) }^{n}\frac{1}{n}}\right\rbrack \) ; (6) \( \mathop{\prod }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }\left\lbrack  {\frac{1}{\mathrm{e}}{\left( 1 + \frac{1}{n}\right) }^{n}}\right\rbrack \) .

2. 设 \( a, b > 0 \) ,求 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\frac{\left( {a + 1}\right) \left( {a + 2}\right) \cdots \left( {a + n}\right) }{\left( {b + 1}\right) \left( {b + 2}\right) \cdots \left( {b + n}\right) } \) .

3. 设数列 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 的每一项满足条件 \( 0 < {a}_{n} < \frac{\pi }{2} \) ,证明: 级数 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }{a}_{n}^{2} \) 收敛的充分必要条件是无穷乘积 \( \mathop{\prod }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }\cos {a}_{n} \) 收敛.

4. 如果对正数数列 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 存在极限 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}n\left( {\frac{{a}_{n}}{{a}_{n + 1}} - 1}\right)  = r > 0 \) ,证明: 级数 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }{\left( -1\right) }^{n - 1}{a}_{n} \) 收敛. (可利用例题 13.4.2.)

5. 讨论无穷乘积 \( \mathop{\prod }\limits_{{n = {n}_{0}}}^{\infty }\frac{\left( {\alpha  + n}\right) \left( {\beta  + n}\right) }{\left( {1 + n}\right) \left( {\gamma  + n}\right) } \) 的敛散性,其中 \( \alpha ,\beta ,\gamma \) 为参数,且设 \( {n}_{0} \) 已足够大, 使得乘积中出现的所有因子均大于 0 .

6. (Euler) 对于 \( 0 < x < 1 \) ,证明:

\[
\left( {1 + x}\right) \left( {1 + {x}^{2}}\right) \left( {1 + {x}^{3}}\right) \cdots  = \frac{1}{\left( {1 - x}\right) \left( {1 - {x}^{3}}\right) \left( {1 - {x}^{5}}\right) \cdots }.
\]

7. (Stirling) 证明: 若级数 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }{a}_{n}\left( {{x}^{2} - 1}\right) \left( {{x}^{2} - {2}^{2}}\right) \cdots \left( {{x}^{2} - {n}^{2}}\right) \) 于某个 \( x \) 值收敛, 则级数对任何 \( x \) 值都收敛.

8. (Landau (兰道)) 证明: 级数

\[
\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }\frac{n!{a}_{n}}{x\left( {x + 1}\right) \cdots \left( {x + n}\right) }\;\left( {x \neq  0, - 1, - 2,\cdots }\right)
\]

与 Dirichlet 级数 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }\frac{{a}_{n}}{{n}^{x}} \) 对同样的 \( x \) 值收敛.

9. 设正数数列 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 满足条件 \( \frac{{a}_{n}}{{a}_{n + 1}} = 1 + \frac{1}{n} + O\left( {b}_{n}\right) \) ,并已知级数 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }{b}_{n} \) 绝对收敛,证明: 级数 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }{a}_{n} \) 发散. (可参考第 8 页的 Gauss 判别法.)

10. (Stirling 公式的又一证明) 用无穷乘积方法证明: 数列 \( \left\{  \frac{n!{\mathrm{e}}^{n}}{{n}^{n + \frac{1}{2}}}\right\} \) 有非零极限, 并求出此极限.
