#### 23.2.6 练习题

1. 设 \( {\int }_{a}^{+\infty }f\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}y \) 对于 \( x \) 在 \( {x}_{0} \) 的邻域 \( U\left( {x}_{0}\right) \) 中收敛， \( {f}_{x}\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( U\left( {x}_{0}\right)  \times  \lbrack a, + \infty ) \) 中存在,且当 \( x \rightarrow  {x}_{0} \) 时, \( {f}_{x}\left( {x, y}\right) \) 关于 \( y \) 在任何有限区间上一致收敛于 \( {f}_{x}\left( {{x}_{0}, y}\right) \) , 又知积分 \( {\int }_{a}^{+\infty }{f}_{x}\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}y \) 在 \( U\left( {x}_{0}\right) \) 上一致收敛. 证明: \( {\left. \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\left( {\int }_{a}^{+\infty }f\left( x, y\right) \mathrm{d}y\right) \right| }_{x = {x}_{0}} \) 存在且等于 \( {\int }_{a}^{+\infty }{f}_{x}\left( {{x}_{0}, y}\right) \mathrm{d}y \) .

2. \( F\left( \alpha \right)  = {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{\sin \left( {1 - {\alpha }^{2}}\right) x}{x}\mathrm{\;d}x \) ,证明: \( F\left( \alpha \right) \) 在 \( \left( {-\infty , - 1}\right)  \cup  \left( {-1,1}\right)  \cup  \left( {1, + \infty }\right) \) 上连续,在 \( \alpha  =  \pm  1 \) 处间断.

3. 证明: \( F\left( \alpha \right)  = {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{\sin \alpha {x}^{2}}{x}\mathrm{\;d}x \) 在 \( \alpha  \in  \left( {0, + \infty }\right) \) 上不一致收敛,但在 \( \left( {0, + \infty }\right) \) 上连续.

4. 证明: \( F\left( x\right)  = {\int }_{1}^{+\infty }\frac{x{\mathrm{e}}^{-{yx}}}{y}\mathrm{\;d}y \) 在 \( \lbrack 0, + \infty ) \) 上连续,在 \( \left( {0, + \infty }\right) \) 上 \( {F}^{\prime }\left( x\right) \) 存在,且

\[
{F}^{\prime }\left( x\right)  = {\int }_{1}^{+\infty }\frac{\partial }{\partial x}\left( {\frac{x}{y}{\mathrm{e}}^{-{yx}}}\right) \mathrm{d}y.
\]

5. 设 \( F\left( \alpha \right)  = {\int }_{0}^{\pi }\frac{\sin x}{{x}^{\alpha }{\left( \pi  - x\right) }^{2 - \alpha }}\mathrm{d}x \) ,证明: \( F\left( \alpha \right) \) 在 \( \left( {0,2}\right) \) 中连续.

6. 设 \( F\left( y\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }y{\mathrm{e}}^{-{x}^{2}{y}^{2}}\cos \left\lbrack  {x\left( {1 - y}\right) }\right\rbrack  \mathrm{d}x \) ,求 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{y \rightarrow  1}}F\left( y\right) \) .

7. 利用 Dirichlet 积分或 Euler-Poisson 积分求下列积分值:

(1) \( {\int }_{-\infty }^{+\infty }{\left( \frac{\sin x}{x}\right) }^{2}\mathrm{\;d}x \)

(2) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{{\sin }^{3}x}{x}\mathrm{\;d}x \)

(3) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }{x}^{2}{\mathrm{e}}^{-\alpha {x}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x\left( {\alpha  > 0}\right) \) ;

(4) \( {\int }_{-\infty }^{+\infty }{\mathrm{e}}^{-\left( {a{x}^{2} + {bx} + c}\right) }\mathrm{\;d}x\left( {a > 0}\right) \) .

8. 利用积分号下求导的方法求下列积分:

(1) \( I\left( a\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }{\mathrm{e}}^{-{ax}}\frac{\sin x}{x}\mathrm{\;d}x\;\left( {a \geq  0}\right) \) ;

(2) \( I\left( \alpha \right)  = {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{\arctan {\alpha x}}{x\left( {1 + {x}^{2}}\right) }\mathrm{d}x\;\left( {\alpha  \geq  0}\right) \) ;

(3) \( f\left( x\right)  = {\int }_{1}^{+\infty }\frac{1}{y}x{\mathrm{e}}^{-{yx}}\mathrm{\;d}y\;\left( {x \geq  0}\right) \) .

9. 利用积分号下求积分的方法求下列积分:

(1) \( I\left( \alpha \right)  = {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{\arctan {\alpha x}}{x\left( {1 + {x}^{2}}\right) }\mathrm{d}x\left( {\alpha  \geq  0}\right) \) (提示: \( \frac{\arctan {\alpha x}}{x} = {\int }_{0}^{\alpha }\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{1 + {y}^{2}{x}^{2}} \) );

(2) \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{\cos {\beta x}}{{x}^{2} + {\alpha }^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) (提示: \( \frac{1}{{x}^{2} + {\alpha }^{2}} = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\varepsilon  \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}{\int }_{\varepsilon }^{+\infty }{\mathrm{e}}^{-t\left( {{x}^{2} + {\alpha }^{2}}\right) }\mathrm{d}t \) ).

10. 求含参变量瑕积分

\[
I\left( \alpha \right)  = {\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{\ln \left( {1 - {\alpha }^{2}{x}^{2}}\right) }{\sqrt{1 - {x}^{2}}}\mathrm{\;d}x\;\left( {\left| \alpha \right|  \leq  1}\right) .
\]

11. 计算

I\left( y\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }{\mathrm{e}}^{-{x}^{2}}\cos \left( {2yx}\right) \mathrm{d}x\;\left( {-\infty  < y <  + \infty }\right) .
