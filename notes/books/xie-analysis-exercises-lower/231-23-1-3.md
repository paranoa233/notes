#### 23.1.3 练习题

1. 求 \( F\left( \theta \right)  = {\int }_{0}^{\pi }\ln \left( {1 + \theta \cos x}\right) \mathrm{d}x\;\left( {\left| \theta \right|  < 1}\right) \) .

2. 设 \( f\left( {s, t}\right) \) 为可微函数, \( F\left( x\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{x}\mathrm{\;d}t{\int }_{{t}^{2}}^{{x}^{2}}f\left( {t, s}\right) \mathrm{d}s \) . 求 \( {F}^{\prime }\left( x\right) \) .

3. 设 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( \left( {a, b}\right)  \times  \left( {c, d}\right) \) 上连续有界,证明:

\[
I\left( x\right)  = {\int }_{c}^{d}f\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}y
\]

在 \( \left( {a, b}\right) \) 上连续.

4. 设 \( I\left( a\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{2\pi }\ln \left( {{R}^{2} + {a}^{2} - {2aR}\cos \theta }\right) \mathrm{d}\theta ,\left| a\right|  < R \) ,证明: \( {I}^{\prime }\left( a\right)  = 0 \) .

5. 设 \( a, b > 0 \) ,求 \( {\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{{x}^{b} - {x}^{a}}{\ln x}\sin \left( {\ln \frac{1}{x}}\right) \mathrm{d}x \) .

6. 设 \( F\left( t\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{a}\mathrm{\;d}x{\int }_{0}^{a}f\left( {x + y + t}\right) \mathrm{d}y \) ,其中 \( f \) 为连续函数,证明:

\[
{F}^{\prime \prime }\left( t\right)  = f\left( {t + {2a}}\right)  - {2f}\left( {t + a}\right)  + f\left( t\right) .
\]

7. 设 \( f\left( t\right)  = {\left( {\int }_{0}^{t}{\mathrm{e}}^{-{x}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x\right) }^{2}, g\left( t\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{{\mathrm{e}}^{-{t}^{2}\left( {1 + {x}^{2}}\right) }}{1 + {x}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) . 证明: \( f\left( t\right)  + g\left( t\right)  \equiv  \frac{\pi }{4} \) ,并由此计算 \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }{\mathrm{e}}^{-{x}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x \) .
