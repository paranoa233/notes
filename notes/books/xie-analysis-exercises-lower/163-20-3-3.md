#### 20.3.3 练习题

1. 把方程 \( \left( {x - y}\right) \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} + y\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = 0 \) 变为 \( x \) 作因变量, \( y, z \) 为自变量的形式.

2. 引用新函数 \( r = \sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}},\varphi  = \arctan \frac{y}{x} \) 变换微分式

\[
w = x\frac{{\mathrm{d}}^{2}y}{\mathrm{\;d}{t}^{2}} - y\frac{{\mathrm{d}}^{2}x}{\mathrm{\;d}{t}^{2}}.
\]

3. 以 \( \xi  = x + y,\eta  = x - y \) 为新自变量,变换方程 \( \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} \) .

4. 取 \( u = y + z{\mathrm{e}}^{-x}, v = x + z{\mathrm{e}}^{-y} \) 为新的自变量,变换微分式

\[
F = \left( {z + {\mathrm{e}}^{x}}\right) \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} + \left( {z + {\mathrm{e}}^{y}}\right) \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} - \left( {{z}^{2} - {\mathrm{e}}^{x + y}}\right) .
\]

5. 设 \( u = x{\mathrm{e}}^{z}, v = y{\mathrm{e}}^{z}, w = z{\mathrm{e}}^{z} \) ,试以 \( w \) 为新的因变量, \( u, v \) 为新的自变量,变换方程 \( \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} \) .

6. 以 \( w \) 为新的因变量, \( \xi ,\eta ,\zeta \) 为新的自变量,变换方程

\[
x\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} + y\frac{\partial u}{\partial y} + z\frac{\partial u}{\partial z} = u + \frac{xy}{z},
\]

其中 \( \xi  = \frac{x}{z},\eta  = \frac{y}{z},\zeta  = z, w = \frac{u}{z} \) .

7. 试求 \( \frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {x}^{2}} + \frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {y}^{2}} + \frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {z}^{2}} = 0 \) 在球坐标下的形式.

8. 以 \( u = x + {2y} + 2, v = x - y - 1 \) 为新的自变量,变换方程

\[
2\frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial {x}^{2}} + \frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial x\partial y} - \frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial {y}^{2}} + \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = 0.
\]

9. 以 \( w \) 为新的因变量, \( u, v \) 为新的自变量,变换方程

\[
\frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial {x}^{2}} - 2\frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial x\partial y} + \left( {1 + \frac{y}{x}}\right) \frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial {y}^{2}} = 0,
\]

其中 \( u = x, v = x + y, w = x + y + z \) .

10. 设 \( {xu} = {x}^{2} + {y}^{2},{yv} = {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} \) ,证明: \( \frac{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) }{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) } =  - \frac{uv}{xy} \) .

11. 若 \( u\left( {x, y, z}\right) , v\left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 可微,证明:

\[
\frac{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) }{\partial \left( {y, z}\right) }\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) }{\partial \left( {z, x}\right) }\frac{\partial u}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) }{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) }\frac{\partial u}{\partial z} = 0.
\]

12. 设 \( u = {xy}, v = \frac{x}{y} \) ,试求 \( \frac{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) }{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) },\frac{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) }{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) } \) .

13. 设 \( u = \frac{x}{{r}^{2}}, v = \frac{y}{{r}^{2}}, w = \frac{z}{{r}^{2}} \) ,其中 \( {r}^{2} = {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2} \) ,试求 \( \frac{\partial \left( {u, v, w}\right) }{\partial \left( {x, y, z}\right) } \) .
