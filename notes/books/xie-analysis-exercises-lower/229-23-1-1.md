#### 23.1.1 定义与性质

在实际问题中我们经常会遇到带参数的积分,比如椭圆 \( \frac{{x}^{2}}{{a}^{2}} + \frac{{y}^{2}}{{b}^{2}} = 1(b > \; a > 0) \) 的弧长为

\[
l = 4{\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\sqrt{{a}^{2}{\sin }^{2}t + {b}^{2}{\cos }^{2}t}\mathrm{\;d}t = {4b}{\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\sqrt{1 - {k}^{2}{\sin }^{2}t}\mathrm{\;d}t,
\]

其中 \( k = \frac{\sqrt{{b}^{2} - {a}^{2}}}{b} \) 是离心率. 这里 \( I\left( k\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{\pi /2}\sqrt{1 - {k}^{2}{\sin }^{2}t}\mathrm{\;d}t \) 称为第二类完全椭圆积分,它不能用初等函数表示 \( {}^{\text{ ① }} \) . \( I\left( k\right) \) 就是含参变量积分的一个例子. 一般地, 设 \( D \subset  \mathbf{R} \) ,二元函数 \( f\left( {x, t}\right) \) 定义在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack   \times  D \) 上, \( \forall t \in  D \) ,设 \( f\left( {x, t}\right) \) 作为 \( x \) 的函数在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 上常义可积,即通常所说的有界可积,则

\[
\varphi \left( t\right)  = {\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( {x, t}\right) \mathrm{d}x
\]

是定义在 \( D \) 上的含参变量常义积分. 它具有如下主要性质.

命题 23.1.1 (极限性质) 设 \( {t}_{0} \) 是 \( D \) 的聚点,如果 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  {t}_{0}}}f\left( {x, t}\right)  = \psi \left( x\right) \) ,且收敛关于 \( x \in  \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 是一致的,则 \( \psi \left( x\right) \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 上有界可积,且

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  {t}_{0}}}\varphi \left( t\right)  = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  {t}_{0}}}{\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( {x, t}\right) \mathrm{d}x = {\int }_{a}^{b}\psi \left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x.
\]

命题 23.1.2 (连续性) 设 \( f\left( {x, t}\right) \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack   \times  \left\lbrack  {c, d}\right\rbrack \) 上连续,则

\[
\varphi \left( t\right)  = {\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( {x, t}\right) \mathrm{d}x
\]

在 \( \left\lbrack  {c, d}\right\rbrack \) 上连续.

命题 23.1.3 (交换积分次序) 设 \( f\left( {x, t}\right) \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack   \times  \left\lbrack  {c, d}\right\rbrack \) 上连续，则

\[
{\int }_{c}^{d}\mathrm{\;d}t{\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( {x, t}\right) \mathrm{d}x = {\int }_{a}^{b}\mathrm{\;d}x{\int }_{c}^{d}f\left( {x, t}\right) \mathrm{d}t.
\]

① 关于椭圆弧长以及椭圆积分参见上册 356 页和第十六章例题 16.1.1.

---

命题 23.1.4 (可微性) 设 \( f\left( {x, t}\right) ,{f}_{t}\left( {x, t}\right) \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack   \times  \left\lbrack  {c, d}\right\rbrack \) 上连续,则

\[
\varphi \left( t\right)  = {\int }_{a}^{b}f\left( {x, t}\right) \mathrm{d}x
\]

在 \( \left\lbrack  {c, d}\right\rbrack \) 上可导,且

\[
{\varphi }^{\prime }\left( t\right)  = {\int }_{a}^{b}{f}_{t}\left( {x, t}\right) \mathrm{d}x.
\]

如果在积分限中也含有参变量, 则我们有如下更一般的结论.

命题 23.1.5 设 \( f\left( {x, t}\right) \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack   \times  \left\lbrack  {c, d}\right\rbrack \) 上连续, \( \alpha \left( t\right) \) 和 \( \beta \left( t\right) \) 都在 \( \left\lbrack  {c, d}\right\rbrack \) 上连续, 且

\[
a \leq  \alpha \left( t\right) ,\beta \left( t\right)  \leq  b,\forall t \in  \left\lbrack  {c, d}\right\rbrack  ,
\]

则

\[
\varphi \left( t\right)  = {\int }_{\alpha \left( t\right) }^{\beta \left( t\right) }f\left( {x, t}\right) \mathrm{d}x
\]

在 \( \left\lbrack  {c, d}\right\rbrack \) 上连续. 进一步若 \( {f}_{t}\left( {x, t}\right) \) 也在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack   \times  \left\lbrack  {c, d}\right\rbrack \) 上连续, \( \alpha \left( t\right) \) 和 \( \beta \left( t\right) \) 都在 \( \left\lbrack  {c, d}\right\rbrack \) 上可导,则 \( \varphi \left( t\right) \) 也在 \( \left\lbrack  {c, d}\right\rbrack \) 上可导,且

\[
{\varphi }^{\prime }\left( t\right)  = f\left( {\beta \left( t\right) , t}\right) {\beta }^{\prime }\left( t\right)  - f\left( {\alpha \left( t\right) , t}\right) {\alpha }^{\prime }\left( t\right)  + {\int }_{\alpha \left( t\right) }^{\beta \left( t\right) }{f}_{t}\left( {x, t}\right) \mathrm{d}x.
\]

例题 23.1.1 设 \( f\left( x\right) \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 上连续,考察函数

\[
F\left( t\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{t}{{x}^{2} + {t}^{2}}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x
\]

的连续性.

解 显然 \( F\left( t\right) \) 定义在 \( \left( {-\infty , + \infty }\right) \) 上且 \( \forall {t}_{0} \neq  0, h\left( {x, t}\right)  = \frac{{tf}\left( x\right) }{{x}^{2} + {t}^{2}} \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack   \times \; \left\lbrack  {\frac{1}{2}{t}_{0},2{t}_{0}}\right\rbrack \) 上连续 (或在 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack   \times  \left\lbrack  {2{t}_{0},\frac{1}{2}{t}_{0}}\right\rbrack \) 上连续),由命题 23.1.2 知 \( F\left( t\right) \) 在 \( {t}_{0} \) 点连续.

下面讨论 \( F\left( t\right) \) 在 \( t = 0 \) 处的连续性. 我们先考虑

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}F\left( t\right)  = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}{\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{t}{{x}^{2} + {t}^{2}}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x.
\]

注意到

\[
{\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{t}{{x}^{2} + {t}^{2}}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = {\int }_{0}^{{t}^{1/3}}\frac{t}{{x}^{2} + {t}^{2}}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x + {\int }_{{t}^{1/3}}^{1}\frac{t}{{x}^{2} + {t}^{2}}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x.
\]

而当 \( t \rightarrow  {0}^{ + } \) 时,

\[
{\int }_{0}^{{t}^{1/3}}\frac{t}{{x}^{2} + {t}^{2}}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = f\left( \xi \right) \arctan \frac{{t}^{1/3}}{t} \rightarrow  f\left( 0\right) \frac{\pi }{2},
\]

\[
\left| {{\int }_{{t}^{1/3}}^{1}\frac{t}{{x}^{2} + {t}^{2}}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x}\right|  \leq  \mathop{\max }\limits_{{0 \leq  x \leq  1}}\{ \left| {f\left( x\right) }\right| \}  \cdot  \frac{t}{{t}^{2/3} + {t}^{2}} \rightarrow  0.
\]

因此

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}{\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{t}{{x}^{2} + {t}^{2}}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = f\left( 0\right) \frac{\pi }{2}.
\]

同理可证

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  {0}^{ - }}}F\left( t\right)  =  - f\left( 0\right) \frac{\pi }{2}
\]

所以当 \( f\left( 0\right)  = 0 \) 时， \( F\left( t\right) \) 在点 \( t = 0 \) 处连续，否则在该点处不连续.

例题 23.1.2 设

\[
F\left( t\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{{t}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x{\int }_{x - t}^{x + t}\sin \left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} - {t}^{2}}\right) \mathrm{d}y,
\]

求 \( {F}^{\prime }\left( t\right) \) .

解 令 \( f\left( {x, t}\right)  = {\int }_{x - t}^{x + t}\sin \left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} - {t}^{2}}\right) \mathrm{d}y \) ,其中 \( x, t \) 为参变量. 由含参变量积分的求导公式

而

\[
\frac{\partial }{\partial t}{\int }_{x - t}^{x + t}\sin \left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} - {t}^{2}}\right) \mathrm{d}y
\]

\[
= \sin \left\lbrack  {{x}^{2} + {\left( x + t\right) }^{2} - {t}^{2}}\right\rbrack   - \left( {-1}\right)  \cdot  \sin \left\lbrack  {{x}^{2} + {\left( x - t\right) }^{2} - {t}^{2}}\right\rbrack
\]

\[
+ {\int }_{x - t}^{x + t}\left( {-{2t}}\right) \cos \left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} - {t}^{2}}\right) \mathrm{d}y
\]

\[
= 2\sin 2{x}^{2}\cos {2xt} - {2t}{\int }_{x - t}^{x + t}\cos \left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} - {t}^{2}}\right) \mathrm{d}y.
\]

最后得到

\[
{F}^{\prime }\left( t\right)  = {2t}{\int }_{{t}^{2} - t}^{{t}^{2} + t}\sin \left( {{t}^{4} + {y}^{2} - {t}^{2}}\right) \mathrm{d}y + 2{\int }_{0}^{{t}^{2}}\sin 2{x}^{2}\cos {2xt}\mathrm{\;d}x
\]

\[
- {2t}{\int }_{0}^{{t}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x{\int }_{x - t}^{x + t}\cos \left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} - {t}^{2}}\right) \mathrm{d}y.
\]
