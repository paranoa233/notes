#### 14.3.4 练习题

1. 求级数 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 0}}^{\infty }\frac{{1}^{n} + {2}^{n} + \cdots  + {k}^{n}}{{n}^{2}}{\left( \frac{1 - x}{1 + x}\right) }^{n} \) 的收敛域,其中 \( k > 1 \) 为整数.

2. 设 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 为等差数列, \( {a}_{0} \neq  0 \) ,试求 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 0}}^{\infty }{a}_{n}{x}^{n} \) 的收敛半径.

3. 求幂级数 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 0}}^{\infty }\frac{{a}^{n}}{1 + {a}^{2n}}{x}^{n}\left( {a > 0}\right) \) 的收敛半径.

4. 对任意正整数 \( k \) ,证明: 幂级数 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 0}}^{\infty }\frac{{\left( n!\right) }^{k}}{\left( {kn}\right) !}{x}^{n} \) 的收敛半径为 \( {k}^{k} \) ,收敛域为 \( \left( {-{k}^{k},{k}^{k}}\right) \) .

5. 求下列幂级数(或广义幂级数)的收敛域:

(1) \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }{\left( 1 + \frac{1}{n}\right) }^{{n}^{2}}{x}^{n} \) (2) \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }\frac{{\left( -1\right) }^{n}}{n!}{\left( \frac{n}{\mathrm{e}}\right) }^{n}{x}^{n} \)

(3) \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }\frac{{a}^{n} + {\left( -b\right) }^{n}}{n}{x}^{n}, a, b > 0 \) (4) \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 0}}^{\infty }\frac{{3}^{-\sqrt{n}}}{\sqrt{1 + {n}^{2}}}{x}^{n} \)

(5) \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }\left( {1 + \frac{1}{2} + \cdots  + \frac{1}{n}}\right) {x}^{n} \) (6) \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }{n}^{{n}^{2}}{x}^{{n}^{3}} \)

(7) \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }\sin \frac{1}{3n}{\left( {x}^{2} + x + 1\right) }^{n} \) (8) \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 0}}^{\infty }\frac{n - 1}{{n}^{2} + 1}{\left( \frac{x}{{3x} + 1}\right) }^{n} \) .

6. 求下列幂级数的收敛域与和函数:

(1) \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 2}}^{\infty }{\left( -1\right) }^{n}\frac{{x}^{n + 1}}{{n}^{2} - 1} \) (2) \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }\frac{{\left( -1\right) }^{n - 1}}{n\left( {{2n} - 1}\right) }{x}^{2n} \)

(3) \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }{\left( -1\right) }^{n - 1}\frac{{2n} + 1}{n}{x}^{2n} \) (4) \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }{\left( -1\right) }^{n}{n}^{2}{x}^{n} \)

(5) \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 0}}^{\infty }\left( {{2}^{n + 2} - 1}\right) {x}^{2n} \) (6) \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }n{\left( x - 1\right) }^{n} \) .

7. 证明: \( u\left( x\right)  = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 0}}^{\infty }\frac{1}{\left( {4n}\right) !}{x}^{4n} \) 在 \( \left( {-\infty , + \infty }\right) \) 上满足微分方程 \( {u}^{\left( 4\right) } = u \) .

8. 证明: Bessel (贝塞尔) 函数 \( {J}_{0}\left( x\right)  = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 0}}^{\infty }\frac{{\left( -1\right) }^{n}}{{\left( n!\right) }^{2}{2}^{2n}}{x}^{2n} \) 满足微分方程

\[
x{u}^{\prime \prime } + {u}^{\prime } + {xu} = 0.
\]

9. 设 \( S\left( x\right)  = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }\frac{1}{{n}^{2}\ln \left( {1 + n}\right) }{x}^{n} \) ,证明:

(1) 函数 \( S \in  C\left\lbrack  {-1,1}\right\rbrack \) ;

(2) \( {S}_{ + }^{\prime }\left( {-1}\right) \) 有限，而 \( {S}_{ - }^{\prime }\left( 1\right)  =  + \infty \) .

(可参考上册 194 页命题 7.1.7 和本章 14.3.2 小节的第 7 题.)

10. 设 \( f\left( x\right)  = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }\frac{{2}^{n}}{{n}^{2}}{x}^{n} \) ,证明: 函数 \( f \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {-\frac{1}{2},\frac{1}{2}}\right\rbrack \) 上连续,在 \( \left\lbrack  {-\frac{1}{2},\frac{1}{2}}\right) \) 上可导,并讨论 \( f \) 在 \( \frac{1}{2} \) 处的可导性.

11. 设 \( f\left( x\right)  = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 1}}^{\infty }\frac{{x}^{n}}{{n}^{2}}, - 1 \leq  x \leq  1 \) . 证明: 当 \( x \in  \left( {0,1}\right) \) 时有

\[
f\left( x\right)  + f\left( {1 - x}\right)  + \ln x\ln \left( {1 - x}\right)  = \frac{{\pi }^{2}}{6}.
\]

12. 设幂级数 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 0}}^{\infty }{a}_{n}{x}^{n} \) 的收敛半径 \( R =  + \infty \) ,将级数的部分和函数列记为 \( \left\{  {S}_{n}\right\} \) , 将和函数记为 \( S \) ,证明: 函数列 \( \left\{  {S \circ  {S}_{n}}\right\} \) 在 \( \left( {-\infty , + \infty }\right) \) 上内闭一致收敛于 \( S \circ  S \) .

13. 设级数 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 0}}^{\infty }{a}_{n} \) 发散,记 \( {S}_{n} = {a}_{0} + {a}_{1} + \cdots  + {a}_{n}, n = 0,1,\cdots \) . 证明: (1) 幂级数 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 0}}^{\infty }{a}_{n}{x}^{n} \) 和 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 0}}^{\infty }{S}_{n}{x}^{n} \) 有相同的收敛半径; (2) 若 \( {a}_{n} \geq  0, n = 0,1,\cdots \) ,且 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\frac{{a}_{n}}{{S}_{n}} = 0 \) ,则 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{n = 0}}^{\infty }{a}_{n}{x}^{n} \) 的收敛半径为 1 .
