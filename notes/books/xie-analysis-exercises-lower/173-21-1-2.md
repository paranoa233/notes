#### 21.1.2 曲面的法向量、法线与切平面

光滑曲面 \( z = f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在点 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 处的法向量为

\[
\mathbf{n} =  \pm  \left( {{f}_{x}\left( {x, y}\right) ,{f}_{y}\left( {x, y}\right) , - 1}\right) .
\]

这里所说的光滑是指偏导数连续.

由 \( F\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0 \) 确定的光滑曲面在 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 处的法向量为

\[
\mathbf{n} =  \pm  \left( {{F}_{x}\left( {x, y, z}\right) ,{F}_{y}\left( {x, y, z}\right) ,{F}_{z}\left( {x, y, z}\right) }\right) ,
\]

其中 \( {F}_{x},{F}_{y},{F}_{z} \) 不同时为零.

由参数方程

\[
x = x\left( {u, v}\right) ,\;y = y\left( {u, v}\right) ,\;z = z\left( {u, v}\right)
\]

决定的光滑曲面在点 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 的法向量为

\[
\mathbf{n} =  \pm  \left( {\frac{\partial \left( {y, z}\right) }{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) },\frac{\partial \left( {z, x}\right) }{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) },\frac{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) }{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) }}\right) .
\]

有了法向量之后，过曲面上任一点的法线方程和切平面方程都很容易写出来.

例题 21.1.2 求曲面

\[
x = u + v,\;y = {u}^{2} + {v}^{2},\;z = {u}^{3} + {v}^{3}
\]

的切平面当切点 \( \mathbf{M}\left( {u, v}\right) , u \neq  v \) ,趋于曲面的边界 \( u = v \) 上的点 \( {\mathbf{M}}_{0}\left( {{u}_{0},{v}_{0}}\right) \) 时的极限位置.

解 先求 \( u \neq  v \) 时的切平面方程. 由于

\[
\frac{\partial \left( {y, z}\right) }{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) } = \left| \begin{matrix} {2u} & {2v} \\  3{u}^{2} & 3{v}^{2} \end{matrix}\right|  = {6uv}\left( {v - u}\right) ,\;\frac{\partial \left( {z, x}\right) }{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) } = \left| \begin{matrix} 3{u}^{2} & 3{v}^{2} \\  1 & 1 \end{matrix}\right|  = 3\left( {{u}^{2} - {v}^{2}}\right) ,
\]

\[
\frac{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) }{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) } = \left| \begin{matrix} 1 & 1 \\  {2u} & {2v} \end{matrix}\right|  = 2\left( {v - u}\right) ,
\]

于是过 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 点的切平面方程为

\[
{6uv}\left( {v - u}\right) \left( {X - \left( {u + v}\right) }\right)  + 3\left( {{u}^{2} - {v}^{2}}\right) \left( {Y - \left( {{u}^{2} + {v}^{2}}\right) }\right)
\]

\[
+ 2\left( {v - u}\right) \left( {Z - \left( {{u}^{3} + {v}^{3}}\right) }\right)  = 0.
\]

消去 \( v - u \) ,并整理得到

\[
{6uvX} - 3\left( {u + v}\right) Y + {2Z} = {3uv}\left( {u + v}\right)  - {u}^{3} - {v}^{3}.
\]

令 \( \left( {u, v}\right)  \rightarrow  \left( {{u}_{0},{u}_{0}}\right) \) ,则有

\[
6{u}_{0}^{2}X - 6{u}_{0}Y + {2Z} = 4{u}_{0}^{3}.
\]

将 \( \left( {X, Y, Z}\right) \) 改写为 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) ,可见所求的极限位置为

\[
3{u}_{0}^{2}x - 3{u}_{0}y + z = 2{u}_{0}^{3}.
\]
