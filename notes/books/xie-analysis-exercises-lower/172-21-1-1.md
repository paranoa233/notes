#### 21.1.1 曲线的切向量、切线与法平面

设空间曲线 \( l \) 在点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right) \) 处的切向量为

\[
\tau  = \left( {{\tau }_{x},{\tau }_{y},{\tau }_{z}}\right) ,
\]

则曲线 \( l \) 在点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right) \) 的切线方程与法平面方程分别是

\[
\frac{x - {x}_{0}}{{\tau }_{x}} = \frac{y - {y}_{0}}{{\tau }_{y}} = \frac{z - {z}_{0}}{{\tau }_{z}}
\]

\[
{\tau }_{x}\left( {x - {x}_{0}}\right)  + {\tau }_{y}\left( {y - {y}_{0}}\right)  + {\tau }_{z}\left( {z - {z}_{0}}\right)  = 0.
\]

如果空间曲线 \( l \) 的参数方程是

\[
x = x\left( t\right) ,\;y = y\left( t\right) ,\;z = z\left( t\right) ,\;a \leq  t \leq  b,
\]

其中 \( t \) 是参数. 又设 \( {x}^{\prime }\left( t\right) ,{y}^{\prime }\left( t\right) ,{z}^{\prime }\left( t\right) \) 都在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 上连续,并且不同时为 0,这样的曲线称为光滑曲线. 这时曲线上点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right)  = \left( {x\left( {t}_{0}\right) , y\left( {t}_{0}\right) , z\left( {t}_{0}\right) }\right) \) 的切向量为

\[
\mathbf{\tau } = \left( {{x}^{\prime }\left( {t}_{0}\right) ,{y}^{\prime }\left( {t}_{0}\right) ,{z}^{\prime }\left( {t}_{0}\right) }\right) .
\]

如果空间曲线 \( l \) 是用两个曲面

\[
F\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0,\;G\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0
\]

的交线来表示的,又设 \( F \) 和 \( G \) 关于 \( x, y, z \) 有连续的偏导数. 点 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right) \) 满足这一方程组,即 \( F\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right)  = 0, G\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right)  = 0 \) ,并且 \( F, G \) 的 Jacobi 矩阵

\[
\left( \begin{array}{lll} \frac{\partial F}{\partial x} & \frac{\partial F}{\partial y} & \frac{\partial F}{\partial z} \\  \frac{\partial G}{\partial x} & \frac{\partial G}{\partial y} & \frac{\partial G}{\partial z} \end{array}\right)
\]

在点 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) 的秩为 2,则曲线 \( l \) 在点 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) 的切向量为

\[
\mathbf{\tau } = {\left. \left( \frac{\partial \left( {F, G}\right) }{\partial \left( {y, z}\right) },\frac{\partial \left( {F, G}\right) }{\partial \left( {z, x}\right) },\frac{\partial \left( {F, G}\right) }{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) }\right) \right| }_{{\mathbf{p}}_{0}}.
\]

例题 21.1.1 设 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 为可微函数,曲线方程为

\[
z = f\left( {x, y}\right) ,\;\frac{x - {x}_{0}}{\cos \alpha } = \frac{y - {y}_{0}}{\sin \alpha }.
\]

求曲线上点 \( {\mathbf{M}}_{0}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right) \) 的切线与 \( {xOy} \) 平面所成角的正切.

解 令 \( F\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = z - f\left( {x, y}\right) , G\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = \sin \alpha \left( {x - {x}_{0}}\right)  - \cos \alpha \left( {y - {y}_{0}}\right) \) ,则

\[
\frac{\partial \left( {F, G}\right) }{\partial \left( {y, z}\right) } = \left| \begin{matrix}  - {f}_{y} & 1 \\   - \cos \alpha & 0 \end{matrix}\right|  = \cos \alpha ,\;\frac{\partial \left( {F, G}\right) }{\partial \left( {z, x}\right) } = \left| \begin{matrix} 1 &  - {f}_{x} \\  0 & \sin \alpha  \end{matrix}\right|  = \sin \alpha ,
\]

\[
\frac{\partial \left( {F, G}\right) }{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) } = \left| \begin{matrix}  - {f}_{x} &  - {f}_{y} \\  \sin \alpha &  - \cos \alpha  \end{matrix}\right|  = {f}_{x}\cos \alpha  + {f}_{y}\sin \alpha .
\]

于是曲线上点 \( {\mathbf{M}}_{0}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right) \) 的切线与 \( z \) 轴正方向所成夹角 \( \varphi \) 的余弦为

\[
\cos \varphi  = \frac{{f}_{x}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \cos \alpha  + {f}_{y}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \sin \alpha }{\sqrt{1 + {\left\lbrack  {f}_{x}\left( {x}_{0},{y}_{0}\right) \cos \alpha  + {f}_{y}\left( {x}_{0},{y}_{0}\right) \sin \alpha \right\rbrack  }^{2}}}.
\]

由此可计算出切线与 \( {xOy} \) 平面所成角的正切为

\[
{f}_{x}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \cos \alpha  + {f}_{y}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \sin \alpha .
\]
