#### 19.3.4 练习题

1. 设 \( u = {\mathrm{e}}^{x} + \sin y + t, x = {st}, y = s + t \) ,求 \( \frac{\partial u}{\partial t} \) .

2. 设 \( u = f\left( {s, t}\right) , s = \frac{x}{y}, t = \frac{y}{z} \) ,求 \( \frac{\partial u}{\partial x},\frac{\partial u}{\partial y},\frac{\partial u}{\partial z} \) .

3. 设 \( u = f\left( {s, t, y}\right) , s = \varphi \left( {x, y}\right) , t = \psi \left( {x, y}\right) \) ,求 \( \frac{\partial u}{\partial x},\frac{\partial u}{\partial y} \) .

4. 设 \( u = {\mathrm{e}}^{x}\sin y, x = {2st}, y = t + {s}^{2} \) ,求 \( \frac{\partial u}{\partial s},\frac{\partial u}{\partial t} \) .

5. 设 \( u = f\left( {a{x}^{2} + b{y}^{2} + c{z}^{2}}\right) \) ,求 \( \mathrm{d}u \) .

6. 设 \( f\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = {\left( \frac{x}{y}\right) }^{1/z} \) ,求 \( \mathrm{d}f\left( {1,1,1}\right) \) .

7. 设 \( w = F\left( {{xy},{yz}}\right) , F \) 为有连续偏导数的二元函数,证明:

\[
x\frac{\partial w}{\partial x} + z\frac{\partial w}{\partial z} = y\frac{\partial w}{\partial y}.
\]

8. 设 \( z = f\left( {xy}\right) , f \) 为可微的一元函数,证明:

\[
x\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} - y\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = 0
\]

9. 设二元函数 \( u = F\left( {x, y}\right) \) 满足方程

\[
x\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} + y\frac{\partial u}{\partial y} = 0,
\]

证明: \( F\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在极坐标系下只是 \( \theta \) 的函数.

10. 设二元函数 \( F \) 在直角坐标系中可写成 \( F\left( {x, y}\right)  = f\left( x\right) g\left( y\right) \) ,在极坐标中可写成 \( F\left( {r\cos \theta , r\sin \theta }\right)  = h\left( \theta \right) \) ,求 \( F\left( {x, y}\right) \) .

11. 证明: \( u = \varphi \left( {x + {at}}\right)  + \psi \left( {x - {at}}\right) \) 满足方程

\[
\frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {t}^{2}} - {a}^{2}\frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {x}^{2}} = 0,
\]

其中 \( \varphi ,\psi \) 为具有二阶连续导数的一元函数.

12. 设 \( x = x\left( {u, v}\right) , y = y\left( {u, v}\right) \) 满足

\[
\frac{\partial x}{\partial u} = \frac{\partial y}{\partial v},\;\frac{\partial x}{\partial v} =  - \frac{\partial y}{\partial u},
\]

又设 \( w = w\left( {x, y}\right) \) 满足方程

\[
\frac{{\partial }^{2}w}{\partial {x}^{2}} + \frac{{\partial }^{2}w}{\partial {y}^{2}} = 0
\]

证明:

(1) \( w = w\left( {x\left( {u, v}\right) , y\left( {u, v}\right) }\right) \) 满足方程 \( \frac{{\partial }^{2}w}{\partial {u}^{2}} + \frac{{\partial }^{2}w}{\partial {v}^{2}} = 0 \) ;

(2) \( \frac{{\partial }^{2}\left( {xy}\right) }{\partial {u}^{2}} + \frac{{\partial }^{2}\left( {xy}\right) }{\partial {v}^{2}} = 0 \)

13. 证明: 可微函数 \( z = f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 仅是 \( {ax} + {by}\left( {{ab} \neq  0}\right) \) 的函数的充分必要条件是

\[
b\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = a\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}.
\]

14. 设 \( u\left( {x, y}\right) \) 有连续二阶偏导数, \( F\left( {s, t}\right) \) 有连续一阶偏导数,且满足

\[
F\left( {{u}_{x},{u}_{y}}\right)  = 0,\;{F}_{s}^{2} + {F}_{t}^{2} \neq  0,
\]

证明: \( {u}_{xx}{u}_{yy} - {u}_{xy}^{2} = 0 \) .

15. 设 \( u = f\left( \sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}}\right) \) 有连续二阶偏导数,且满足 \( \frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {x}^{2}} + \frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {y}^{2}} = 0 \) . 试求 \( u\left( {x, y}\right) \) .
