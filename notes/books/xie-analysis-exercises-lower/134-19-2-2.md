#### 19.2.2 多元函数的连续性、偏导数存在性及可微性之间的关系

多元函数在一个点处的连续性、偏导数存在性及可微性之间有下列关系:

![bo_d7fsu8491nqc7381io7g_183_445_1026_765_365_0.jpg](images/xie_analysis_exercises_lower_009_bod7fsu8491nqc7381io7g18344510267653650.jpg)

可微

由此可得到证明一个函数 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 不可微的常用方法如下:

- (1) \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 点至少有一个偏导数不存在;

(2) \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 点不连续；

(3) 从定义出发证明 \( {\Delta f} - {f}_{x}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) {\Delta x} - {f}_{y}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) {\Delta y} \neq  o\left( r\right) \) .

例题 19.2.2 设 \( f\left( {x, y}\right)  = \sqrt{\left| xy\right| } \) ,证明:

(1) \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 点连续;

(2) \( \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\left( {0,0}\right) ,\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\left( {0,0}\right) \) 都存在;

(3) \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 点不可微.

证 (1) 由于 \( \left| {\Delta f}\right|  = {\left| \Delta x\right| }^{1/2}{\left| \Delta y\right| }^{1/2} \) ,于是

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\left( {{\Delta x},{\Delta y}}\right)  \rightarrow  \left( {0,0}\right) }}f\left( {x, y}\right)  = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\left( {{\Delta x},{\Delta y}}\right)  \rightarrow  \left( {0,0}\right) }}\left\lbrack  {f\left( {0,0}\right)  + {\Delta f}}\right\rbrack   = 0 = f\left( {0,0}\right) .
\]

(2)直接按定义计算得

\[
\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\left( {0,0}\right)  = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{{\Delta x} \rightarrow  0}}\frac{f\left( {{\Delta x},0}\right)  - f\left( {0,0}\right) }{\Delta x} = 0,
\]

\[
\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\left( {0,0}\right)  = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{{\Delta y} \rightarrow  0}}\frac{f\left( {0,{\Delta y}}\right)  - f\left( {0,0}\right) }{\Delta y} = 0.
\]

(3) 由于

\[
{\Delta f} - {f}_{x}\left( {0,0}\right) {\Delta x} - {f}_{y}\left( {0,0}\right) {\Delta y} = {\left| \Delta x\right| }^{\frac{1}{2}}{\left| \Delta y\right| }^{\frac{1}{2}},
\]

取 \( {\Delta x} = {\Delta y} \rightarrow  0 \) ,则

\[
\frac{{\Delta f} - {f}_{x}\left( {0,0}\right) {\Delta x} - {f}_{y}\left( {0,0}\right) {\Delta y}}{r} = \frac{{\left| \Delta x\right| }^{\frac{1}{2}}{\left| \Delta y\right| }^{\frac{1}{2}}}{\sqrt{{\left( \Delta x\right) }^{2} + {\left( \Delta y\right) }^{2}}} \rightarrow  \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \neq  0,
\]

所以 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 点不可微.

注 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 处可微时,成立无穷小增量公式:

\( f\left( {{x}_{0} + {\Delta x},{y}_{0} + {\Delta y}}\right)  = f\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right)  + {f}_{x}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) {\Delta x} + {f}_{y}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) {\Delta y} + o\left( {\left| {\Delta x}\right|  + \left| {\Delta y}\right| }\right) . \)

上述例子说明仅有 \( {f}_{x}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) ,{f}_{y}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 存在还不足以保证二维无穷小增量公式成立. 这与一维无穷小增量公式 (上册 159 页 (6.1)) 成立的条件是不一样的, 由此可以体会一元导数与多元偏导数的区别.

例题 19.2.3 设 \( f\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left| {x - y}\right| \varphi \left( {x, y}\right) \) ,其中 \( \varphi \left( {x, y}\right) \) 在点 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 的一个邻域上有定义,要求给函数 \( \varphi \left( {x, y}\right) \) 加上适当的条件,使得

(1) \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在点 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 连续;

(2) \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在点 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 存在偏导数；

(3) \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在点 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 可微.

解 (1) 由于 \( f\left( {0,0}\right)  = 0 \) ,而在点 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 附近

\[
\left| {f\left( {x, y}\right) }\right|  \leq  2\sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}}\left| {\varphi \left( {x, y}\right) }\right| ,
\]

于是当

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\left( {x, y}\right)  \rightarrow  \left( {0,0}\right) }}\sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}}\varphi \left( {x, y}\right)  = 0
\]

时, \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 点连续.

特别地,当 \( \varphi \left( {x, y}\right) \) 在点 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 附近有界时, \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在点 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 连续.

(2)由于单侧导数

\[
{\left( \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\right) }_{ \pm  }\left( {0,0}\right)  = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  {0}^{ \pm  }}}\frac{f\left( {x,0}\right)  - f\left( {0,0}\right) }{x}
\]

\[
= \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  {0}^{ \pm  }}}\frac{\pm {x\varphi }\left( {x,0}\right) }{x} =  \pm  \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  {0}^{ \pm  }}}\varphi \left( {x,0}\right) ,
\]

从而当 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}\varphi \left( {x,0}\right)  =  - \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  {0}^{ - }}}\varphi \left( {x,0}\right) \) 时, \( \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\left( {0,0}\right)  = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}\varphi \left( {x,0}\right) \) .

同理当 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{y \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}\varphi \left( {0, y}\right)  =  - \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{y \rightarrow  {0}^{ - }}}\varphi \left( {0, y}\right) \) 时, \( \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\left( {0,0}\right)  = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{y \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}\varphi \left( {0, y}\right) \) . 特别地,当 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\left( {x, y}\right)  \rightarrow  \left( {0,0}\right) }}\varphi \left( {x, y}\right)  = 0 \) 时, \( \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\left( {0,0}\right)  = \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\left( {0,0}\right)  = 0. \)

(3) 由于

\[
f\left( {x, y}\right)  - \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\left( {0,0}\right) x - \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\left( {0,0}\right) y
\]

\[
= \left| {x - y}\right| \varphi \left( {x, y}\right)  - \left\lbrack  {\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}\varphi \left( {x,0}\right) }\right\rbrack  x - \left\lbrack  {\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{y \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}\varphi \left( {0, y}\right) }\right\rbrack  y
\]

\[
= \left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \left\lbrack  {\varphi \left( {x, y}\right)  - \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}\varphi \left( {x,0}\right) }\right\rbrack  x - \left\lbrack  {\varphi \left( {x, y}\right)  + \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{y \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}\varphi \left( {0, y}\right) }\right\rbrack  y, & \text{ 若 }x \geq  y, \\   - \left\lbrack  {\varphi \left( {x, y}\right)  + \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}\varphi \left( {x,0}\right) }\right\rbrack  x + \left\lbrack  {\varphi \left( {x, y}\right)  - \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{y \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}\varphi \left( {0, y}\right) }\right\rbrack  y, & \text{ 若 }x < y, \end{array}\right.
\]

由此可以推出, 当

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\left( {x, y}\right)  \rightarrow  \left( {0,0}\right) }}\varphi \left( {x, y}\right)  = 0
\]

时, \( \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\left( {0,0}\right)  = \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\left( {0,0}\right)  = 0 \) 且 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 点可微.
