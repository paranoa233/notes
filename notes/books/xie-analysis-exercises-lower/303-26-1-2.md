#### 26.1.2 旋度

设 \( \mathbf{a} = P\mathbf{i} + Q\mathbf{j} + R\mathbf{k} \) 是定义在区域 \( D \) 上的向量场,又设 \( P, Q, R \) 在 \( D \) 上有连续偏导数. 记

\[
\operatorname{curl}\mathbf{a} = \left| \begin{matrix} \mathbf{i} & \mathbf{j} & \mathbf{k} \\  \frac{\partial }{\partial x} & \frac{\partial }{\partial y} & \frac{\partial }{\partial z} \\  P & Q & R \end{matrix}\right|
\]

\[
= \left( {\frac{\partial R}{\partial y} - \frac{\partial Q}{\partial z}}\right) \mathbf{i} + \left( {\frac{\partial P}{\partial z} - \frac{\partial R}{\partial x}}\right) \mathbf{j} + \left( {\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial P}{\partial y}}\right) \mathbf{k},
\]

称之为向量场 \( \mathbf{a} \) 的旋度. 它是一个向量,有时也记为 \( \nabla  \times  \mathbf{a} \) . 利用旋度的记号,可将 Stokes 公式 (25.13) 写为

\[
{\iint }_{D}\operatorname{curl}\mathbf{a} \cdot  \mathbf{n}\mathrm{d}S = {\oint }_{\partial D}\mathbf{a} \cdot  \mathbf{\tau }\mathrm{d}s
\]

其中 \( D \) 是空间曲面, \( \partial D \) 是它的边界, \( \mathbf{a} = \left( {P, Q, R}\right) \) ,等式左端的 \( \mathbf{n} \) 为 \( D \) 的单位外法向量,等式右端的 \( \tau \) 是 \( \partial D \) 的切向量. 它们两者的方向服从右手系法则.

在下面需要星形区域的概念.

定义 设 \( \mathbf{M} \) 是区域 \( G \) 内一点,称 \( G \) 是关于 \( \mathbf{M} \) 的星形区域,如果对任意一点 \( \mathbf{P} \in  G \) ,都有 \( \mathbf{P} \) 与 \( \mathbf{M} \) 之间的直线段 \( \mathbf{P}\mathbf{M} \subset  G \) .

例题 26.1.2 设 \( G \) 是 \( {\mathbf{R}}^{3} \) 中关于其内一点 \( \mathbf{M} \) 的星形区域. \( \mathbf{F} = \left( {P, Q, R}\right) \) 是 \( G \) 上的光滑向量场,且 \( \operatorname{div}\mathbf{F} = 0 \) . 证明: 存在 \( G \) 上的光滑向量场 \( \mathbf{A} \) ,使得

\[
\mathbf{F} = \operatorname{curl}\mathbf{A}.
\]

证 不妨设 \( \mathbf{M} \) 为原点. 由于 \( G \) 关于原点为星形区域,于是对任何 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right)  \in \; G \) ,有

\[
P\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{\partial }{\partial t}\left\lbrack  {{t}^{2}P\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) }\right\rbrack  \mathrm{d}t
\]

\[
= 2{\int }_{0}^{1}{tP}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) \mathrm{d}t + {\int }_{0}^{1}{t}^{2}\frac{\partial }{\partial t}P\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) \mathrm{d}t. \tag{26.6}
\]

由已知条件有 \( \frac{\partial P}{\partial x} =  - \frac{\partial Q}{\partial y} - \frac{\partial R}{\partial z} \) ,将它代入 (26.6) 中,则

\[
P\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 2{\int }_{0}^{1}{tP}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) \mathrm{d}t
\]

\[
- {\int }_{0}^{1}{t}^{2}x\left\lbrack  {\frac{\partial Q}{\partial y}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right)  + \frac{\partial R}{\partial z}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) }\right\rbrack  \mathrm{d}t
\]

\[
+ {\int }_{0}^{1}{t}^{2}y\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) \mathrm{d}t + {\int }_{0}^{1}{t}^{2}z\frac{\partial P}{\partial z}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) \mathrm{d}t
\]

\[
= \frac{\partial }{\partial y}\left\lbrack  {y{\int }_{0}^{1}{tP}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) \mathrm{d}t - x{\int }_{0}^{1}{tQ}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) \mathrm{d}t}\right\rbrack
\]

\[
- \frac{\partial }{\partial z}\left\lbrack  {x{\int }_{0}^{1}{tR}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) \mathrm{d}t - z{\int }_{0}^{1}{tP}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) \mathrm{d}t}\right\rbrack  .
\]

记

\[
{A}_{1}\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = z{\int }_{0}^{1}{tQ}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) \mathrm{d}t - y{\int }_{0}^{1}{tR}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) \mathrm{d}t
\]

\[
{A}_{2}\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = x{\int }_{0}^{1}{tR}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) \mathrm{d}t - z{\int }_{0}^{1}{tP}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) \mathrm{d}t
\]

\[
{A}_{3}\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = y{\int }_{0}^{1}{tP}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) \mathrm{d}t - x{\int }_{0}^{1}{tQ}\left( {{tx},{ty},{tz}}\right) \mathrm{d}t
\]

则

\[
P\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = \frac{\partial {A}_{3}}{\partial y} - \frac{\partial {A}_{2}}{\partial z}.
\]

同理可证

\[
Q\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = \frac{\partial {A}_{1}}{\partial z} - \frac{\partial {A}_{3}}{\partial x}, R\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = \frac{\partial {A}_{2}}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial {A}_{1}}{\partial y}.
\]

令 \( \mathbf{A} = \left( {{A}_{1},{A}_{2},{A}_{3}}\right) \) ,则

\[
\mathbf{F} = \operatorname{curl}\mathbf{A}.
\]
