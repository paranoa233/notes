#### 21.4.1 条件极值

在满足约束条件 \( {\varphi }_{i}\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  = 0\left( {i = 1,2,\cdots , m, m < n}\right) \) 时,求函数 \( f\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) \) 的极值问题,可归结为对 Lagrange 函数

\[
L\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  = f\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  + \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{m}{\lambda }_{i}{\varphi }_{i}\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) \tag{21.9}
\]

求普通函数极值的问题,其中 \( {\lambda }_{i}\left( {i = 1,2,\cdots , m}\right) \) 为常数因子. 这种方法称为 Lagrange 乘子法. Lagrange 乘子法的证明见 \( \left\lbrack  {55}\right\rbrack \) 第三册的 6.7.5 小节 (其中证明了 4 个命题),其几何解释可见教科书 \( \left\lbrack  {9,{24},{63}}\right\rbrack \) 等.

Lagrange 乘子法的具体步骤是:

1. 作出 Lagrange 函数 (21.9);

2. 由 \( {L}_{{x}_{i}} = 0\left( {i = 1,2,\cdots , n}\right) \) 与 \( {\varphi }_{i} = 0\left( {i = 1,2,\cdots , m}\right) \) 联立解出 \( L \) 的全部驻点与 \( {\lambda }_{i}\left( {i = 1,2,\cdots , m}\right) \) 的具体值,并要求驻点处矩阵

\[
\left( \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{1}}{\partial {x}_{1}} & \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{1}}{\partial {x}_{2}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{1}}{\partial {x}_{n}} \\  \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{2}}{\partial {x}_{1}} & \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{2}}{\partial {x}_{2}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{2}}{\partial {x}_{n}} \\  \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\  \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{m}}{\partial {x}_{1}} & \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{m}}{\partial {x}_{2}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{m}}{\partial {x}_{n}} \end{matrix}\right) \tag{21.10}
\]

的秩为 \( m \) ;

3. 对每个驻点 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) ,算出 Hesse 矩阵 \( \mathbf{H}\left( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\right)  = {\left( \frac{{\partial }^{2}L}{\partial {x}_{i}\partial {x}_{j}}\right) }_{n \times  n}\left( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\right) \) .

(1) 若 \( \mathbf{H}\left( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\right) \) 正定,则 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) 为 (条件) 极小值点;

(2)若 \( \mathbf{H}\left( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\right) \) 负定，则 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) 为(条件)极大值点；

(3) 若 \( \mathbf{H}\left( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\right) \) 既不是正定,也不是负定,则由

\[
\mathrm{d}{\varphi }_{i}\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  = \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{i}}{\partial {x}_{i}}\mathrm{\;d}{x}_{i} = 0,\;i = 1,2,\cdots , m
\]

以及矩阵 (21.10) 的秩为 \( m \) ,解出 \( \mathrm{d}{x}_{1},\mathrm{\;d}{x}_{2},\cdots ,\mathrm{d}{x}_{n} \) 中的 \( m \) 个. 不妨设可解出 \( \mathrm{d}{x}_{1},\mathrm{\;d}{x}_{2},\cdots ,\mathrm{d}{x}_{m} \) ,将其代入 \( n \) 元二次型 \( \left( {\mathrm{d}{x}_{1}\frac{\partial }{\partial {x}_{1}} + \mathrm{d}{x}_{2}\frac{\partial }{\partial {x}_{2}} + \cdots  + }\right. \; {\left. \mathrm{d}{x}_{n}\frac{\partial }{\partial {x}_{n}}\right) }^{2}L\left( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\right) \) 中,化为二次型 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i, j = 1}}^{{n - m}}{a}_{ij}\mathrm{\;d}{x}_{i}\mathrm{\;d}{x}_{j} \) . 令 \( \mathbf{A} = {\left( {a}_{ij}\right) }_{\left( {n - m}\right)  \times  \left( {n - m}\right) } \) . 若 \( \mathbf{A} \) 是正定的,则 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) 为 (条件) 极小值点. 若 \( \mathbf{A} \) 是负定的,则 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) 为 (条件) 极大值点. 若 \( \mathbf{A} \) 是不定的,则 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) 不是 (条件) 极值点. 若 \( \mathbf{A} \) 是半定的,则需进一步判定.

如此看来,若出现 \( \mathbf{H}\left( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\right) \) 既不正定,也不负定的情况下,讨论将相当复杂.

例题 21.4.1 求 \( f\left( {x, y}\right)  = a{x}^{2} + {2bxy} + c{y}^{2}\left( {b \neq  0}\right) \) 在条件 \( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} = 1 \) 之下的极值.

解 1 作 Lagrange 函数

\[
L\left( {x, y,\lambda }\right)  = a{x}^{2} + {2bxy} + c{y}^{2} - \lambda \left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} - 1}\right) .
\]

解方程组

\[
{L}_{x} = {2ax} + {2by} - {2\lambda x} = 0, \tag{21.11}
\]

\[
{L}_{y} = {2bx} + {2cy} - {2\lambda y} = 0, \tag{21.12}
\]

\[
{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} = 1 \tag{21.13}
\]

由 (21.13) 知 \( x, y \) 不可能同时为零,于是由 (21.11),(21.12) 得

\[
\left| \begin{matrix} a - \lambda & b \\  b & c - \lambda  \end{matrix}\right|  = {\lambda }^{2} - \left( {a + c}\right) \lambda  + {ac} - {b}^{2} = 0. \tag{21.14}
\]

因判别式 \( \Delta  = {\left( a + c\right) }^{2} - 4\left( {{ac} - {b}^{2}}\right)  = {\left( a - c\right) }^{2} + 4{b}^{2} > 0 \) ,从而解得两个相异实根

\[
{\lambda }_{ \pm  } = \frac{1}{2}\left\lbrack  {a + c \pm  \sqrt{{\left( a - c\right) }^{2} + 4{b}^{2}}}\right\rbrack  .
\]

对每个 \( \lambda \) ,由 (21.11) (或(21.12)) 与 (21.13) 解出两组解

\[
\left( {{\lambda }_{ + },{x}_{1},{y}_{1}}\right) ,\left( {{\lambda }_{ + },{x}_{2},{y}_{2}}\right) ,\left( {{\lambda }_{ - },{x}_{3},{y}_{3}}\right) ,\left( {{\lambda }_{ - },{x}_{4},{y}_{4}}\right) \text{ , }
\]

且 \( {y}_{1}{y}_{2}{y}_{3}{y}_{4} \neq  0 \) . 由 (21.11) 与 (21.12) 知

\[
\left| \begin{array}{ll} {L}_{xx} & {L}_{xy} \\  {L}_{xy} & {L}_{yy} \end{array}\right|  = \left| \begin{matrix} 2\left( {a - \lambda }\right) & {2b} \\  {2b} & 2\left( {c - \lambda }\right)  \end{matrix}\right|  = 0.
\]

从而在驻点,矩阵 \( \left( \begin{array}{ll} {L}_{xx} & {L}_{xy} \\  {L}_{xy} & {L}_{yy} \end{array}\right) \) 既不是正定的,也不是负定的. 此时,对 (21.13) 求微分得

\[
{2x}\mathrm{\;d}x + {2y}\mathrm{\;d}y = 0,
\]

解出

\[
\mathrm{d}y =  - \frac{x}{y}\mathrm{\;d}x.
\]

然后代入 \( {\mathrm{d}}^{2}L = 2\left( {a - \lambda }\right) \mathrm{d}{x}^{2} + {4b}\mathrm{\;d}x\mathrm{\;d}y + 2\left( {c - \lambda }\right) \mathrm{d}{y}^{2} \) 中,得

\[
{\mathrm{d}}^{2}L = \frac{2}{{y}^{2}}\left\lbrack  {\left( {a - \lambda }\right) {y}^{2} - {2bxy} + \left( {c - \lambda }\right) {x}^{2}}\right\rbrack  \mathrm{d}{x}^{2}
\]

\[
= \frac{2}{{y}^{2}}\left( {a + c - {2\lambda }}\right) \mathrm{d}{x}^{2}\left\{  \begin{array}{ll}  < 0, & \lambda  = {\lambda }_{ + }, \\   > 0, & \lambda  = {\lambda }_{ - }. \end{array}\right.
\]

因此,对应于 \( {\lambda }_{ + }\left( {\lambda }_{ - }\right) , f \) 取得极大 (小) 值.

解 2 用隐函数方法.

容易证明 \( b \neq  0 \) 时, \( f \) 的极值不在 \( \left( {\pm 1,0}\right) \) 取得,而除去这两点外, \( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} - 1 = 0 \) 在单位圆上任意点的某邻域内有连续可导的隐函数 \( y = h\left( x\right) \) ,且 \( {y}^{\prime } =  - \frac{x}{y} \) .

记 \( F\left( x\right)  = f\left( {x, h\left( x\right) }\right) \) ,则

\[
{F}^{\prime }\left( x\right)  = {2ax} + {2b}\left( {y + x{y}^{\prime }}\right)  + {2cy}{y}^{\prime }
\]

\[
= 2\left( {{ax} + {by}}\right)  + 2\left( {{bx} + {cy}}\right) \left( {-\frac{x}{y}}\right)  = \frac{2}{y}\left\lbrack  {\left( {a - c}\right) {xy} + b\left( {{y}^{2} - {x}^{2}}\right) }\right\rbrack  .
\]

令 \( {F}^{\prime }\left( x\right)  = 0 \) ,记 \( u = \frac{x}{y} \) ,由上式得

\[
b{u}^{2} - \left( {a - c}\right) u - b = 0.
\]

它的判别式 \( \Delta  = {\left( a - c\right) }^{2} + 4{b}^{2} > 0 \) ,故方程有相异实根

\[
{u}_{ \pm  } = \frac{1}{2b}\left\lbrack  {a - c \pm  \sqrt{{\left( a - c\right) }^{2} + 4{b}^{2}}}\right\rbrack  ,
\]

且异号. 将它们代入 \( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} = 1 \) 中可解得对应于 \( {u}_{ + } \) 的解 \( {x}_{1},{x}_{2} \) 和对应于 \( {u}_{ - } \) 的解 \( {x}_{3},{x}_{4} \) . 在这些驻点上

\[
{F}^{\prime \prime }\left( x\right)  = \frac{2}{y}\left\{  {\left( {a - c}\right) y - {2bx} + \left\lbrack  {\left( {a - c}\right) x + {2by}}\right\rbrack  \left( {-\frac{x}{y}}\right) }\right\}
\]

\[
= \frac{2}{{y}^{2}}\left\lbrack  {\left( {a - c}\right) \left( {{y}^{2} - {x}^{2}}\right)  - {4bxy}}\right\rbrack
\]

\[
= \frac{2xy}{{y}^{2}}\left\lbrack  {-\frac{{\left( a - c\right) }^{2}}{b} - {4b}}\right\rbrack
\]

\[
=  - \frac{2u}{b}\left\lbrack  {{\left( a - c\right) }^{2} + 4{b}^{2}}\right\rbrack  \left\{  \begin{array}{ll}  < 0, & u = {u}_{ + }, \\   > 0, & u = {u}_{ - }. \end{array}\right.
\]

故 \( {x}_{1},{x}_{2} \) 是 \( f \) 的极大值点, \( {x}_{3},{x}_{4} \) 是 \( f \) 的极小值点,其极大值和极小值分别为

\[
\left( {a{u}_{ + }^{2} + {2b}{u}_{ + } + c}\right) /\left( {{u}_{ + }^{2} + 1}\right) \text{ 和 }\left( {a{u}_{ - }^{2} + {2b}{u}_{ - } + c}\right) /\left( {{u}_{ - }^{2} + 1}\right) .
\]

解 3 注意到

\[
f\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left( {x, y}\right) \left( \begin{array}{ll} a & b \\  b & c \end{array}\right) \left( \begin{array}{l} x \\  y \end{array}\right)
\]

是一个实的二元二次型, 利用高等代数中的有关定理: 任意一个实二次型

\[
\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i, j = 1}}^{n}{a}_{ij}{x}_{i}{x}_{j}\;\left( {{a}_{ij} = {a}_{ji}, i, j = 1,2,\cdots , n}\right)
\]

都可以经过正交的线性变换变成平方和

\[
{\lambda }_{1}{y}_{1}^{2} + {\lambda }_{2}{y}_{2}^{2} + \cdots  + {\lambda }_{n}{y}_{n}^{2},
\]

其中平方项的系数 \( {\lambda }_{1},{\lambda }_{2},\cdots ,{\lambda }_{n} \) 就是矩阵 \( {\left( {a}_{ij}\right) }_{n \times  n} \) 的特征多项式全部的根. 因此 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 可化为

\[
f\left( {x, y}\right)  = {\lambda }_{1}{u}^{2} + {\lambda }_{2}{v}^{2}\text{ ，且 }{u}^{2} + {v}^{2} = 1\text{ ， }
\]

其中 \( u, v \) 是 \( x, y \) 的线性组合,然后再用 Lagrange 乘子法或初等方法求解.

注 有些极值问题会有初等解法, 但这些初等解法一般都有一定的局限性.
