#### 22.2.1 矩形区域上的二重积分

矩形上的二重积分在一定条件下可以化为二次积分进行计算.

设 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在矩形 \( A = \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack   \times  \left\lbrack  {c, d}\right\rbrack \) 上可积,且对每个固定的 \( x \in  \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) ,积分

\[
\varphi \left( x\right)  = {\int }_{c}^{d}f\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}y
\]

存在,则 \( \varphi \left( x\right) \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 上可积,并且

\[
{\iint }_{A}f\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{\;d}y = {\int }_{a}^{b}\mathrm{\;d}x{\int }_{c}^{d}f\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}y.
\]

由此可以看出,若 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( A \) 上连续,则两个二次积分是相等的 (都等于二重积分), 积分值与积分顺序无关. 但积分顺序不同时, 积分的难度可能相差很大. 请看下面的例子.

例题 22.2.1 设 \( A = \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack   \times  \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) ,求

\[
I = {\iint }_{A}\frac{y\mathrm{\;d}x\mathrm{\;d}y}{{\left( 1 + {x}^{2} + {y}^{2}\right) }^{3/2}}.
\]

解 先对 \( y \) 后对 \( x \) 积分,得到

\[
I = {\int }_{0}^{1}\mathrm{\;d}x{\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{y\mathrm{\;d}y}{{\left( 1 + {x}^{2} + {y}^{2}\right) }^{3/2}}
\]

\[
= {\int }_{0}^{1}\left( {\frac{1}{\sqrt{{x}^{2} + 1}} - \frac{1}{\sqrt{{x}^{2} + 2}}}\right) \mathrm{d}x = \ln \frac{2 + \sqrt{2}}{1 + \sqrt{3}}.
\]

先对 \( x \) 后对 \( y \) 积分,则得到

\[
I = {\int }_{0}^{1}y\mathrm{\;d}y{\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{{\left( 1 + {x}^{2} + {y}^{2}\right) }^{3/2}}
\]

\[
= {\int }_{0}^{1}y\left\lbrack  {\frac{1}{1 + {y}^{2}} \cdot  {\left. \frac{x}{{\left( 1 + {x}^{2} + {y}^{2}\right) }^{1/2}}\right| }_{x = 0}^{x = 1}}\right\rbrack  \mathrm{d}y
\]

\[
= {\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{y\mathrm{\;d}y}{\left( {1 + {y}^{2}}\right) {\left( 2 + {y}^{2}\right) }^{1/2}}\;\left( {\sqrt{2 + {y}^{2}} = t}\right)
\]

\[
= {\int }_{\sqrt{2}}^{\sqrt{3}}\frac{\mathrm{d}t}{{t}^{2} - 1} = {\left. \frac{1}{2}\ln \frac{t - 1}{t + 1}\right| }_{\sqrt{2}}^{\sqrt{3}} = \frac{1}{2}\ln \frac{\left( {\sqrt{3} - 1}\right) \left( {\sqrt{2} + 1}\right) }{\left( {\sqrt{3} + 1}\right) \left( {\sqrt{2} - 1}\right) }
\]

\[
= \frac{1}{2}\ln \frac{2{\left( \sqrt{2} + 1\right) }^{2}}{{\left( \sqrt{3} + 1\right) }^{2}} = \ln \frac{2 + \sqrt{2}}{1 + \sqrt{3}}.
\]

在 \( f \) 不满足可积或累次可积的条件时,情况就比较复杂,请看下面反例.

例题 22.2.2 设函数 \( f \) 定义在 \( A = \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack   \times  \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 上,

\[
f\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left\{  \begin{array}{ll} 1, & \text{ 当 }x\text{ 是无理数, } \\  {2y}, & \text{ 当 }x\text{ 是有理数, } \end{array}\right.
\]

则 (1) \( f \) 在 \( A \) 上不可积;

(2) \( {\int }_{0}^{1}\mathrm{\;d}x{\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}y \) 存在, \( {\int }_{0}^{1}\mathrm{\;d}y{\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}x \) 不存在.

证 (1) \( \forall y \in  \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack  , y \neq  \frac{1}{2}, f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 作为 \( x \) 的函数在 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 内处处不连续,所以 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( A \) 上的 \( y \neq  \frac{1}{2} \) 的每点处都不连续. 于是 \( f \) 在 \( A \) 上不可积.

(2)由于

\[
{\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}y = \left\{  \begin{array}{ll} {\int }_{0}^{1}\mathrm{\;d}y = 1, & \text{ 当 }x\text{ 为无理数, } \\  {\int }_{0}^{1}{2y}\mathrm{\;d}y = 1, & \text{ 当 }x\text{ 为有理数, } \end{array}\right.
\]

所以

\[
{\int }_{0}^{1}\mathrm{\;d}x{\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}y = {\int }_{0}^{1}\mathrm{\;d}x = 1.
\]

另一方面, \( \forall y \in  \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack  , y \neq  \frac{1}{2}, f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 作为 \( x \) 的一元函数,在 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 内每一点处都不连续,于是 \( {\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}x \) 对每个 \( y \neq  \frac{1}{2} \) 都不存在,从而

\[
{\int }_{0}^{1}\mathrm{\;d}y{\int }_{0}^{1}f\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}x
\]

不存在.

类似地, 也有二重积分存在, 但两个二次积分不存在以及两个二次积分存在且相等, 但二重积分不存在的例子.
