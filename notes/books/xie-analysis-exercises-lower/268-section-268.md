## 第一组参考题

1. 证明不等式

\[
\left| {{\int }_{C}P\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}x + Q\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}y}\right|  \leq  {ML},
\]

其中 \( L \) 是曲线 \( C \) 的弧长, \( M = \mathop{\max }\limits_{{\left( {x, y}\right)  \in  C}}\left\{  \sqrt{{P}^{2}\left( {x, y}\right)  + {Q}^{2}\left( {x, y}\right) }\right\} \) ,并利用这个不等式证明 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{R \rightarrow   + \infty }}{I}_{R} = 0 \) ,其中

\[
{I}_{R} = {\oint }_{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} = {R}^{2}}\frac{y\mathrm{\;d}x - x\mathrm{\;d}y}{{\left( {x}^{2} + xy + {y}^{2}\right) }^{2}}.
\]

2. 求第一型曲线积分

(1) \( {\int }_{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} = {R}^{2}}\ln \sqrt{{\left( x - a\right) }^{2} + {y}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}s\left( {\left| a\right|  \neq  R}\right) \) ; (2) \( {\int }_{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} = {R}^{2}}\ln \sqrt{{\left( x - a\right) }^{2} + {\left( y - b\right) }^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}s\left( {{a}^{2} + {b}^{2} \neq  {R}^{2}}\right) \) .

3. 设 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( L \) 上连续, \( L \) 是逐段光滑的简单闭曲线,证明:

\[
u\left( {x, y}\right)  = {\oint }_{L}f\left( {\xi ,\eta }\right) \ln \sqrt{{\left( x - \xi \right) }^{2} + {\left( y - \eta \right) }^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}s
\]

当 \( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} \rightarrow   + \infty \) 时趋于 0 的充分必要条件是

\[
{\oint }_{L}f\left( {\xi ,\eta }\right) \mathrm{d}s = 0.
\]

4. 设 \( u\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( {\mathbf{R}}^{2} \) 上连续,证明:

\[
u\left( {x, y}\right)  = \frac{1}{\pi {r}^{2}}{\iint }_{{\left( x - \xi \right) }^{2} + {\left( y - \eta \right) }^{2} \leq  {r}^{2}}u\left( {\xi ,\eta }\right) \mathrm{d}\xi \mathrm{d}\eta
\]

\( \forall r > 0 \) 都成立的充分必要条件是

\[
u\left( {x, y}\right)  = \frac{1}{2\pi r}{\oint }_{{\left( x - \xi \right) }^{2} + {\left( y - \eta \right) }^{2} = {r}^{2}}u\left( {\xi ,\eta }\right) \mathrm{d}s
\]

\( \forall r > 0 \) 都成立.

5. 设 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( G \) 上一阶连续可微,在 \( \partial G \) 上 \( f\left( {x, y}\right)  = 0, G = \left\{  {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} \leq  {a}^{2}}\right\} \) . 证明:

\[
\left| {{\iint }_{G}f\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{\;d}y}\right|  \leq  \frac{\pi }{3}{a}^{3}\mathop{\max }\limits_{G}\left\{  {\left( {f}_{x}^{2} + {f}_{y}^{2}\right) }^{\frac{1}{2}}\right\}  .
\]

6. 设 \( P\left( {x, y}\right) , Q\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在平面上有连续偏导数,而且对以 \( \forall \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right)  \in  {\mathbf{R}}^{2} \) 为心,以 \( \forall r > 0 \) 为半径的上半圆 \( C : x = {x}_{0} + r\cos \theta , y = {y}_{0} + r\sin \theta \left( {0 \leq  \theta  \leq  \pi }\right) \) 都有

\[
{\int }_{C}P\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}x + Q\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}y = 0.
\]

证明: \( P\left( {x, y}\right)  = \frac{\partial Q}{\partial x}\left( {x, y}\right)  = 0\left( {\forall \left( {x, y}\right)  \in  {\mathbf{R}}^{2}}\right) \) .
