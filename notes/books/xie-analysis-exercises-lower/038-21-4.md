## §21.4 条件极值与条件最值

#### 21.4.1 条件极值

在满足约束条件 \( {\varphi }_{i}\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  = 0\left( {i = 1,2,\cdots , m, m < n}\right) \) 时,求函数 \( f\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) \) 的极值问题,可归结为对 Lagrange 函数

\[
L\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  = f\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  + \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{m}{\lambda }_{i}{\varphi }_{i}\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right) \tag{21.9}
\]

求普通函数极值的问题,其中 \( {\lambda }_{i}\left( {i = 1,2,\cdots , m}\right) \) 为常数因子. 这种方法称为 Lagrange 乘子法. Lagrange 乘子法的证明见 \( \left\lbrack  {55}\right\rbrack \) 第三册的 6.7.5 小节 (其中证明了 4 个命题),其几何解释可见教科书 \( \left\lbrack  {9,{24},{63}}\right\rbrack \) 等.

Lagrange 乘子法的具体步骤是:

1. 作出 Lagrange 函数 (21.9);

2. 由 \( {L}_{{x}_{i}} = 0\left( {i = 1,2,\cdots , n}\right) \) 与 \( {\varphi }_{i} = 0\left( {i = 1,2,\cdots , m}\right) \) 联立解出 \( L \) 的全部驻点与 \( {\lambda }_{i}\left( {i = 1,2,\cdots , m}\right) \) 的具体值,并要求驻点处矩阵

\[
\left( \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{1}}{\partial {x}_{1}} & \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{1}}{\partial {x}_{2}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{1}}{\partial {x}_{n}} \\  \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{2}}{\partial {x}_{1}} & \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{2}}{\partial {x}_{2}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{2}}{\partial {x}_{n}} \\  \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\  \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{m}}{\partial {x}_{1}} & \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{m}}{\partial {x}_{2}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{m}}{\partial {x}_{n}} \end{matrix}\right) \tag{21.10}
\]

的秩为 \( m \) ;

3. 对每个驻点 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) ,算出 Hesse 矩阵 \( \mathbf{H}\left( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\right)  = {\left( \frac{{\partial }^{2}L}{\partial {x}_{i}\partial {x}_{j}}\right) }_{n \times  n}\left( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\right) \) .

(1) 若 \( \mathbf{H}\left( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\right) \) 正定,则 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) 为 (条件) 极小值点;

(2)若 \( \mathbf{H}\left( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\right) \) 负定，则 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) 为(条件)极大值点；

(3) 若 \( \mathbf{H}\left( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\right) \) 既不是正定,也不是负定,则由

\[
\mathrm{d}{\varphi }_{i}\left( {{x}_{1},{x}_{2},\cdots ,{x}_{n}}\right)  = \frac{\partial {\varphi }_{i}}{\partial {x}_{i}}\mathrm{\;d}{x}_{i} = 0,\;i = 1,2,\cdots , m
\]

以及矩阵 (21.10) 的秩为 \( m \) ,解出 \( \mathrm{d}{x}_{1},\mathrm{\;d}{x}_{2},\cdots ,\mathrm{d}{x}_{n} \) 中的 \( m \) 个. 不妨设可解出 \( \mathrm{d}{x}_{1},\mathrm{\;d}{x}_{2},\cdots ,\mathrm{d}{x}_{m} \) ,将其代入 \( n \) 元二次型 \( \left( {\mathrm{d}{x}_{1}\frac{\partial }{\partial {x}_{1}} + \mathrm{d}{x}_{2}\frac{\partial }{\partial {x}_{2}} + \cdots  + }\right. \; {\left. \mathrm{d}{x}_{n}\frac{\partial }{\partial {x}_{n}}\right) }^{2}L\left( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\right) \) 中,化为二次型 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i, j = 1}}^{{n - m}}{a}_{ij}\mathrm{\;d}{x}_{i}\mathrm{\;d}{x}_{j} \) . 令 \( \mathbf{A} = {\left( {a}_{ij}\right) }_{\left( {n - m}\right)  \times  \left( {n - m}\right) } \) . 若 \( \mathbf{A} \) 是正定的,则 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) 为 (条件) 极小值点. 若 \( \mathbf{A} \) 是负定的,则 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) 为 (条件) 极大值点. 若 \( \mathbf{A} \) 是不定的,则 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) 不是 (条件) 极值点. 若 \( \mathbf{A} \) 是半定的,则需进一步判定.

如此看来,若出现 \( \mathbf{H}\left( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\right) \) 既不正定,也不负定的情况下,讨论将相当复杂.

例题 21.4.1 求 \( f\left( {x, y}\right)  = a{x}^{2} + {2bxy} + c{y}^{2}\left( {b \neq  0}\right) \) 在条件 \( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} = 1 \) 之下的极值.

解 1 作 Lagrange 函数

\[
L\left( {x, y,\lambda }\right)  = a{x}^{2} + {2bxy} + c{y}^{2} - \lambda \left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} - 1}\right) .
\]

解方程组

\[
{L}_{x} = {2ax} + {2by} - {2\lambda x} = 0, \tag{21.11}
\]

\[
{L}_{y} = {2bx} + {2cy} - {2\lambda y} = 0, \tag{21.12}
\]

\[
{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} = 1 \tag{21.13}
\]

由 (21.13) 知 \( x, y \) 不可能同时为零,于是由 (21.11),(21.12) 得

\[
\left| \begin{matrix} a - \lambda & b \\  b & c - \lambda  \end{matrix}\right|  = {\lambda }^{2} - \left( {a + c}\right) \lambda  + {ac} - {b}^{2} = 0. \tag{21.14}
\]

因判别式 \( \Delta  = {\left( a + c\right) }^{2} - 4\left( {{ac} - {b}^{2}}\right)  = {\left( a - c\right) }^{2} + 4{b}^{2} > 0 \) ,从而解得两个相异实根

\[
{\lambda }_{ \pm  } = \frac{1}{2}\left\lbrack  {a + c \pm  \sqrt{{\left( a - c\right) }^{2} + 4{b}^{2}}}\right\rbrack  .
\]

对每个 \( \lambda \) ,由 (21.11) (或(21.12)) 与 (21.13) 解出两组解

\[
\left( {{\lambda }_{ + },{x}_{1},{y}_{1}}\right) ,\left( {{\lambda }_{ + },{x}_{2},{y}_{2}}\right) ,\left( {{\lambda }_{ - },{x}_{3},{y}_{3}}\right) ,\left( {{\lambda }_{ - },{x}_{4},{y}_{4}}\right) \text{ , }
\]

且 \( {y}_{1}{y}_{2}{y}_{3}{y}_{4} \neq  0 \) . 由 (21.11) 与 (21.12) 知

\[
\left| \begin{array}{ll} {L}_{xx} & {L}_{xy} \\  {L}_{xy} & {L}_{yy} \end{array}\right|  = \left| \begin{matrix} 2\left( {a - \lambda }\right) & {2b} \\  {2b} & 2\left( {c - \lambda }\right)  \end{matrix}\right|  = 0.
\]

从而在驻点,矩阵 \( \left( \begin{array}{ll} {L}_{xx} & {L}_{xy} \\  {L}_{xy} & {L}_{yy} \end{array}\right) \) 既不是正定的,也不是负定的. 此时,对 (21.13) 求微分得

\[
{2x}\mathrm{\;d}x + {2y}\mathrm{\;d}y = 0,
\]

解出

\[
\mathrm{d}y =  - \frac{x}{y}\mathrm{\;d}x.
\]

然后代入 \( {\mathrm{d}}^{2}L = 2\left( {a - \lambda }\right) \mathrm{d}{x}^{2} + {4b}\mathrm{\;d}x\mathrm{\;d}y + 2\left( {c - \lambda }\right) \mathrm{d}{y}^{2} \) 中,得

\[
{\mathrm{d}}^{2}L = \frac{2}{{y}^{2}}\left\lbrack  {\left( {a - \lambda }\right) {y}^{2} - {2bxy} + \left( {c - \lambda }\right) {x}^{2}}\right\rbrack  \mathrm{d}{x}^{2}
\]

\[
= \frac{2}{{y}^{2}}\left( {a + c - {2\lambda }}\right) \mathrm{d}{x}^{2}\left\{  \begin{array}{ll}  < 0, & \lambda  = {\lambda }_{ + }, \\   > 0, & \lambda  = {\lambda }_{ - }. \end{array}\right.
\]

因此,对应于 \( {\lambda }_{ + }\left( {\lambda }_{ - }\right) , f \) 取得极大 (小) 值.

解 2 用隐函数方法.

容易证明 \( b \neq  0 \) 时, \( f \) 的极值不在 \( \left( {\pm 1,0}\right) \) 取得,而除去这两点外, \( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} - 1 = 0 \) 在单位圆上任意点的某邻域内有连续可导的隐函数 \( y = h\left( x\right) \) ,且 \( {y}^{\prime } =  - \frac{x}{y} \) .

记 \( F\left( x\right)  = f\left( {x, h\left( x\right) }\right) \) ,则

\[
{F}^{\prime }\left( x\right)  = {2ax} + {2b}\left( {y + x{y}^{\prime }}\right)  + {2cy}{y}^{\prime }
\]

\[
= 2\left( {{ax} + {by}}\right)  + 2\left( {{bx} + {cy}}\right) \left( {-\frac{x}{y}}\right)  = \frac{2}{y}\left\lbrack  {\left( {a - c}\right) {xy} + b\left( {{y}^{2} - {x}^{2}}\right) }\right\rbrack  .
\]

令 \( {F}^{\prime }\left( x\right)  = 0 \) ,记 \( u = \frac{x}{y} \) ,由上式得

\[
b{u}^{2} - \left( {a - c}\right) u - b = 0.
\]

它的判别式 \( \Delta  = {\left( a - c\right) }^{2} + 4{b}^{2} > 0 \) ,故方程有相异实根

\[
{u}_{ \pm  } = \frac{1}{2b}\left\lbrack  {a - c \pm  \sqrt{{\left( a - c\right) }^{2} + 4{b}^{2}}}\right\rbrack  ,
\]

且异号. 将它们代入 \( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} = 1 \) 中可解得对应于 \( {u}_{ + } \) 的解 \( {x}_{1},{x}_{2} \) 和对应于 \( {u}_{ - } \) 的解 \( {x}_{3},{x}_{4} \) . 在这些驻点上

\[
{F}^{\prime \prime }\left( x\right)  = \frac{2}{y}\left\{  {\left( {a - c}\right) y - {2bx} + \left\lbrack  {\left( {a - c}\right) x + {2by}}\right\rbrack  \left( {-\frac{x}{y}}\right) }\right\}
\]

\[
= \frac{2}{{y}^{2}}\left\lbrack  {\left( {a - c}\right) \left( {{y}^{2} - {x}^{2}}\right)  - {4bxy}}\right\rbrack
\]

\[
= \frac{2xy}{{y}^{2}}\left\lbrack  {-\frac{{\left( a - c\right) }^{2}}{b} - {4b}}\right\rbrack
\]

\[
=  - \frac{2u}{b}\left\lbrack  {{\left( a - c\right) }^{2} + 4{b}^{2}}\right\rbrack  \left\{  \begin{array}{ll}  < 0, & u = {u}_{ + }, \\   > 0, & u = {u}_{ - }. \end{array}\right.
\]

故 \( {x}_{1},{x}_{2} \) 是 \( f \) 的极大值点, \( {x}_{3},{x}_{4} \) 是 \( f \) 的极小值点,其极大值和极小值分别为

\[
\left( {a{u}_{ + }^{2} + {2b}{u}_{ + } + c}\right) /\left( {{u}_{ + }^{2} + 1}\right) \text{ 和 }\left( {a{u}_{ - }^{2} + {2b}{u}_{ - } + c}\right) /\left( {{u}_{ - }^{2} + 1}\right) .
\]

解 3 注意到

\[
f\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left( {x, y}\right) \left( \begin{array}{ll} a & b \\  b & c \end{array}\right) \left( \begin{array}{l} x \\  y \end{array}\right)
\]

是一个实的二元二次型, 利用高等代数中的有关定理: 任意一个实二次型

\[
\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i, j = 1}}^{n}{a}_{ij}{x}_{i}{x}_{j}\;\left( {{a}_{ij} = {a}_{ji}, i, j = 1,2,\cdots , n}\right)
\]

都可以经过正交的线性变换变成平方和

\[
{\lambda }_{1}{y}_{1}^{2} + {\lambda }_{2}{y}_{2}^{2} + \cdots  + {\lambda }_{n}{y}_{n}^{2},
\]

其中平方项的系数 \( {\lambda }_{1},{\lambda }_{2},\cdots ,{\lambda }_{n} \) 就是矩阵 \( {\left( {a}_{ij}\right) }_{n \times  n} \) 的特征多项式全部的根. 因此 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 可化为

\[
f\left( {x, y}\right)  = {\lambda }_{1}{u}^{2} + {\lambda }_{2}{v}^{2}\text{ ，且 }{u}^{2} + {v}^{2} = 1\text{ ， }
\]

其中 \( u, v \) 是 \( x, y \) 的线性组合,然后再用 Lagrange 乘子法或初等方法求解.

注 有些极值问题会有初等解法, 但这些初等解法一般都有一定的局限性.

#### 21.4.2 条件最值

在很多实际问题中, 要求的是条件最值. 从理论上讲, 可通过求条件极值及与边界值、不可导点的值等的比较来得到条件最值. 但从上节看到, 确定条件极值是一件比较麻烦的事. 在实际解题过程中, 有许多简捷的方法, 下面作些讨论.

以三元函数 \( u = f\left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 和一个约束条件 \( g\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0 \) 为例 (设 \( f, g \) 在其定义域上连续可微). 在求 \( u \) 的最值时,只要最值存在,且 \( {g}_{x},{g}_{y},{g}_{z} \) 在 \( {D}_{1} = \; \{ \left( {x, y, z}\right)  \mid  g\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0\} \) 上不同时为 0,则最值点必是极值点,从而必是 Lagrange 函数的驻点. 事实上,设 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right) \) 是最值点,且 \( {g}_{z}\left( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\right)  \neq  0 \) ,则存在点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 的邻域 \( O\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) ,使在其中确定隐函数 \( z = z\left( {x, y}\right) \) . 于是 \( u = f\left( {x, y, z\left( {x, y}\right) }\right) \) 在 \( O\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 中有定义,且于点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 处取最值. 因为 \( O\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 是开区域,所以 \( u = f\left( {x, y, z\left( {x, y}\right) }\right) \) 在点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 处取极值. 由此可得求条件最值的方法如下:

1. 若约束为标准形式 \( g\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0 \) ,且约束集合 \( {D}_{1} = \{ \left( {x, y, z}\right)  \mid  g\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0\} \) 是有界闭集 (此时最值必定存在). 设 \( {g}_{x},{g}_{y},{g}_{z} \) 在 \( {D}_{1} \) 上不同时为 0,则求出 Lagrange 函数的全部驻点及相应的函数值, 再取其最值即可;

2. 若约束为标准形式 \( g\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0 \) ,但 \( {D}_{1} \) 为无界集. 通常加上附加约束,转化为具有附加约束条件的有界域上的条件最值问题;

3. \( {D}_{1} \) 为有界集,并有附加约束情况 (如 \( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2} = 1, x > 0, y > 0, z > 0 \) ). 先扩充为有界闭集 \( \left( {\text{ 如 }{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2} = 1, x \geq  0, y \geq  0, z \geq  0}\right) \) ,从而最值存在,其次求出 Lagrange 函数全部驻点及相应的 \( f \) 的值,令其最大者为 \( {M}_{1} \) ,最小者为 \( {m}_{1} \) , 然后求出 \( f \) 在附加约束边界上的最大值 \( {M}_{2} \) 和最小值 \( {m}_{2} \) ,若 \( {M}_{1} \geq  {M}_{2} \) ,则条件最大值为 \( {M}_{1} \) ; 若 \( {M}_{1} < {M}_{2} \) ,则条件最大值不存在. 条件最小值可类似讨论;

4. 标准约束 \( g\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0,{D}_{1} \) 无界,且具有附加约束 (如 \( x + y + z = 1, x > 0, y > \; 0, z > 0) \) . 通常应该首先证明条件最值存在,再证明条件最值点只能在某一有界域中, 从而转化为情形 3 .

注 对具体问题要作具体分析. 对某个具体问题, 很可能有体现这个问题特点的更为简捷的方法, 甚至是完全初等的方法.

例题 21.4.2 椭球面 \( \frac{{x}^{2}}{3} + {y}^{2} + \frac{{z}^{2}}{2} = 1 \) 被通过原点的平面 \( {2x} + y + z = 0 \) 截成一个椭圆 \( l \) . 求此椭圆的面积.

解 只要求出椭圆 \( l \) 的长短半轴即可. 于是问题转化为在约束条件

\[
F\left( {x, y, z}\right)  \equiv  \frac{{x}^{2}}{3} + {y}^{2} + \frac{{z}^{2}}{2} - 1 = 0, \tag{21.15}
\]

\[
G\left( {x, y, z}\right)  \equiv  {2x} + y + z = 0 \tag{21.16}
\]

下求 \( {r}^{2} = {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2} \) 之最大、最小值. 为此定义

\[
L\left( {x, y, z,\lambda ,\mu }\right)  = {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2} + \lambda \left( {\frac{{x}^{2}}{3} + {y}^{2} + \frac{{z}^{2}}{2} - 1}\right)  + \mu \left( {{2x} + y + z}\right) .
\]

令

\[
{L}_{x} = {2x} + \frac{2}{3}{\lambda x} + {2\mu } = 0, \tag{21.17}
\]

\[
{L}_{y} = {2y} + {2\lambda y} + \mu  = 0, \tag{21.18}
\]

\[
{L}_{z} = {2z} + {\lambda z} + \mu  = 0. \tag{21.19}
\]

将 (21.17)-(21.19) 分别乘 \( x, y, z \) ,然后相加,利用 (21.15),(21.16) 得

\[
\lambda  =  - {r}^{2}. \tag{21.20}
\]

将 (21.20) 代入 (21.17)，得

\[
\left( {{r}^{2} - 3}\right) x = {3\mu }
\]

若 \( {r}^{2} = 3 \) ,则 \( \mu  = 0 \) . 代入 (21.18),(21.19),得 \( y = z = 0 \) ,显然不满足 (21.15) 和 (21.16). 因此 \( {r}^{2} - 3 \neq  0 \) . 所以

\[
x = \frac{3\mu }{{r}^{2} - 3}. \tag{21.21}
\]

同理将 (21.20) 代入 (21.18), (21.19) 得

\[
y = \frac{\mu }{2\left( {{r}^{2} - 1}\right) };\;z = \frac{\mu }{{r}^{2} - 2}. \tag{21.22}
\]

将(21.21), (21.22) 代入 (21.16), 得

\[
\frac{6\mu }{{r}^{2} - 3} + \frac{\mu }{2\left( {{r}^{2} - 1}\right) } + \frac{\mu }{{r}^{2} - 2} = 0. \tag{21.23}
\]

由于 \( \mu  \neq  0 \) ,消去 \( \mu \) ,得

\[
{15}{\left( {r}^{2}\right) }^{2} - {49}{r}^{2} + {36} = 0.
\]

由此可以解出两个根 \( {r}_{1}^{2} \) 和 \( {r}_{2}^{2} \) 即为条件驻点对应的函数值. 由于约束集合是有界闭集,故 \( {r}^{2} \) 的最值存在,又因为在约束集合上 \( \frac{\partial \left( {F, G}\right) }{\partial \left( {y, z}\right) },\frac{\partial \left( {F, G}\right) }{\partial \left( {z, x}\right) },\frac{\partial \left( {F, G}\right) }{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) } \) 不同时为 0,故最值必定在条件驻点达到，因而这两个值也即为 \( {r}^{2} \) 的最大最小值，即长、短半轴的平方. 根据 Viète 定理有 \( {r}_{1}^{2} \cdot  {r}_{2}^{2} = \frac{36}{15} = \frac{12}{5} \) ,于是所求的面积为

\[
s = \pi {r}_{1}{r}_{2} = {2\pi }\sqrt{\frac{3}{5}}.
\]

注 1 本题所用解法也是条件极值或最值问题中常用的技巧, 其特点是不必求出条件驻点, 而直接求出所要求的条件最值.

注 2 在得到 (21.20) 之后可用下面的解法来求 \( \lambda \) :

由 (21.15) 知 \( x, y, z \) 不能同时为 0,于是在方程组 (21.16)-(21.19) 中视 \( x, y, z \) , \( \mu \) 为未知量, \( \lambda \) 为系数,则方程组有非零解. 于是系数矩阵的行列式为 0,即

\[
\left| \begin{matrix} 2 + \frac{2}{3}\lambda & 0 & 0 & 2 \\  0 & 2 + {2\lambda } & 0 & 1 \\  0 & 0 & 2 + \lambda & 1 \\  2 & 1 & 1 & 0 \end{matrix}\right|
\]

\[
= \left( {2 + \frac{2}{3}\lambda }\right) \left| \begin{matrix} 2 + {2\lambda } & 0 & 1 \\  0 & 2 + \lambda & 1 \\  1 & 1 & 0 \end{matrix}\right|  - 2\left| \begin{matrix} 0 & 2 + {2\lambda } & 0 \\  0 & 0 & 2 + \lambda \\  2 & 1 & 1 \end{matrix}\right|
\]

\[
=  - \left( {2 + \frac{2}{3}\lambda }\right) \left( {2 + \lambda  + 2 + {2\lambda }}\right)  - 4\left( {2 + {2\lambda }}\right) \left( {2 + \lambda }\right)  = 0,
\]

即

\[
{15}{\lambda }^{2} - {49\lambda } + {36} = 0.
\]

例题 21.4.3 设 \( p \geq  1 \) . 求 \( f\left( {x, y}\right)  = \frac{1}{2}\left( {{x}^{p} + {y}^{p}}\right) \) 在 \( x + y = C \) ( \( C \) 为正常数), \( x \geq  0, y \geq  0 \) 的条件下的最小值.

解 设

令

\[
L\left( {x, y,\lambda }\right)  = \frac{1}{2}\left( {{x}^{p} + {y}^{p}}\right)  + \lambda \left( {x + y - C}\right) .
\]

\[
{L}_{x} = \frac{p}{2}{x}^{p - 1} + \lambda  = 0
\]

\[
{L}_{y} = \frac{p}{2}{y}^{p - 1} + \lambda  = 0
\]

\[
x + y = C.
\]

解得驻点 \( x = y = \frac{C}{2} \) . 由于约束集是有界闭集,比较驻点值与附加约束集合边界点的值 \( f\left( {\frac{C}{2},\frac{C}{2}}\right)  = {\left( \frac{C}{2}\right) }^{p}, f\left( {0, C}\right)  = f\left( {C,0}\right)  = \frac{1}{2}{C}^{p} \) ,可知 \( {\left( \frac{C}{2}\right) }^{p} \) 为 \( f \) 在条件 \( x, y \geq  0 \) 下的最小值.

注 由本题结论可得

\[
\frac{1}{2}\left( {{x}^{p} + {y}^{p}}\right)  \geq  {\left( \frac{x + y}{2}\right) }^{p}.
\]

一般地，可以证明:若 \( p \geq  1,{x}_{i} \geq  0, i = 1,2,\cdots , n \) ，则有 (参见上册 259 页题 8)

\[
\frac{1}{n}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{x}_{i}^{p} \geq  {\left( \frac{1}{n}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}{x}_{i}\right) }^{p}.
\]

很多不等式都可以类似地化为条件最值问题来证明.

例题 21.4.4 求 \( f\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = \ln x + 2\ln y + 3\ln z \) 的最大值,其中 \( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2} = \; 6{r}^{2}\left( {r > 0}\right) , x > 0, y > 0, z > 0 \) . 且证明对任何正数 \( a, b, c \) ,有

\[
a{b}^{2}{c}^{3} \leq  {108}{\left( \frac{a + b + c}{6}\right) }^{6}. \tag{21.24}
\]

分析 原问题等价于求 \( u = x{y}^{2}{z}^{3} \) 的最大值,这样做是因为对数函数在零点无定义,而幂函数则不然,因而可将约束条件化为 \( x \geq  0, y \geq  0, z \geq  0 \) . 注意到在边界上 \( u \) 取最小值. 故在 \( {D}_{1} = \left\{  {\left( {x, y, z}\right)  \mid  {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2} = 6{r}^{2}, x \geq  0, y \geq  0, z \geq  0}\right\} \) 的内部一定有 \( u \) 的最大值点.

解 构造 Lagrange 函数

\[
L\left( {x, y, z,\lambda }\right)  = x{y}^{2}{z}^{3} + \lambda \left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2} - 6{r}^{2}}\right) .
\]

令

\[
{L}_{x} = {y}^{2}{z}^{3} + {2\lambda x} = 0, \tag{21.25}
\]

\[
{L}_{y} = {2xy}{z}^{3} + {2\lambda y} = 0, \tag{21.26}
\]

\[
{L}_{z} = {3x}{y}^{2}{z}^{2} + {2\lambda z} = 0, \tag{21.27}
\]

\[
{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2} = 6{r}^{2}. \tag{21.28}
\]

在 (21.25)-(21.27) 两边分别乘以 \( x, y, z \) ,然后相加,利用 (21.28) 得

\[
x{y}^{2}{z}^{3} =  - {2\lambda }{r}^{2}. \tag{21.29}
\]

再将 (21.29) 代入 (21.25)-(21.27) 中，明显地 \( \lambda  \neq  0 \) ，则

\[
x = r, y = \sqrt{2}r, z = \sqrt{3}r,\lambda  =  - 3\sqrt{3}{r}^{4}
\]

对应了惟一驻点,于是 \( x{y}^{2}{z}^{3} \) 的最大值为 \( 6\sqrt{3}{r}^{6} \) ,即

\[
x{y}^{2}{z}^{3} \leq  6\sqrt{3}{r}^{6}.
\]

两边平方得

\[
{x}^{2}{y}^{4}{z}^{6} \leq  {108}{r}^{12}.
\]

令 \( a = {x}^{2}, b = {y}^{2}, c = {z}^{2} \) ,则 \( {r}^{2} = \frac{a + b + c}{6} \) . 于是

\[
a{b}^{2}{c}^{3} \leq  {108}{\left( \frac{a + b + c}{6}\right) }^{6}.
\]

注 也可以用算术平均值一几何平均值不等式 (上册第 4 页) 证不等式 (21.24):

\[
a{b}^{2}{c}^{3} = {2}^{2}{3}^{3}a{\left( \frac{b}{2}\right) }^{2}{\left( \frac{c}{3}\right) }^{3} \leq  {108}{\left( \frac{a + b + c}{6}\right) }^{6}. \tag{21.30}
\]

#### 21.4.3 隐函数的极值

求隐函数的极值有两种方法: 第一种是用隐函数求导, 称为直接法; 第二种是化为条件极值去做, 称为间接法. 下面的例子给出了求隐函数极值的这两种方法.

例题 21.4.5 求由方程

\[
2{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2} + {2xy} - {2x} - {2y} - {4z} + 4 = 0 \tag{21.31}
\]

所确定的函数 \( z = z\left( {x, y}\right) \) 的极值.

解 1 (直接法) 设 \( g\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 2{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2} + {2xy} - {2x} - {2y} - {4z} + 4 \) , \( \frac{\partial g}{\partial z} = {2z} - 4 \) . 由 \( \frac{\partial g}{\partial z} \neq  0 \) 得 \( z \neq  2 \) . 在满足 (21.31) 的 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right) , z \neq  2 \) 附近确定了隐函数 \( z = z\left( {x, y}\right) \) . 由 (21.31) 对隐函数 \( z = z\left( {x, y}\right) \) 求导,得

\[
{4x} + {2z}\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} + {2y} - 2 - 4\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = 0, \tag{21.32}
\]

\[
{2y} + {2z}\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} + {2x} - 2 - 4\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = 0. \tag{21.33}
\]

令 \( \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = 0 \) ,则

\[
{2x} + y - 1 = 0,\;y + x - 1 = 0.
\]

由此得驻点为 \( \left( {0,1}\right) \) ，将之代入 \( \left( {21.31}\right) \) 得

\[
{z}^{2} - {4z} + 3 = 0.
\]

于是 \( {z}_{1} = 1,{z}_{2} = 3 \) ,在 (21.32),(21.33) 两边求导,得

\[
4 + 2{\left( \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\right) }^{2} + {2z}\frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial {x}^{2}} - 4\frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial {x}^{2}} = 0, \tag{21.34}
\]

\[
2\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} + {2z}\frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial x\partial y} + 2 - 4\frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial x\partial y} = 0, \tag{21.35}
\]

\[
2 + 2{\left( \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}\right) }^{2} + {2z}\frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial {y}^{2}} - 4\frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial {y}^{2}} = 0. \tag{21.36}
\]

在 (21.34)-(21.36) 中令 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right)  = \left( {0,1,1}\right) ,\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = 0 \) ,得 \( A = {z}_{xx} = \; 2, B = {z}_{xy} = 1, C = {z}_{yy} = 1 \) ,于是 \( D = {AC} - {B}^{2} > 0 \) . 故 \( z = 1 \) 为极小值.

在 (21.34)-(21.36) 中令 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right)  = \left( {0,1,3}\right) ,\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = 0 \) ,得 \( A = {z}_{xx} = \; - 2, B = {z}_{xy} =  - 1, C = {z}_{yy} =  - 1 \) ,于是 \( D = {AC} - {B}^{2} > 0 \) . 由此可见 \( z = 3 \) 为极大值.

解 2 以 (21.31) 为约束条件，取目标函数 \( f\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = z \) ，则 Lagrange 函数为

\[
L\left( {x, y, z,\lambda }\right)  = z + \lambda \left( {2{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2} + {2xy} - {2x} - {2y} - {4z} + 4}\right) .
\]

令

\[
{L}_{x} = {4\lambda x} + {2\lambda y} - {2\lambda } = 0, \tag{21.37}
\]

\[
{L}_{y} = {2\lambda x} + {2\lambda y} - {2\lambda } = 0, \tag{21.38}
\]

\[
{L}_{z} = 1 + {2\lambda z} - {4\lambda } = 0. \tag{21.39}
\]

显然 \( \lambda  \neq  0 \) ,于是由 (21.37) 和 (21.38) 得驻点为 \( \left( {0,1}\right) \) . 代入 (21.31) 得 \( {z}_{1} = 1,{z}_{2} = \) 3. 再由 (21.39) 得 \( {\lambda }_{1} = \frac{1}{2},{\lambda }_{2} =  - \frac{1}{2} \) . 由于

\[
{L}_{xx} = {4\lambda },{L}_{yy} = {L}_{zz} = {2\lambda },{L}_{xy} = {2\lambda },{L}_{xz} = {L}_{yz} = 0,
\]

于是 \( L \) 在 \( \left( {0,1,1}\right) \) 与 \( \left( {0,1,3}\right) \) 的 Hesse 矩阵分别为

\[
\left( \begin{array}{lll} 2 & 1 & 0 \\  1 & 1 & 0 \\  0 & 0 & 1 \end{array}\right) ,\;\left( \begin{matrix}  - 2 &  - 1 & 0 \\   - 1 &  - 1 & 0 \\  0 & 0 &  - 1 \end{matrix}\right)
\]

前者正定,后者负定,所以 \( z = 1 \) 为极小值, \( z = 3 \) 为极大值.

注 在上述条件极值问题中约束条件给出了一个有界闭集, 所以条件最值一定取到. 而且可以证明条件最值一定是条件极值,所以 \( z = 1 \) 和 \( z = 3 \) 分别是 (21.31) 确定的隐函数 \( z = z\left( {x, y}\right) \) 的最小值和最大值.
