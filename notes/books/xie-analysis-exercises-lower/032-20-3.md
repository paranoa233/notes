## §20.3 变量代换问题

今后, 例如在偏微分方程的求解过程中, 经常需要对自变量或函数作变量代换, 以求简化方程形式乃至求出方程的解. 变量代换计算的关键是隐函数组或反函数组的求导.

#### 20.3.1 仅变换自变量的情形

例题 20.3.1 在方程 \( {\left( \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\right) }^{2} + {\left( \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\right) }^{2} = u \) 中作极坐标变换 \( x = r\cos \theta , y = \; r\sin \theta \) ,试求方程在变换后的形式.

解 1 认为 \( u\left( {x, y}\right)  = u\left( {r\cos \theta , r\sin \theta }\right) \) ,则

\[
{u}_{r} = {u}_{x}\cos \theta  + {u}_{y}\sin \theta
\]

\[
{u}_{\theta } = r\left( {-{u}_{x}\sin \theta  + {u}_{y}\cos \theta }\right) .
\]

解此方程组得

\[
{u}_{x} = \frac{1}{r}\left( {r\cos \theta {u}_{r} - \sin \theta {u}_{\theta }}\right)
\]

\[
{u}_{y} = \frac{1}{r}\left( {r\sin \theta {u}_{r} + \cos \theta {u}_{\theta }}\right)
\]

代入原方程中得

\[
{u}_{r}^{2} + \frac{1}{{r}^{2}}{u}_{\theta }^{2} = u
\]

解 2 认为 \( u\left( {r,\theta }\right)  = u\left( {\sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}},\arctan \frac{y}{x}}\right) \) ,则

\[
{u}_{x} = {u}_{r}\frac{x}{r} - {u}_{\theta }\frac{y}{{r}^{2}}
\]

\[
{u}_{y} = {u}_{r}\frac{y}{r} + {u}_{\theta }\frac{x}{{r}^{2}}.
\]

于是 \( {u}_{x}^{2} + {u}_{y}^{2} = {u}_{r}^{2} + \frac{1}{{r}^{2}}{u}_{\theta }^{2} \) ,从而方程化为

\[
{u}_{r}^{2} + \frac{1}{{r}^{2}}{u}_{\theta }^{2} = u
\]

注 上述两种方法是自变量变换中通常使用的方法, 具体用哪一种方法使运算简单, 视具体情况而定.

例题 20.3.2 通过代换 \( x = {uv}, y = \frac{1}{2}\left( {{u}^{2} - {v}^{2}}\right) \) ,变换方程

\[
{\left( \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\right) }^{2} + {\left( \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}\right) }^{2} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}}}.
\]

解 由链式法则

\[
{z}_{u} = v{z}_{x} + u{z}_{y},\;{z}_{v} = u{z}_{x} - v{z}_{y}.
\]

两式平方后相加得

\[
{z}_{u}^{2} + {z}_{v}^{2} = \left( {{u}^{2} + {v}^{2}}\right) \left( {{z}_{x}^{2} + {z}_{y}^{2}}\right) .
\]

于是

\[
{z}_{x}^{2} + {z}_{y}^{2} = \frac{1}{{u}^{2} + {v}^{2}}\left( {{z}_{u}^{2} + {z}_{v}^{2}}\right) .
\]

另一方面

\[
{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} = \frac{1}{4}{\left( {u}^{2} + {v}^{2}\right) }^{2},
\]

代入原方程得

\[
{z}_{u}^{2} + {z}_{v}^{2} = 2
\]

#### 20.3.2 自变量与因变量同时变换的情形

这种情况比只变换自变量的情况稍复杂些, 它要多一个函数关系分析的步骤.

例题 20.3.3 通过代换 \( x = t, y = \frac{t}{1 + {tu}}, z = \frac{t}{1 + {tv}} \) ,试把方程

\[
{x}^{2}{z}_{x} + {y}^{2}{z}_{y} = {z}^{2} \tag{20.17}
\]

变为以 \( v \) 为因变量, \( t, u \) 为自变量的形式.

分析 题目的意思是有一个函数 \( z = z\left( {x, y}\right) \) 满足方程 (20.17),作自变量代换

\[
x = t,\;y = \frac{t}{1 + {tu}} \tag{20.18}
\]

以及因变量代换

\[
z = \frac{t}{1 + {tv}} \tag{20.19}
\]

得到函数 \( v = v\left( {t, u}\right) \) . 求 \( v = v\left( {t, u}\right) \) 满足的方程.

解 1 直接从关系式 (20.19) 出发求 \( {z}_{x} \) 和 \( {z}_{y} \) :

\[
{z}_{x} = {\left( \frac{t}{1 + {tv}}\right) }_{t}{t}_{x} + {\left( \frac{t}{1 + {tv}}\right) }_{v}\left( {{v}_{t}{t}_{x} + {v}_{u}{u}_{x}}\right) , \tag{20.20}
\]

\[
{z}_{y} = {\left( \frac{t}{1 + {tv}}\right) }_{t}{t}_{y} + {\left( \frac{t}{1 + {tv}}\right) }_{v}\left( {{v}_{t}{t}_{y} + {v}_{u}{u}_{y}}\right) . \tag{20.21}
\]

由 (20.18) 得

\[
t = x,\;u = \frac{1}{y} - \frac{1}{x}.
\]

于是

\[
{t}_{x} = 1,\;{t}_{y} = 0
\]

\[
{u}_{x} = \frac{1}{{x}^{2}},\;{u}_{y} =  - \frac{1}{{y}^{2}}.
\]

将它们代入 (20.20), (20.21), 得到

\[
{z}_{x} = {\left( \frac{t}{1 + {tv}}\right) }_{t} + {\left( \frac{t}{1 + {tv}}\right) }_{v}\left( {{v}_{t} + \frac{1}{{x}^{2}}{v}_{u}}\right)
\]

\[
{z}_{y} =  - {\left( \frac{t}{1 + {tv}}\right) }_{v}\frac{1}{{y}^{2}}{v}_{u}
\]

将以上式子代入 (20.17), 得到

\[
{x}^{2}\left\lbrack  {{\left( \frac{t}{1 + {tv}}\right) }_{t} + {\left( \frac{t}{1 + {tv}}\right) }_{v}{v}_{t}}\right\rbrack   = {z}^{2}.
\]

整理得

\[
{v}_{t} = 0\text{ . }
\]

解 2 从 (20.19) 中解出 \( v \) 得

\[
v = \frac{1}{z} - \frac{1}{t}
\]

于是

\[
{v}_{t} =  - \frac{1}{{z}^{2}}\left( {{z}_{x}{x}_{t} + {z}_{y}{y}_{t}}\right)  + \frac{1}{{t}^{2}}, \tag{20.22}
\]

\[
{v}_{u} =  - \frac{1}{{z}^{2}}\left( {{z}_{x}{x}_{u} + {z}_{y}{y}_{u}}\right) . \tag{20.23}
\]

由 (20.18) 得

\[
{x}_{t} = 1,\;{x}_{u} = 0
\]

\[
{y}_{t} = \frac{1}{{\left( 1 + tu\right) }^{2}},\;{y}_{u} =  - \frac{{t}^{2}}{{\left( 1 + tu\right) }^{2}}.
\]

将它们代入(20.22)，(20.23)得

\[
{v}_{t} =  - \frac{1}{{z}^{2}}\left\lbrack  {{z}_{x} + \frac{1}{{\left( 1 + tu\right) }^{2}}{z}_{y}}\right\rbrack   + \frac{1}{{t}^{2}}
\]

\[
{v}_{u} = \frac{1}{{z}^{2}} \cdot  \frac{{t}^{2}}{{\left( 1 + tu\right) }^{2}}{z}_{y}
\]

由此解出 \( {z}_{x},{z}_{y} \) ,得

\[
{z}_{x} = {z}^{2}\left( {\frac{1}{{t}^{2}} - {v}_{t}}\right)  - \frac{{z}^{2}}{{t}^{2}}{v}_{u}
\]

\[
{z}_{y} = \frac{{z}^{2}{\left( 1 + tu\right) }^{2}}{{t}^{2}}{v}_{u}
\]

将它们代入原方程, 得

\[
{v}_{t} = 0\text{ . }
\]

#### 20.3.3 练习题

1. 把方程 \( \left( {x - y}\right) \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} + y\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = 0 \) 变为 \( x \) 作因变量, \( y, z \) 为自变量的形式.

2. 引用新函数 \( r = \sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}},\varphi  = \arctan \frac{y}{x} \) 变换微分式

\[
w = x\frac{{\mathrm{d}}^{2}y}{\mathrm{\;d}{t}^{2}} - y\frac{{\mathrm{d}}^{2}x}{\mathrm{\;d}{t}^{2}}.
\]

3. 以 \( \xi  = x + y,\eta  = x - y \) 为新自变量,变换方程 \( \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} \) .

4. 取 \( u = y + z{\mathrm{e}}^{-x}, v = x + z{\mathrm{e}}^{-y} \) 为新的自变量,变换微分式

\[
F = \left( {z + {\mathrm{e}}^{x}}\right) \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} + \left( {z + {\mathrm{e}}^{y}}\right) \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} - \left( {{z}^{2} - {\mathrm{e}}^{x + y}}\right) .
\]

5. 设 \( u = x{\mathrm{e}}^{z}, v = y{\mathrm{e}}^{z}, w = z{\mathrm{e}}^{z} \) ,试以 \( w \) 为新的因变量, \( u, v \) 为新的自变量,变换方程 \( \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} \) .

6. 以 \( w \) 为新的因变量, \( \xi ,\eta ,\zeta \) 为新的自变量,变换方程

\[
x\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} + y\frac{\partial u}{\partial y} + z\frac{\partial u}{\partial z} = u + \frac{xy}{z},
\]

其中 \( \xi  = \frac{x}{z},\eta  = \frac{y}{z},\zeta  = z, w = \frac{u}{z} \) .

7. 试求 \( \frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {x}^{2}} + \frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {y}^{2}} + \frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {z}^{2}} = 0 \) 在球坐标下的形式.

8. 以 \( u = x + {2y} + 2, v = x - y - 1 \) 为新的自变量,变换方程

\[
2\frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial {x}^{2}} + \frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial x\partial y} - \frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial {y}^{2}} + \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = 0.
\]

9. 以 \( w \) 为新的因变量, \( u, v \) 为新的自变量,变换方程

\[
\frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial {x}^{2}} - 2\frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial x\partial y} + \left( {1 + \frac{y}{x}}\right) \frac{{\partial }^{2}z}{\partial {y}^{2}} = 0,
\]

其中 \( u = x, v = x + y, w = x + y + z \) .

10. 设 \( {xu} = {x}^{2} + {y}^{2},{yv} = {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} \) ,证明: \( \frac{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) }{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) } =  - \frac{uv}{xy} \) .

11. 若 \( u\left( {x, y, z}\right) , v\left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 可微,证明:

\[
\frac{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) }{\partial \left( {y, z}\right) }\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) }{\partial \left( {z, x}\right) }\frac{\partial u}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) }{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) }\frac{\partial u}{\partial z} = 0.
\]

12. 设 \( u = {xy}, v = \frac{x}{y} \) ,试求 \( \frac{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) }{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) },\frac{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) }{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) } \) .

13. 设 \( u = \frac{x}{{r}^{2}}, v = \frac{y}{{r}^{2}}, w = \frac{z}{{r}^{2}} \) ,其中 \( {r}^{2} = {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2} \) ,试求 \( \frac{\partial \left( {u, v, w}\right) }{\partial \left( {x, y, z}\right) } \) .
