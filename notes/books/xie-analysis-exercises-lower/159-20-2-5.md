#### 20.2.5 练习题

1. 设 \( x = {\mathrm{e}}^{v} + {u}^{3}, y = {\mathrm{e}}^{u} - {v}^{3} \) ,求反函数组的一阶偏导数 \( \frac{\partial u}{\partial x},\frac{\partial u}{\partial y} \) .

2. 对由方程组

\[
\left\{  \begin{array}{l} x + y + z = 0 \\  {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2} = 1 \end{array}\right.
\]

确定的函数 \( x = x\left( z\right) , y = y\left( z\right) \) ,求在点 \( \left( {\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}},\frac{-1}{\sqrt{2}},0}\right) \) 处的导数 \( \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{\;d}z},\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{\;d}z} \) .

3. 对方程组

\[
\left\{  \begin{array}{l} {x}^{3} + {y}^{3} + {z}^{3} = {3xyz}, \\  x + y + z = a \end{array}\right.
\]

确定的隐函数组 \( y = y\left( x\right) , z = z\left( x\right) \) ,求出导数 \( \frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{\;d}x},\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{\;d}x},\frac{{\mathrm{d}}^{2}y}{\mathrm{\;d}{x}^{2}},\frac{{\mathrm{d}}^{2}z}{\mathrm{\;d}{x}^{2}} \) .

4. 设

\[
\left\{  \begin{array}{l} x = u\cos \frac{v}{u}, \\  y = \sin \frac{v}{u}, \end{array}\right.
\]

求反函数组的偏导数 \( {u}_{x},{u}_{y},{v}_{x},{v}_{y} \) .

5. 设 \( u = u\left( x\right) \) 是由方程组 \( u = f\left( {x, y, z}\right) , g\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0, h\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0 \) 所确定. 求 \( \frac{\mathrm{d}u}{\mathrm{\;d}x},\frac{{\mathrm{d}}^{2}u}{\mathrm{\;d}{x}^{2}}. \)

6. 求由方程组 \( x = u\cos v, y = u\sin v, z = v \) 确定的 \( z = z\left( {x, y}\right) \) 的所有二阶偏导数.

7. 设 \( z = z\left( {x, y}\right) \) 为由方程组 \( x = {\mathrm{e}}^{u + v}, y = {\mathrm{e}}^{u - v}, z = {uv} \) 所定义的函数,求当 \( \left( {u, v}\right)  = \left( {0,0}\right) \) 时的 \( \mathrm{d}z,{\mathrm{\;d}}^{2}z \) .

8. 设 \( u = f\left( {x, y, z}\right) , g\left( {{x}^{2},{\mathrm{e}}^{y}, z}\right)  = 0, y = \sin x \) ,且已知 \( f \) 与 \( g \) 都有一阶连续偏导数, 求 \( \frac{\mathrm{d}u}{\mathrm{\;d}x} \) .
