#### 23.4.2 参考题

1. 设 \( f\left( {x, t}\right) ,{f}_{x}\left( {x, t}\right) \) 连续,记 \( u\left( {x, t}\right)  = \frac{1}{2a}{\int }_{0}^{t}\mathrm{\;d}\tau {\int }_{x - a\left( {t - \tau }\right) }^{x + a\left( {t - \tau }\right) }f\left( {\xi ,\tau }\right) \mathrm{d}\xi \) ,其中 \( a \) 为正常数,证明: \( u\left( {x, t}\right) \) 满足

\[
\frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {t}^{2}} = {a}^{2}\frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {x}^{2}} + f\left( {x, t}\right) .
\]

2. 设 \( n \) 为正整数,证明: Bessel 函数

\[
{J}_{n}\left( x\right)  = \frac{1}{\pi }{\int }_{0}^{\pi }\cos \left( {{n\varphi } - x\sin \varphi }\right) \mathrm{d}\varphi
\]

满足

\[
{x}^{2}{J}_{n}^{\prime \prime }\left( x\right)  + x{J}_{n}^{\prime }\left( x\right)  + \left( {{x}^{2} - {n}^{2}}\right) {J}_{n}\left( x\right)  = 0.
\]

3. 设 \( f\left( x\right) \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 上连续可微,且 \( f\left( 0\right)  = 0 \) ,定义

\[
\varphi \left( x\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{x}\frac{f\left( t\right) }{\sqrt{x - t}}\mathrm{\;d}t,0 < x \leq  1,\varphi \left( 0\right)  = 0.
\]

证明:

(1) \( \varphi \left( x\right) \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 上一阶连续可导,且

\[
{\varphi }^{\prime }\left( x\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{x}\frac{{f}^{\prime }\left( t\right) }{\sqrt{x - t}}\mathrm{\;d}t,0 < x \leq  1,{\varphi }^{\prime }\left( 0\right)  = 0;
\]

(2) \( f\left( x\right)  = \frac{1}{\pi }{\int }_{0}^{x}\frac{{\varphi }^{\prime }\left( t\right) }{\sqrt{x - t}}\mathrm{\;d}t,0 < x \leq  1 \) .

4. 设 \( f\left( x\right) \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {0, A}\right\rbrack \) 上单调 \( \left( {A > 0}\right) \) ,证明:

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\alpha  \rightarrow   + \infty }}{\int }_{0}^{A}f\left( x\right) \frac{\sin {\alpha x}}{x}\mathrm{\;d}x = \frac{\pi }{2}f\left( {0}^{ + }\right) .
\]

5. 设 \( F\left( t\right)  = t{\int }_{0}^{+\infty }{\mathrm{e}}^{-{tx}}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \) ,其中 \( f\left( x\right) \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {0, b}\right\rbrack \) 上有界可积 \( \left( {\forall b > 0}\right) \) ,且 \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{x \rightarrow   + \infty }}f\left( x\right)  = \alpha \) ,证明: \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}F\left( t\right)  = \alpha \) .

6. 设 \( f\left( x\right) \) 连续,且 \( {\int }_{-\infty }^{+\infty }{f}^{2}\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \) 收敛,证明:

(1) \( g\left( t\right)  = {\int }_{-\infty }^{+\infty }f\left( {t + u}\right) f\left( u\right) \mathrm{d}u \) 在 \( \left( {-\infty , + \infty }\right) \) 上连续且有界;

(2) \( \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\varepsilon  \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}\frac{1}{2\sqrt{\varepsilon \pi }}{\int }_{-\infty }^{+\infty }{\mathrm{e}}^{-{t}^{2}/\left( {4\varepsilon }\right) }g\left( t\right) \mathrm{d}t = {\int }_{-\infty }^{+\infty }{f}^{2}\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \) .

7. 讨论下列函数在 \( \left( {0,1}\right) \) 上的连续性:

(1) \( f\left( \alpha \right)  = {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{{\mathrm{e}}^{-t}}{{\left| \sin t\right| }^{\alpha }}\mathrm{d}t \)

(2) \( g\left( \alpha \right)  = {\int }_{0}^{1}\frac{f\left( t\right) }{\sqrt{\left| t - \alpha \right| }}\mathrm{d}t \) ,其中 \( f\left( t\right) \) 是 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 上的有界可积函数.

8. 求曲面 \( {\left( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2}\right) }^{2} + {z}^{4} = y \) 所围立体的体积.

9. 求立体

\[
{\left( \frac{x}{a}\right) }^{\frac{1}{n}} + {\left( \frac{y}{b}\right) }^{\frac{1}{n}} + {\left( \frac{z}{c}\right) }^{\frac{1}{n}} \leq  1,\;x, y, z \geq  0
\]

的质心的 \( x \) 坐标.

10. 求星形线 \( {x}^{\frac{2}{3}} + {y}^{\frac{2}{3}} = {R}^{\frac{2}{3}} \) 所包围的面积对 \( x \) 轴的惯性矩.

11. 设 \( f\left( x\right) \) 在 \( \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack \) 中连续,证明:

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{t \rightarrow   + \infty }}{\int }_{0}^{1}t{\mathrm{e}}^{-{t}^{2}{x}^{2}}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = \frac{\sqrt{\pi }}{2}f\left( 0\right) .
\]

12. 设 \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \) 收敛,证明:

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{y \rightarrow  {0}^{ + }}}{\int }_{0}^{+\infty }{\mathrm{e}}^{-{xy}}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x.
\]

13. 求 \( {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\cos {x}^{p}\mathrm{\;d}x \) ,其中 \( p > 1 \) .

14. 求 \( {\int }_{0}^{1}\ln \Gamma \left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \) .

15. 求 Laplace (拉普拉斯) 积分

\[
{I}_{k} = {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{\cos {bx}}{{\left( {a}^{2} + {x}^{2}\right) }^{k}}\mathrm{\;d}x,\;{J}_{k} = {\int }_{0}^{+\infty }\frac{x\sin {bx}}{{\left( {a}^{2} + {x}^{2}\right) }^{k}}\mathrm{\;d}x, a, b > 0, k \in  {\mathbf{N}}_{ + }.
\]

16. 应用 \( \Gamma \) 函数的 Gauss 乘积分解公式 (13.38) 证明: Euler 常数

\[
\gamma  = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{n \rightarrow  \infty }}\left( {1 + \frac{1}{2} + \cdots  + \frac{1}{n} - \ln n}\right)  =  - {\Gamma }^{\prime }\left( 1\right) .
\]

17. (1) 利用 \( n \) 次单位根分解式证明:

\[
1 + x + \cdots  + {x}^{n - 1} = \mathop{\prod }\limits_{{k = 1}}^{{n - 1}}\left( {x - {\mathrm{e}}^{\frac{{2k\pi }\mathrm{i}}{n}}}\right) ;
\]

(2)利用(1)证明:

\[
\mathop{\prod }\limits_{{k = 1}}^{{n - 1}}\sin \frac{k\pi }{n} = \frac{n}{{2}^{n - 1}}
\]

(3) 证明: Euler 乘积

\[
\Gamma \left( \frac{1}{n}\right) \Gamma \left( \frac{2}{n}\right) \cdots \Gamma \left( \frac{n - 1}{n}\right)  = \frac{{\left( 2\pi \right) }^{\frac{n - 1}{2}}}{\sqrt{n}}.
\]
