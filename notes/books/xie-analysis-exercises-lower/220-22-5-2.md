#### 22.5.2 物理应用

矩 力学中的某些量常常与物体的密度函数的各阶矩有关. 下面讨论三维的情形，二维的情形是类似的.

设 \( V \) 是由分片光滑的连续曲面围成的区域. \( \mu \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 在 \( V \) 上连续，分别称

\[
{M}_{x}\left( k\right)  = {\iiint }_{V}{x}^{k}\mu \left( {x, y, z}\right) \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{\;d}y\mathrm{\;d}z
\]

\[
{M}_{y}\left( k\right)  = {\iiint }_{V}{y}^{k}\mu \left( {x, y, z}\right) \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{\;d}y\mathrm{\;d}z
\]

\[
{M}_{z}\left( k\right)  = {\iiint }_{V}{z}^{k}\mu \left( {x, y, z}\right) \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{\;d}y\mathrm{\;d}z
\]

为密度函数 \( \mu \) 关于 \( x, y, z \) 的 \( k \) 阶矩. 利用微元法容易得到

1. 质量 \( m = {M}_{x}\left( 0\right)  = {M}_{y}\left( 0\right)  = {M}_{z}\left( 0\right)  = M\left( 0\right)  = {\iiint }_{V}\mu \left( {x, y, z}\right) \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{\;d}y\mathrm{\;d}z \) .

\[
{X}_{c} = \frac{{M}_{x}\left( 1\right) }{M\left( 0\right) } = \frac{1}{m}{\iiint }_{V}{x\mu }\left( {x, y, z}\right) \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{\;d}y\mathrm{\;d}z
\]

\[
{Y}_{c} = \frac{{M}_{y}\left( 1\right) }{M\left( 0\right) } = \frac{1}{m}{\iiint }_{V}{y\mu }\left( {x, y, z}\right) \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{\;d}y\mathrm{\;d}z
\]

\[
{Z}_{c} = \frac{{M}_{z}\left( 1\right) }{M\left( 0\right) } = \frac{1}{m}{\iiint }_{V}{z\mu }\left( {x, y, z}\right) \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{\;d}y\mathrm{\;d}z.
\]

3. 转动惯量为

\[
{I}_{x} = {M}_{y}\left( 2\right)  + {M}_{z}\left( 2\right) ,\;{I}_{y} = {M}_{z}\left( 2\right)  + {M}_{x}\left( 2\right) ,\;{I}_{z} = {M}_{x}\left( 2\right)  + {M}_{y}\left( 2\right) ,
\]

\[
{I}_{yz} = {M}_{x}\left( 2\right) ,\;{I}_{zx} = {M}_{y}\left( 2\right) ,\;{I}_{xy} = {M}_{z}\left( 2\right) .
\]

例题 22.5.4 若直线 \( x = 0, x = a, y = 0 \) 与正连续曲线 \( y = f\left( x\right) \) 围成的区域的质心的 \( x \) 坐标是 \( g\left( a\right) \) ,证明:

\[
f\left( x\right)  = \frac{A{g}^{\prime }\left( x\right) }{{\left\lbrack  x - g\left( x\right) \right\rbrack  }^{2}}\exp \left( {\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x - g\left( x\right) }}\right) ,
\]

其中 \( A \) 为正常数， \( a \) 是参数.

证 见图 22.10,

\[
g\left( a\right)  = \frac{{M}_{x}\left( 1\right) }{M\left( 0\right) } = \frac{{\int }_{0}^{a}{xf}\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x}{{\int }_{0}^{a}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x},
\]

即

\[
g\left( a\right) {\int }_{0}^{a}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = {\int }_{0}^{a}{xf}\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x.
\]

两边对 \( a \) 求导得

\[
g\left( a\right) f\left( a\right)  + {g}^{\prime }\left( a\right) {\int }_{0}^{a}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = {af}\left( a\right) .
\]

令 \( F\left( a\right)  = {\int }_{0}^{a}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x \) ,注意到 \( a - g\left( a\right)  \neq  0 \) ,则

\[
\frac{{F}^{\prime }\left( a\right) }{F\left( a\right) } = \frac{{g}^{\prime }\left( a\right) }{a - g\left( a\right) }.
\]

两边对 \( a \) 积分,得

\[
\ln F\left( a\right)  = \int \frac{{g}^{\prime }\left( a\right) }{a - g\left( a\right) }\mathrm{d}a + C.
\]

所以

\[
{\int }_{0}^{a}f\left( x\right) \mathrm{d}x = F\left( a\right)  = A\exp \left( {\int \frac{{g}^{\prime }\left( a\right) }{a - g\left( a\right) }\mathrm{d}a}\right) .
\]

两边对 \( a \) 求导得

\[
f\left( a\right)  = \frac{A{g}^{\prime }\left( a\right) }{a - g\left( a\right) }\exp \left( {\int \frac{{g}^{\prime }\left( a\right) }{a - g\left( a\right) }\mathrm{d}a}\right) .
\]

考虑到

\[
\int \frac{{g}^{\prime }\left( a\right) }{a - g\left( a\right) }\mathrm{d}a = \int \frac{{g}^{\prime }\left( a\right)  - 1}{a - g\left( a\right) }\mathrm{d}a + \int \frac{\mathrm{d}a}{a - g\left( a\right) }
\]

\[
=  - \ln \left( {a - g\left( a\right) }\right)  + \int \frac{\mathrm{d}a}{a - g\left( a\right) },
\]

则

\[
f\left( a\right)  = \frac{A{g}^{\prime }\left( a\right) }{{\left\lbrack  a - g\left( a\right) \right\rbrack  }^{2}}\exp \left( {\int \frac{\mathrm{d}a}{a - g\left( a\right) }}\right) .
\]

引力 考虑体密度函数为 \( \mu \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 的立体 \( V \) 对具有质量 \( m \) 的点 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right) \) 的引力 \( \mathbf{F} \) . 由引力定律及微元法得 \( \mathbf{F} = \left( {{F}_{x},{F}_{y},{F}_{z}}\right) \) ,其中

\[
{F}_{x} = {\iiint }_{V}\frac{{Gm\mu }\left( {x, y, z}\right) \left( {x - {x}_{0}}\right) }{{r}^{3}}\mathrm{\;d}x\mathrm{\;d}y\mathrm{\;d}z,
\]

\[
{F}_{y} = {\iiint }_{V}\frac{{Gm\mu }\left( {x, y, z}\right) \left( {y - {y}_{0}}\right) }{{r}^{3}}\mathrm{\;d}x\mathrm{\;d}y\mathrm{\;d}z
\]

\[
{F}_{z} = {\iiint }_{V}\frac{{Gm\mu }\left( {x, y, z}\right) \left( {z - {z}_{0}}\right) }{{r}^{3}}\mathrm{\;d}x\mathrm{\;d}y\mathrm{\;d}z
\]

其中 \( G \) 为引力常量, \( r = {\left\lbrack  {\left( x - {x}_{0}\right) }^{2} + {\left( y - {y}_{0}\right) }^{2} + {\left( z - {z}_{0}\right) }^{2}\right\rbrack  }^{1/2} \) .
