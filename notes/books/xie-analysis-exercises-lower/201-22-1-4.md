#### 22.1.4 练习题

1. 设 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) , g\left( {x, y}\right) \) 都是 \( D \) 上的可积函数,证明:

\[
h\left( {x, y}\right)  = \max \{ f\left( {x, y}\right) , g\left( {x, y}\right) \}
\]

也是 \( D \) 上的可积函数. 2. 设 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在点 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 的某邻域中连续,求

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\rho  \rightarrow  0}}\frac{1}{\pi {\rho }^{2}}{\iint }_{{\left( x - {x}_{0}\right) }^{2} + {\left( y - {y}_{0}\right) }^{2} \leq  {\rho }^{2}}f\left( {x, y}\right) \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{\;d}y.
\]

3. 证明:

\[
{\iint }_{\left| x\right|  + \left| y\right|  \leq  1}f\left( {x + y}\right) \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{\;d}y = {\int }_{-1}^{1}f\left( u\right) \mathrm{d}u.
\]

4. 证明:

\[
{\iint }_{D}f\left( {xy}\right) \mathrm{d}x\mathrm{\;d}y = \ln 2{\int }_{1}^{2}f\left( u\right) \mathrm{d}u
\]

其中 \( D \) 为 \( {xy} = 1,{xy} = 2, y = x, y = {4x} \) 在第一象限所围成的区域.
