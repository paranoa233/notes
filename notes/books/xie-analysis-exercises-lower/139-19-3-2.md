#### 19.3.2 例题

例题 19.3.1 对于幂指函数 \( u = {x}^{y} \) ,令 \( x = \varphi \left( t\right) , y = \psi \left( t\right) \) ,求 \( \frac{\mathrm{d}u}{\mathrm{\;d}t} \) .

解 由 (19.3) 得

\[
\frac{\mathrm{d}u}{\mathrm{\;d}t} = \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}{\varphi }^{\prime }\left( t\right)  + \frac{\partial u}{\partial y}{\psi }^{\prime }\left( t\right)
\]

\[
= y{x}^{y - 1}{\varphi }^{\prime }\left( t\right)  + {x}^{y}\ln x{\psi }^{\prime }\left( t\right)  = {x}^{y}\left( {\frac{y}{x}{\varphi }^{\prime }\left( t\right)  + \ln x{\psi }^{\prime }\left( t\right) }\right)
\]

\[
= {\left\lbrack  \varphi \left( t\right) \right\rbrack  }^{\psi \left( t\right) }\left\{  {\frac{\psi \left( t\right) }{\varphi \left( t\right) }{\varphi }^{\prime }\left( t\right)  + \left\lbrack  {\ln \psi \left( t\right) }\right\rbrack  {\psi }^{\prime }\left( t\right) }\right\}  .
\]

(以前我们曾用另外的方法证明过它, 参见上册 165-166 页的对数求导法.)

作为公式 (19.3) 的另一个应用, 讨论行列式

\[
\Delta  = \left| \begin{matrix} {a}_{11} & {a}_{12} & \cdots & {a}_{1n} \\  {a}_{21} & {a}_{22} & \cdots & {a}_{2n} \\  \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\  {a}_{n1} & {a}_{n2} & \cdots & {a}_{nn} \end{matrix}\right|
\]

的求导,其中假定元素 \( {a}_{ij}\left( {i, j = 1,2,\cdots , n}\right) \) 都是 \( t \) 的一元函数,它们关于 \( t \) 的导数 \( \frac{\mathrm{d}{a}_{ij}}{\mathrm{\;d}t} \) 都存在.

例题 19.3.2 证明: 行列式 \( \Delta \) 的导数等于把 \( \Delta \) 内的第 1 行直到第 \( n \) 行的元素依次换成它们的导数而得出的 \( n \) 个行列式之和.

证 回忆行列式关于第 \( i \) 行元素的展开式

\[
\Delta  = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{j = 1}}^{n}{A}_{ij}{a}_{ij}
\]

其中 \( {A}_{ij} \) 为 \( {a}_{ij} \) 的代数余子式. 容易看出 \( {A}_{i1},{A}_{i2},\cdots ,{A}_{in} \) 中不含有元素 \( {a}_{ij} \) , \( 1 \leq  j \leq  n \) ,于是

\[
\frac{\partial \Delta }{\partial {a}_{ij}} = {A}_{ij}
\]

按照公式 (19.3) 有

\[
\frac{\mathrm{d}\Delta }{\mathrm{d}t} = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{j = 1}}^{n}\frac{\partial \Delta }{\partial {a}_{ij}} \cdot  \frac{\mathrm{d}{a}_{ij}}{\mathrm{\;d}t} = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{n}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{j = 1}}^{n}{A}_{ij}\frac{\mathrm{d}{a}_{ij}}{\mathrm{\;d}t}.
\]

注意到 \( \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{j = 1}}^{n}{A}_{ij}\frac{\mathrm{d}{a}_{ij}}{\mathrm{\;d}t} \) 也表示一个行列式的展开式，它同已知行列式 \( \Delta \) 的差别仅在于把 \( \Delta \) 中第 \( i \) 行的元素 \( {a}_{i1},{a}_{i2},\cdots ,{a}_{in} \) 换成它们的导数 \( \frac{\mathrm{d}{a}_{i1}}{\mathrm{\;d}t},\frac{\mathrm{d}{a}_{i2}}{\mathrm{\;d}t},\cdots ,\frac{\mathrm{d}{a}_{in}}{\mathrm{\;d}t} \) ,由此可知结论成立.

注 在使用链式法则 (19.2) 时,要求 \( f\left( {{u}_{1},{u}_{2},\cdots ,{u}_{m}}\right) \) 在点 \( \left( {{u}_{1},{u}_{2},\cdots ,{u}_{m}}\right) \) 可微，否则公式 (19.2) 有可能失效，请看下面的例子:

\[
f\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \frac{{x}^{2}y}{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}}, & \text{ 当 }\left( {x, y}\right)  \neq  \left( {0,0}\right) , \\  0, & \text{ 当 }\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left( {0,0}\right) . \end{array}\right.
\]

由于

\[
{f}_{x}\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \frac{{2x}{y}^{3}}{{\left( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2}\right) }^{2}}, & \text{ 当 }\left( {x, y}\right)  \neq  \left( {0,0}\right) , \\  0, & \text{ 当 }\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left( {0,0}\right) , \end{array}\right.
\]

\[
{f}_{y}\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left\{  \begin{array}{ll} \frac{{x}^{2}\left( {{x}^{2} - {y}^{2}}\right) }{{\left( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2}\right) }^{2}}, & \text{ 当 }\left( {x, y}\right)  \neq  \left( {0,0}\right) , \\  0, & \text{ 当 }\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left( {0,0}\right) , \end{array}\right.
\]

可以看出 \( {f}_{x} \) 与 \( {f}_{y} \) 都在 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 点不连续,容易证明 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 点不可微.

令 \( x = y = t \) ,则 \( f\left( {t, t}\right)  = \frac{1}{2}t \) ,从而 \( \frac{\mathrm{d}f}{\mathrm{\;d}t} = \frac{1}{2} \) . 如果用链式法则,有

\[
\frac{\mathrm{d}f}{\mathrm{\;d}t} = {f}_{x}{x}_{t}^{\prime } + {f}_{y}{y}_{t}^{\prime }
\]

当 \( \left( {x, y}\right)  = \left( {0,0}\right) \) 时, \( \frac{\mathrm{d}f}{\mathrm{\;d}t} = 0 \) .

问题出在 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在点 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 不可微,也就是不满足链式法则的条件.

例题 19.3.3 设 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( {\mathbf{R}}^{2} \) 上有连续偏导数,且 \( f\left( {x,{x}^{2}}\right)  \equiv  1 \) .

(1) 若 \( {f}_{x}\left( {x,{x}^{2}}\right)  = x \) ,求 \( {f}_{y}\left( {x,{x}^{2}}\right) \) ;

(2) 若 \( {f}_{y}\left( {x, y}\right)  = {x}^{2} + {2y} \) ,求 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) .

解 (1) 对 \( f\left( {x,{x}^{2}}\right)  \equiv  1 \) 两边求导得

\[
{f}_{x} + {2x}{f}_{y} = 0.
\]

由条件得

\[
x + {2x}{f}_{y} = 0,
\]

所以当 \( x \neq  0 \) 时, \( {f}_{y}\left( {x,{x}^{2}}\right)  =  - \frac{1}{2} \) . 由 \( {f}_{y} \) 的连续性知,当 \( x = 0 \) 时也有 \( {f}_{y}\left( {x,{x}^{2}}\right)  = \; - \frac{1}{2} \) .

( 2 )令 \( F\left( {x, y}\right)  = f\left( {x, y}\right)  - \left( {{x}^{2}y + {y}^{2}}\right) \) ，则 \( F\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( {\mathbf{R}}^{2} \) 上连续可微，且

\[
{F}_{y}\left( {x, y}\right)  = 0.
\]

于是 \( F\left( {x, y}\right) \) 只是 \( x \) 的函数,即 \( F\left( {x, y}\right)  = \varphi \left( x\right) \) ,从而

\[
f\left( {x, y}\right)  = {x}^{2}y + {y}^{2} + \varphi \left( x\right) .
\]

再由 \( f\left( {x,{x}^{2}}\right)  \equiv  1 \) 得 \( \varphi \left( x\right)  = 1 - 2{x}^{4} \) ,所以

\[
f\left( {x, y}\right)  = {x}^{2}y + {y}^{2} + 1 - 2{x}^{4}.
\]

例题 19.3.4 设 \( u = f\left( {x, y}\right) , v = g\left( {x, y, u}\right) , w = h\left( {x, u, v}\right) \) ,求 \( \frac{\partial w}{\partial x},\frac{\partial w}{\partial y} \) .

解 复合这几个函数得到

\[
w = h\left\lbrack  {x, f\left( {x, y}\right) , g\left( {x, y, f\left( {x, y}\right) }\right) }\right\rbrack  .
\]

从而

\[
\frac{\partial w}{\partial x} = {h}_{1} + {h}_{2}\frac{\partial f}{\partial x} + {h}_{3}\left( {{g}_{1} + {g}_{3}\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}}\right) ,
\]

\[
\frac{\partial w}{\partial y} = {h}_{2}\frac{\partial f}{\partial y} + {h}_{3}\left( {{g}_{2} + {g}_{3}\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}}\right) .
\]

例题 19.3.5 设二元连续可微函数 \( F \) 在直角坐标下可写为 \( F\left( {x, y}\right)  = f\left( x\right) g\left( y\right) \) , 在极坐标系中可写为 \( F\left( {r\cos \theta , r\sin \theta }\right)  = h\left( r\right) \) . 若 \( F\left( {x, y}\right) \) 无零点,求 \( F\left( {x, y}\right) \) .

解 注意

\[
\frac{\partial F}{\partial \theta } = \frac{\partial F}{\partial x}\frac{\partial x}{\partial \theta } + \frac{\partial F}{\partial y}\frac{\partial y}{\partial \theta }
\]

\[
= {f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) g\left( y\right) \left( {-r\sin \theta }\right)  + f\left( x\right) {g}^{\prime }\left( y\right) r\cos \theta
\]

\[
=  - y{f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) g\left( y\right)  + {xf}\left( x\right) {g}^{\prime }\left( y\right) .
\]

另一方面有

\[
\frac{\partial F}{\partial \theta } = \frac{\partial h\left( r\right) }{\partial \theta } = 0.
\]

于是 \( - y{f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) g\left( y\right)  + {xf}\left( x\right) {g}^{\prime }\left( y\right)  = 0 \) . 当 \( x \neq  0, y \neq  0 \) 时有

\[
\frac{{f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) }{{xf}\left( x\right) } = \frac{{g}^{\prime }\left( y\right) }{{yg}\left( y\right) }. \tag{19.6}
\]

由于上式对任意的 \( x \neq  0, y \neq  0 \) 恒成立,于是

\[
\frac{{f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) }{{xf}\left( x\right) } = \frac{{g}^{\prime }\left( y\right) }{{yg}\left( y\right) } = \lambda , \tag{19.7}
\]

其中 \( \lambda \) 为任一常数. 由 (19.7) 得

\[
\frac{{f}^{\prime }\left( x\right) }{f\left( x\right) } = {\lambda x}
\]

即 \( {\left( \ln f\right) }^{\prime } = {\left( \frac{\lambda }{2}{x}^{2}\right) }^{\prime } \) ,所以

\[
\ln f\left( x\right)  = \frac{\lambda }{2}{x}^{2} + C, \tag{19.8}
\]

其中 \( C \) 为任意常数. 由 (19.8) 得到

\[
f\left( x\right)  = {C}_{1}{\mathrm{e}}^{\frac{\lambda {x}^{2}}{2}},
\]

其中 \( {C}_{1} \) 为任意常数. 由 (19.7) 还可得到

\[
g\left( y\right)  = {C}_{2}{\mathrm{e}}^{\frac{\lambda {y}^{2}}{2}},
\]

其中 \( {C}_{2} \) 为任意常数. 最后得到

\[
F\left( {x, y}\right)  = C{\mathrm{e}}^{\frac{\lambda \left( {{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}}\right) }{2}},
\]

其中 \( C,\lambda \) 为任意常数. \( F\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( x = 0 \) 或 \( y = 0 \) 的值由连续性得到.

例题 19.3.6 设 \( a, b \neq  0, f \) 具有二阶连续偏导数,且

\[
{a}^{2}\frac{{\partial }^{2}f}{\partial {x}^{2}} + {b}^{2}\frac{{\partial }^{2}f}{\partial {y}^{2}} = 0, \tag{19.9}

f\left( {{ax},{bx}}\right)  = {ax}, \tag{19.10}

{f}_{x}\left( {{ax},{bx}}\right)  = b{x}^{2}. \tag{19.11}

\[
a{f}_{x}\left( {{ax},{bx}}\right)  + b{f}_{y}\left( {{ax},{bx}}\right)  = a. \tag{19.12}

\[
a{f}_{xx}\left( {{ax},{bx}}\right)  + b{f}_{xy}\left( {{ax},{bx}}\right)  = {2bx}, \tag{19.13}

{a}^{2}{f}_{xx}\left( {{ax},{bx}}\right)  + {2ab}{f}_{xy}\left( {{ax},{bx}}\right)  + {b}^{2}{f}_{yy}\left( {{ax},{bx}}\right)  = 0. \tag{19.14}
\]

由 (19.9), (19.14) 得

{f}_{xy}\left( {{ax},{bx}}\right)  = 0. \tag{19.15}
\]

\[
{f}_{xx}\left( {{ax},{bx}}\right)  = \frac{2b}{a}x. \tag{19.16}
\]

最后将 (19.16) 代入 (19.9) 得

\[
{f}_{yy}\left( {{ax},{bx}}\right)  =  - \frac{2a}{b}x.
