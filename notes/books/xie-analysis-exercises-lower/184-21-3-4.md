#### 21.3.4 练习题

1. 设 \( u\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 的邻域上具有连续的二阶偏导数,证明: 当 \( h \rightarrow  0 \) 时有

\[
\frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {x}^{2}}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right)  + \frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {y}^{2}}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right)  = \frac{1}{{h}^{2}}\left\lbrack  {u\left( {{x}_{0} + h,{y}_{0}}\right)  + u\left( {{x}_{0} - h,{y}_{0}}\right) }\right.
\]

\[
+ u\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0} + h}\right)  + u\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0} - h}\right)  - {4u}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \rbrack  + o\left( 1\right) .
\]

2. 对于下列函数,点 \( \left( {0,0}\right) \) 是否为驻点? 是否为极值点?

(1) \( f\left( {x, y}\right)  = {x}^{2} - {4xy} + 5{y}^{2} - 1 \)

(2) \( f\left( {x, y}\right)  = \sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}} \)

(3) \( f\left( {x, y}\right)  = {\left( x + y\right) }^{2} - {y}^{2} \) .

3. 求下列函数的极值点及相应的极值:

(1) \( u\left( {x, y}\right)  = {x}^{2}{\left( y - 1\right) }^{2} \)

(2) \( u\left( {x, y}\right)  = 3{x}^{2}y - {x}^{4} - 2{y}^{2} \)

(3) \( u\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left( {1 + {\mathrm{e}}^{y}}\right) \cos x - y{\mathrm{e}}^{y} \) .

4. 求 \( f\left( {x, y}\right)  = \sin x\sin y\sin \left( {x + y}\right) \) 在 \( D = \{ \left( {x, y}\right)  \mid  x \geq  0, y \geq  0, x + y \leq  \pi \} \) 上的最大、最小值.

5. 证明: 函数 \( f\left( {x, y}\right)  = {x}^{3} - 4{x}^{2} + {2xy} - {y}^{2} \) 在 \( {\mathbf{R}}^{2} \) 上有惟一的极大值点,但该极大值点不是最大值点.
