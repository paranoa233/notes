## \( \text{ § }{21.1} \) 偏导数在几何上的应用

#### 21.1.1 曲线的切向量、切线与法平面

设空间曲线 \( l \) 在点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right) \) 处的切向量为

\[
\tau  = \left( {{\tau }_{x},{\tau }_{y},{\tau }_{z}}\right) ,
\]

则曲线 \( l \) 在点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right) \) 的切线方程与法平面方程分别是

\[
\frac{x - {x}_{0}}{{\tau }_{x}} = \frac{y - {y}_{0}}{{\tau }_{y}} = \frac{z - {z}_{0}}{{\tau }_{z}}
\]

\[
{\tau }_{x}\left( {x - {x}_{0}}\right)  + {\tau }_{y}\left( {y - {y}_{0}}\right)  + {\tau }_{z}\left( {z - {z}_{0}}\right)  = 0.
\]

如果空间曲线 \( l \) 的参数方程是

\[
x = x\left( t\right) ,\;y = y\left( t\right) ,\;z = z\left( t\right) ,\;a \leq  t \leq  b,
\]

其中 \( t \) 是参数. 又设 \( {x}^{\prime }\left( t\right) ,{y}^{\prime }\left( t\right) ,{z}^{\prime }\left( t\right) \) 都在 \( \left\lbrack  {a, b}\right\rbrack \) 上连续,并且不同时为 0,这样的曲线称为光滑曲线. 这时曲线上点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right)  = \left( {x\left( {t}_{0}\right) , y\left( {t}_{0}\right) , z\left( {t}_{0}\right) }\right) \) 的切向量为

\[
\mathbf{\tau } = \left( {{x}^{\prime }\left( {t}_{0}\right) ,{y}^{\prime }\left( {t}_{0}\right) ,{z}^{\prime }\left( {t}_{0}\right) }\right) .
\]

如果空间曲线 \( l \) 是用两个曲面

\[
F\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0,\;G\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0
\]

的交线来表示的,又设 \( F \) 和 \( G \) 关于 \( x, y, z \) 有连续的偏导数. 点 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right) \) 满足这一方程组,即 \( F\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right)  = 0, G\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right)  = 0 \) ,并且 \( F, G \) 的 Jacobi 矩阵

\[
\left( \begin{array}{lll} \frac{\partial F}{\partial x} & \frac{\partial F}{\partial y} & \frac{\partial F}{\partial z} \\  \frac{\partial G}{\partial x} & \frac{\partial G}{\partial y} & \frac{\partial G}{\partial z} \end{array}\right)
\]

在点 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) 的秩为 2,则曲线 \( l \) 在点 \( {\mathbf{p}}_{0} \) 的切向量为

\[
\mathbf{\tau } = {\left. \left( \frac{\partial \left( {F, G}\right) }{\partial \left( {y, z}\right) },\frac{\partial \left( {F, G}\right) }{\partial \left( {z, x}\right) },\frac{\partial \left( {F, G}\right) }{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) }\right) \right| }_{{\mathbf{p}}_{0}}.
\]

例题 21.1.1 设 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 为可微函数,曲线方程为

\[
z = f\left( {x, y}\right) ,\;\frac{x - {x}_{0}}{\cos \alpha } = \frac{y - {y}_{0}}{\sin \alpha }.
\]

求曲线上点 \( {\mathbf{M}}_{0}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right) \) 的切线与 \( {xOy} \) 平面所成角的正切.

解 令 \( F\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = z - f\left( {x, y}\right) , G\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = \sin \alpha \left( {x - {x}_{0}}\right)  - \cos \alpha \left( {y - {y}_{0}}\right) \) ,则

\[
\frac{\partial \left( {F, G}\right) }{\partial \left( {y, z}\right) } = \left| \begin{matrix}  - {f}_{y} & 1 \\   - \cos \alpha & 0 \end{matrix}\right|  = \cos \alpha ,\;\frac{\partial \left( {F, G}\right) }{\partial \left( {z, x}\right) } = \left| \begin{matrix} 1 &  - {f}_{x} \\  0 & \sin \alpha  \end{matrix}\right|  = \sin \alpha ,
\]

\[
\frac{\partial \left( {F, G}\right) }{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) } = \left| \begin{matrix}  - {f}_{x} &  - {f}_{y} \\  \sin \alpha &  - \cos \alpha  \end{matrix}\right|  = {f}_{x}\cos \alpha  + {f}_{y}\sin \alpha .
\]

于是曲线上点 \( {\mathbf{M}}_{0}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0},{z}_{0}}\right) \) 的切线与 \( z \) 轴正方向所成夹角 \( \varphi \) 的余弦为

\[
\cos \varphi  = \frac{{f}_{x}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \cos \alpha  + {f}_{y}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \sin \alpha }{\sqrt{1 + {\left\lbrack  {f}_{x}\left( {x}_{0},{y}_{0}\right) \cos \alpha  + {f}_{y}\left( {x}_{0},{y}_{0}\right) \sin \alpha \right\rbrack  }^{2}}}.
\]

由此可计算出切线与 \( {xOy} \) 平面所成角的正切为

\[
{f}_{x}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \cos \alpha  + {f}_{y}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \sin \alpha .
\]

#### 21.1.2 曲面的法向量、法线与切平面

光滑曲面 \( z = f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 在点 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 处的法向量为

\[
\mathbf{n} =  \pm  \left( {{f}_{x}\left( {x, y}\right) ,{f}_{y}\left( {x, y}\right) , - 1}\right) .
\]

这里所说的光滑是指偏导数连续.

由 \( F\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0 \) 确定的光滑曲面在 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 处的法向量为

\[
\mathbf{n} =  \pm  \left( {{F}_{x}\left( {x, y, z}\right) ,{F}_{y}\left( {x, y, z}\right) ,{F}_{z}\left( {x, y, z}\right) }\right) ,
\]

其中 \( {F}_{x},{F}_{y},{F}_{z} \) 不同时为零.

由参数方程

\[
x = x\left( {u, v}\right) ,\;y = y\left( {u, v}\right) ,\;z = z\left( {u, v}\right)
\]

决定的光滑曲面在点 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 的法向量为

\[
\mathbf{n} =  \pm  \left( {\frac{\partial \left( {y, z}\right) }{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) },\frac{\partial \left( {z, x}\right) }{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) },\frac{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) }{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) }}\right) .
\]

有了法向量之后，过曲面上任一点的法线方程和切平面方程都很容易写出来.

例题 21.1.2 求曲面

\[
x = u + v,\;y = {u}^{2} + {v}^{2},\;z = {u}^{3} + {v}^{3}
\]

的切平面当切点 \( \mathbf{M}\left( {u, v}\right) , u \neq  v \) ,趋于曲面的边界 \( u = v \) 上的点 \( {\mathbf{M}}_{0}\left( {{u}_{0},{v}_{0}}\right) \) 时的极限位置.

解 先求 \( u \neq  v \) 时的切平面方程. 由于

\[
\frac{\partial \left( {y, z}\right) }{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) } = \left| \begin{matrix} {2u} & {2v} \\  3{u}^{2} & 3{v}^{2} \end{matrix}\right|  = {6uv}\left( {v - u}\right) ,\;\frac{\partial \left( {z, x}\right) }{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) } = \left| \begin{matrix} 3{u}^{2} & 3{v}^{2} \\  1 & 1 \end{matrix}\right|  = 3\left( {{u}^{2} - {v}^{2}}\right) ,
\]

\[
\frac{\partial \left( {x, y}\right) }{\partial \left( {u, v}\right) } = \left| \begin{matrix} 1 & 1 \\  {2u} & {2v} \end{matrix}\right|  = 2\left( {v - u}\right) ,
\]

于是过 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 点的切平面方程为

\[
{6uv}\left( {v - u}\right) \left( {X - \left( {u + v}\right) }\right)  + 3\left( {{u}^{2} - {v}^{2}}\right) \left( {Y - \left( {{u}^{2} + {v}^{2}}\right) }\right)
\]

\[
+ 2\left( {v - u}\right) \left( {Z - \left( {{u}^{3} + {v}^{3}}\right) }\right)  = 0.
\]

消去 \( v - u \) ,并整理得到

\[
{6uvX} - 3\left( {u + v}\right) Y + {2Z} = {3uv}\left( {u + v}\right)  - {u}^{3} - {v}^{3}.
\]

令 \( \left( {u, v}\right)  \rightarrow  \left( {{u}_{0},{u}_{0}}\right) \) ,则有

\[
6{u}_{0}^{2}X - 6{u}_{0}Y + {2Z} = 4{u}_{0}^{3}.
\]

将 \( \left( {X, Y, Z}\right) \) 改写为 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) ,可见所求的极限位置为

\[
3{u}_{0}^{2}x - 3{u}_{0}y + z = 2{u}_{0}^{3}.
\]

#### 21.1.3 曲线的夹角、曲面的夹角

由解析几何知 \( {\mathbf{R}}^{2} \) 中两个相交向量 \( \mathbf{l} \) 与 \( \mathbf{m} \) 所交的角 \( \omega \) 的余弦为

\[
\cos \omega  = \frac{\mathbf{l} \cdot  \mathbf{m}}{\left| \mathbf{l}\right|  \cdot  \left| \mathbf{m}\right| }
\]

其中 “.” 为 Euclid 内积, \( \left| \cdot \right| \) 为 Euclid 范数. 设连续可微的隐函数定义的曲线 \( F\left( {x, y}\right)  = 0 \) 与 \( G\left( {x, y}\right)  = 0 \) 在 \( \left( {x, y}\right) \) 处相交. 则在 \( \left( {x, y}\right) \) 处两曲线相应的切向量分别为 \( \left( {{F}_{y}, - {F}_{x}}\right) \) 与 \( \left( {{G}_{y}, - {G}_{x}}\right) \) ,其夹角余弦为

\[
\cos \omega  = \frac{{F}_{x}{G}_{x} + {F}_{y}{G}_{y}}{\sqrt{{F}_{x}^{2} + {F}_{y}^{2}} \cdot  \sqrt{{G}_{x}^{2} + {G}_{y}^{2}}}.
\]

两曲线正交的条件是: \( {F}_{x}{G}_{x} + {F}_{y}{G}_{y} = 0 \) .

在 \( {\mathbf{R}}^{3} \) 中也有类似的讨论. 设 \( F\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0 \) 与 \( G\left( {x, y, z}\right)  = 0 \) 为 \( {\mathbf{R}}^{3} \) 中两光滑曲面. 它们在 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 处相交. 两曲面夹角定义为相应切平面的夹角 (或相应法向量的夹角). 设 \( \omega \) 为其夹角,则有

\[
\cos \omega  = \frac{{F}_{x}{G}_{x} + {F}_{y}{G}_{y} + {F}_{z}{G}_{z}}{\sqrt{{F}_{x}^{2} + {F}_{y}^{2} + {F}_{z}^{2}} \cdot  \sqrt{{G}_{x}^{2} + {G}_{y}^{2} + {G}_{z}^{2}}}.
\]

两曲面正交的条件是: \( {F}_{x}{G}_{x} + {F}_{y}{G}_{y} + {F}_{z}{G}_{z} = 0 \) .

例题 21.1.3 设

\[
\frac{xy}{z} = u,\;\sqrt{{x}^{2} + {z}^{2}} + \sqrt{{y}^{2} + {z}^{2}} = v\text{ 和 }\sqrt{{x}^{2} + {z}^{2}} - \sqrt{{y}^{2} + {z}^{2}} = w
\]

是三个分别以 \( u, v, w \) 为参数的单参数曲面族,证明: 过同一点的三曲面族的三个曲面是两两正交的.

证 设三曲面交于点 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 处,其法向量分别为

\[
\left( {\frac{y}{z},\frac{x}{z}, - \frac{xy}{{z}^{2}}}\right)
\]

\[
\left( {\frac{x}{\sqrt{{x}^{2} + {z}^{2}}},\frac{y}{\sqrt{{y}^{2} + {z}^{2}}},\frac{z}{\sqrt{{x}^{2} + {z}^{2}}} + \frac{z}{\sqrt{{y}^{2} + {z}^{2}}}}\right) ,
\]

\[
\left( {\frac{x}{\sqrt{{x}^{2} + {z}^{2}}}, - \frac{y}{\sqrt{{y}^{2} + {z}^{2}}},\frac{z}{\sqrt{{x}^{2} + {z}^{2}}} - \frac{z}{\sqrt{{y}^{2} + {z}^{2}}}}\right) .
\]

由于这三个向量的两两内积均为 0,所以过 \( \left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 点的这三个曲面族的三个曲面是两两正交的.

#### 21.1.4 练习题

1. 在曲线 \( x = t, y = {t}^{2}, z = {t}^{3} \) 上求出一点,使该点的切线平行于平面 \( x + {2y} + z = 4 \) .

2. 证明: 斜驶线

\[
\tan \left( {\frac{\pi }{4} + \frac{\psi }{2}}\right)  = {\mathrm{e}}^{k\varphi },\;k = \text{ 常数, }
\]

与地球的每一条子午线相交成定角,其中 \( \varphi \) 为地球上点的经度, \( \psi \) 为地球上点的纬度.

3. 求曲线 \( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} + {z}^{2} = 6, x + y + z = 0 \) 在点 \( \left( {1, - 2,1}\right) \) 处的切线与法平面方程.

4. 求曲面 \( z = \arctan \frac{y}{x} \) 在点 \( \left( {1,1,\frac{\pi }{4}}\right) \) 处的法线与切平面方程.

5. 证明: 曲面 \( {xyz} = {a}^{3}\left( {a > 0}\right) \) 的每一个切平面与坐标面形成体积相同的四面体.
