#### 25.1.1 第一型曲面积分的定义和计算

在 22.5.1 小节中我们在假定曲面面积存在的情况下, 推导出了曲面面积的计算公式,下面给出曲面面积的严格定义. 考虑一个以分段光滑的闭曲线 \( L \) 为边界的光滑曲面 \( S \) ,设这一曲面被一个分段光滑的曲线网分成许多部分 \( {S}_{1},{S}_{2},\cdots ,{S}_{m} \) , 并在每一部分 \( {S}_{i} \) 内任取一点 \( {M}_{i} \) ,把元素 \( {S}_{i} \) 垂直地投影到曲面在点 \( {M}_{i} \) 处的切平面上,得到在切平面内的平面图形 \( {T}_{i} \) ,其面积 \( \Delta {T}_{i}\left( {i = 1,2,\cdots , m}\right) \) 的和在各个元素 \( {S}_{i} \) 的直径趋于零时的极限

\[
\mathop{\lim }\limits_{{\lambda  \rightarrow  0}}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{i}\Delta {T}_{i}
\]

称为曲面 \( S \) 的面积,其中 \( \lambda  = \mathop{\max }\limits_{{1 \leq  i \leq  m}}\left\{  {d\left( {S}_{i}\right) }\right\} \) 为 \( {S}_{i}\left( {i = 1,2,\cdots , m}\right) \) 中的最大直径. 如果极限为有限数,则称曲面 \( S \) 为可求面积的.

设 \( S \) 是 \( {\mathbf{R}}^{3} \) 中的可求面积的曲面,函数 \( f \) 在 \( S \) 上有定义. 对于 \( S \) 的任一分割 \( T : \left\{  {{S}_{1},{S}_{2},\cdots ,{S}_{m}}\right\} \) ,用 \( \Delta {S}_{i} \) 表示 \( {S}_{i} \) 的面积,任取 \( \left( {{\xi }_{i},{\eta }_{i},{\zeta }_{i}}\right)  \in  {S}_{i} \) ,如果当 \( d\left( T\right)  = \mathop{\max }\limits_{{1 \leq  i \leq  m}}\left\{  {d\left( {S}_{i}\right) }\right\}   \rightarrow  0 \) 时,和数

\[
\mathop{\sum }\limits_{i}f\left( {{\xi }_{i},{\eta }_{i},{\zeta }_{i}}\right) \Delta {S}_{i}
\]

收敛,且极限不依赖于 \( S \) 的具体分割与 \( \left( {{\xi }_{i},{\eta }_{i},{\zeta }_{i}}\right)  \in  {S}_{i} \) 的具体选择,则称函数 \( f \) 在 \( S \) 上的第一型曲面积分存在,该极限值称为 \( f\left( {x, y, z}\right) \) 在 \( S \) 上的第一型曲面积分, 记为

\[
{\iint }_{S}f\left( {x, y, z}\right) \mathrm{d}S = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{d\left( T\right)  \rightarrow  0}}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{i}f\left( {{\xi }_{i},{\eta }_{i},{\zeta }_{i}}\right) \Delta {S}_{i}.
\]

如果曲面 \( S \) 的参数方程是

\[
x = x\left( {u, v}\right) ,\;y = y\left( {u, v}\right) ,\;z = z\left( {u, v}\right) ,\;\left( {u, v}\right)  \in  D,
\]

\( D \) 是 \( {uOv} \) 平面上可求面积的区域, \( x\left( {u, v}\right) , y\left( {u, v}\right) , z\left( {u, v}\right) \) 在 \( D \) 上有连续偏导数,则

\[
\mathrm{d}S = \sqrt{{EG} - {F}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}u\mathrm{\;d}v
\]

其中

\[
E = {x}_{u}^{2} + {y}_{u}^{2} + {z}_{u}^{2}
\]

\[
F = {x}_{u}{x}_{v} + {y}_{u}{y}_{v} + {z}_{u}{z}_{v}
\]

\[
G = {x}_{v}^{2} + {y}_{v}^{2} + {z}_{v}^{2}
\]

且

\[
{\iint }_{S}f\left( {x, y, z}\right) \mathrm{d}S = {\iint }_{D}f\left( {x\left( {u, v}\right) , y\left( {u, v}\right) , z\left( {u, v}\right) }\right) \sqrt{{EG} - {F}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}u\mathrm{\;d}v. \tag{25.1}
\]

特别地,若曲面 \( S \) 的方程为

\[
z = z\left( {x, y}\right) ,\;\left( {x, y}\right)  \in  D,
\]

则

\[
{\iint }_{S}f\left( {x, y, z}\right) \mathrm{d}S = {\iint }_{D}f\left( {x, y, z\left( {x, y}\right) }\right) \sqrt{1 + {z}_{x}^{2} + {z}_{y}^{2}}\mathrm{\;d}x\mathrm{\;d}y. \tag{25.2}
\]

例题 25.1.1 设 \( S \) 为 \( z = \sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}} \) 被 \( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2} = {2ax} \) 割下的部分,求

\[
I = {\iint }_{S}\left( {{x}^{2}{y}^{2} + {y}^{2}{z}^{2} + {z}^{2}{x}^{2}}\right) \mathrm{d}S.
\]

解 1 见图 25.1, 在直角坐标系中计算

\[
{z}_{x} = \frac{x}{z},\;{z}_{y} = \frac{y}{z}
\]

\[
\sqrt{1 + {z}_{x}^{2} + {z}_{y}^{2}} = \sqrt{2}
\]

\[
I = {\iint }_{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} \leq  {2ax}}\left\lbrack  {{x}^{2}{y}^{2} + {\left( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2}\right) }^{2}}\right\rbrack  \sqrt{2}\mathrm{\;d}x\mathrm{\;d}y.
\]

用极坐标变换求上述二重积分, 则

\[
I = \sqrt{2}{\int }_{-\pi /2}^{\pi /2}\mathrm{\;d}\theta {\int }_{0}^{{2a}\cos \theta }\left( {{r}^{4}{\cos }^{2}\theta {\sin }^{2}\theta  + {r}^{4}}\right) r\mathrm{\;d}r
\]

\[
= \sqrt{2}{\int }_{-\pi /2}^{\pi /2}\left( {{\cos }^{2}\theta {\sin }^{2}\theta  + 1}\right)  \cdot  \left( {\left. \frac{1}{6}{r}^{6}\right| }_{0}^{{2a}\cos \theta }\right) \mathrm{d}\theta
\]

\[
= \frac{\sqrt{2}}{6}{\left( 2a\right) }^{6}{\int }_{-\pi /2}^{\pi /2}{\cos }^{6}\theta \left( {{\cos }^{2}\theta {\sin }^{2}\theta  + 1}\right) \mathrm{d}\theta  = \frac{29}{8}\sqrt{2}\pi {a}^{6}.
\]

解 2 用参数式计算. \( z = \sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}} \) 在球坐标系中的方程为 \( \varphi  = \frac{\pi }{4} \) ,因此 \( S \) 的参数方程为

\[
x = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}r\cos \theta ,\;y = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}r\sin \theta ,\;z = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}r,\;\left( {r,\theta }\right)  \in  D.
\]

又 \( S \) 的边界线

\[
z = \sqrt{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}},\;{x}^{2} + {y}^{2} = {2ax}
\]

的球坐标表示为

\[
\varphi  = \frac{\pi }{4},\;{r}^{2}{\sin }^{2}\varphi  = {2ar}\sin \varphi \cos \theta .
\]

于是

\[
D = \left\{  {\left( {r,\theta }\right) \left| {\; - \frac{\pi }{2} \leq  \theta  \leq  \frac{\pi }{2}}\right. ,0 \leq  r \leq  2\sqrt{2}a\cos \theta }\right\}  .
\]

计算得

\[
E = \frac{{r}^{2}}{2}, F = 0, G = 1,
\]

最后得到

\[
I = {\int }_{-\pi /2}^{\pi /2}\mathrm{\;d}\theta {\int }_{0}^{2\sqrt{2}a\cos \theta }\left( {\frac{1}{4}{r}^{4}{\cos }^{2}\theta {\sin }^{2}\theta  + \frac{1}{4}{r}^{4}}\right) \frac{r}{\sqrt{2}}\mathrm{\;d}r
\]

\[
= \frac{29}{8}\sqrt{2}\pi {a}^{6}\text{ . }
\]
