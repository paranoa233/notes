#### 19.1.3 高阶偏导数

就二元函数而论,若 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 的两个偏导数 \( {f}_{x}\left( {x, y}\right) \) 和 \( {f}_{y}\left( {x, y}\right) \) 都存在,则它们都是二元函数. 如果它们关于 \( x \) 的偏导数存在或者关于 \( y \) 的偏导数存在,就称这些偏导数是 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 的二阶偏导数:

\( f \) 关于 \( x \) 的二阶偏导数,记为 \( \frac{{\partial }^{2}f}{\partial {x}^{2}} \) 或 \( {f}_{xx} \) ;

\( f \) 关于 \( y \) 的二阶偏导数,记为 \( \frac{{\partial }^{2}f}{\partial {y}^{2}} \) 或 \( {f}_{yy} \) ;

\( f \) 先关于 \( x \) 后关于 \( y \) 的二阶混合偏导数,记为 \( \frac{{\partial }^{2}f}{\partial y\partial x} \) 或 \( {f}_{xy} \) ;

\( f \) 先关于 \( y \) 后关于 \( x \) 的二阶混合偏导数,记为 \( \frac{{\partial }^{2}f}{\partial x\partial y} \) 或 \( {f}_{yx} \) .

更高阶的偏导数也可以同样定义. 请注意两个混合偏导数 \( \frac{{\partial }^{2}f}{\partial y\partial x} \) 与 \( \frac{{\partial }^{2}f}{\partial x\partial y} \) 并不总是相等的, 例如设

\[
f\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left\{  \begin{array}{ll} {xy} \cdot  \frac{{x}^{2} - {y}^{2}}{{x}^{2} + {y}^{2}}, & \left( {x, y}\right)  \neq  \left( {0,0}\right) , \\  0, & \left( {x, y}\right)  = \left( {0,0}\right) . \end{array}\right.
\]

则有

\[
{f}_{x}\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left\{  \begin{array}{ll} y \cdot  \frac{{x}^{4} + 4{x}^{2}{y}^{2} - {y}^{4}}{{\left( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2}\right) }^{2}}, & \left( {x, y}\right)  \neq  \left( {0,0}\right) , \\  0, & \left( {x, y}\right)  = \left( {0,0}\right) , \end{array}\right.
\]

\[
{f}_{y}\left( {x, y}\right)  = \left\{  \begin{array}{ll} x \cdot  \frac{{x}^{4} - 4{x}^{2}{y}^{2} - {y}^{4}}{{\left( {x}^{2} + {y}^{2}\right) }^{2}}, & \left( {x, y}\right)  \neq  \left( {0,0}\right) , \\  0, & \left( {x, y}\right)  = \left( {0,0}\right) . \end{array}\right.
\]

从而在原点处有

\[
{f}_{xy}\left( {0,0}\right)  = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{{\Delta y} \rightarrow  0}}\frac{{f}_{x}\left( {0,{\Delta y}}\right)  - {f}_{x}\left( {0,0}\right) }{\Delta y} =  - 1,
\]

\[
{f}_{yx}\left( {0,0}\right)  = \mathop{\lim }\limits_{{{\Delta x} \rightarrow  0}}\frac{{f}_{y}\left( {{\Delta x},0}\right)  - {f}_{y}\left( {0,0}\right) }{\Delta x} = 1.
\]

由此可见

\[
{f}_{yx}\left( {0,0}\right)  \neq  {f}_{xy}\left( {0,0}\right) \text{ . }
\]

注 上面定义的函数 \( f\left( {x, y}\right) \) 连续可微 (关于可微的定义见下一小节)，因为 \( {f}_{x} \) 和 \( {f}_{y} \) 都是处处连续的. 但如果混合偏导数 \( {f}_{yx} \) 和 \( {f}_{xy} \) 连续,则构造上述反例是不可能的. 事实上,如果在某一点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 的邻域内 \( {f}_{x},{f}_{y} \) 都存在,且 \( {f}_{yx} \) 与 \( {f}_{xy} \) 都在该点连续,那么在点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 一定成立 \( {f}_{yx} = {f}_{xy} \) . 在一般的分析教科书上都有这个结论及其证明. 我们可以证明下述更强的结论.

命题 19.1.2 设 \( {f}_{x},{f}_{y} \) 在点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 的某邻域内存在且 \( {f}_{xy} \) 在点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 连续, 则 \( {f}_{yx}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 存在,且

\[
{f}_{yx}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right)  = {f}_{xy}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) .
\]

证 定义

\[
\varphi \left( x\right)  = f\left( {x,{y}_{0} + {\Delta y}}\right)  - f\left( {x,{y}_{0}}\right) .
\]

由于函数 \( f \) 存在关于 \( x \) 的偏导数,所以 \( \varphi \left( x\right) \) 可导,应用一元函数的中值定理,有

\[
\varphi \left( {{x}_{0} + {\Delta x}}\right)  - \varphi \left( {x}_{0}\right)  = {\varphi }^{\prime }\left( {{x}_{0} + {\theta }_{1}{\Delta x}}\right) {\Delta x}
\]

\[
= \left\lbrack  {{f}_{x}\left( {{x}_{0} + {\theta }_{1}{\Delta x},{y}_{0} + {\Delta y}}\right)  - {f}_{x}\left( {{x}_{0} + {\theta }_{1}{\Delta x},{y}_{0}}\right) }\right\rbrack  {\Delta x},
\]

其中 \( 0 < {\theta }_{1} < 1 \) ,又由 \( {f}_{x} \) 存在关于 \( y \) 的偏导数,故对以 \( y \) 为自变量的函数 \( {f}_{x}\left( {{x}_{0} + {\theta }_{1}{\Delta x}, y}\right) \) 应用一元函数中值定理,上式化为

\[
\varphi \left( {{x}_{0} + {\Delta x}}\right)  - \varphi \left( {x}_{0}\right)  = {f}_{xy}\left( {{x}_{0} + {\theta }_{1}{\Delta x},{y}_{0} + {\theta }_{2}{\Delta y}}\right) {\Delta x\Delta y},
\]

其中 \( 0 < {\theta }_{1},{\theta }_{2} < 1 \) . 因此

\[
f\left( {{x}_{0} + {\Delta x},{y}_{0} + {\Delta y}}\right)  - f\left( {{x}_{0} + {\Delta x},{y}_{0}}\right)  - f\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0} + {\Delta y}}\right)  + f\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right)
\]

\[
= {f}_{xy}\left( {{x}_{0} + {\theta }_{1}{\Delta x},{y}_{0} + {\theta }_{2}{\Delta y}}\right) {\Delta x\Delta y}.
\]

在上式两边同除以 \( {\Delta y} \) ,并令 \( {\Delta y} \rightarrow  0 \) ,由偏导数的定义以及 \( {f}_{xy} \) 在点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 连续就得到

\[
{f}_{y}\left( {{x}_{0} + {\Delta x},{y}_{0}}\right)  - {f}_{y}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right)  = {f}_{xy}\left( {{x}_{0} + {\theta }_{1}{\Delta x},{y}_{0}}\right) {\Delta x}.
\]

再在上式两边同除以 \( {\Delta x} \) ,并令 \( {\Delta x} \rightarrow  0 \) ,由 \( {f}_{xy} \) 在点 \( \left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) \) 连续得到

\[
{f}_{yx}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right)  = {f}_{xy}\left( {{x}_{0},{y}_{0}}\right) .
\]

例题 19.1.2 证明: 函数 \( u\left( {x, t}\right)  = \frac{1}{{2a}\sqrt{\pi t}}{\mathrm{e}}^{-\frac{{x}^{2}}{4{a}^{2}t}} \) 在上半平面 \( {\mathbf{R}}_{ + }^{2} = \{ \left( {x, t}\right)  \in \; \left. {{\mathbf{R}}^{2} \mid  t > 0}\right\} \) 上满足热传导方程

\[
\frac{\partial u}{\partial t} - {a}^{2}\frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {x}^{2}} = 0, \tag{19.1}
\]

其中 \( a \) 为正常数.

证 \( \forall \left( {x, t}\right)  \in  {\mathbf{R}}_{ + }^{2} \) ,由于

\[
\frac{\partial u}{\partial t}\left( {x, t}\right)  = \frac{1}{{2a}\sqrt{\pi }}\left( {-\frac{1}{2{t}^{3/2}}{\mathrm{e}}^{-\frac{{x}^{2}}{4{a}^{2}t}} + \frac{1}{{t}^{1/2}}{\mathrm{e}}^{-\frac{{x}^{2}}{4{a}^{2}t}}\frac{{x}^{2}}{4{a}^{2}{t}^{2}}}\right)
\]

\[
= \frac{1}{{4a}\sqrt{\pi }{t}^{3/2}}{\mathrm{e}}^{-\frac{{x}^{2}}{4{a}^{2}t}}\left( {-1 + \frac{{x}^{2}}{2{a}^{2}t}}\right) ,
\]

\[
\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\left( {x, t}\right)  = \frac{1}{{2a}\sqrt{\pi t}}{\mathrm{e}}^{-\frac{{x}^{2}}{4{a}^{2}t}}\left( {-\frac{x}{2{a}^{2}t}}\right) ,
\]

\[
\frac{{\partial }^{2}u}{\partial {x}^{2}}\left( {x, t}\right)  = \frac{1}{{2a}\sqrt{\pi t}}\left( {{\mathrm{e}}^{-\frac{{x}^{2}}{4{a}^{2}t}}\frac{{x}^{2}}{4{a}^{4}{t}^{2}} - \frac{1}{2{a}^{2}t}{\mathrm{e}}^{-\frac{{x}^{2}}{4{a}^{2}t}}}\right)
\]

\[
= \frac{1}{4{a}^{3}\sqrt{\pi }{t}^{3/2}}{\mathrm{e}}^{-\frac{{x}^{2}}{4{a}^{2}t}}\left( {\frac{{x}^{2}}{2{a}^{2}t} - 1}\right) ,
\]

所以方程 (19.1) 在 \( {\mathbf{R}}_{ + }^{2} \) 上成立.

例题 19.1.3 设 \( \Gamma \left( {x, y;\xi ,\eta }\right)  = \frac{1}{2\pi }\ln \sqrt{{\left( x - \xi \right) }^{2} + {\left( y - \eta \right) }^{2}} \) ,则

\[
\left| \frac{\partial \Gamma }{\partial y}\right|  \leq  \frac{1}{2\pi }{\left\lbrack  {\left( x - \xi \right) }^{2} + {\left( y - \eta \right) }^{2}\right\rbrack  }^{-\frac{1}{2}},
\]

\[
\left| \frac{{\partial }^{2}T}{\partial x\partial y}\right|  \leq  \frac{1}{\pi }{\left\lbrack  {\left( x - \xi \right) }^{2} + {\left( y - \eta \right) }^{2}\right\rbrack  }^{-1}.
\]

证 由于

\[
\frac{\partial \Gamma }{\partial y} = \frac{1}{2\pi }\frac{y - \eta }{{\left( x - \xi \right) }^{2} + {\left( y - \eta \right) }^{2}},
\]

\[
\frac{{\partial }^{2}\Gamma }{\partial x\partial y} =  - \frac{1}{\pi }\frac{\left( {y - \eta }\right) \left( {x - \xi }\right) }{{\left\lbrack  {\left( x - \xi \right) }^{2} + {\left( y - \eta \right) }^{2}\right\rbrack  }^{2}},
\]

从而结论成立.
