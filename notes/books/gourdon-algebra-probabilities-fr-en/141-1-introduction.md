### 1. Introduction

On se donne un polynôme \( F = {a}_{0}{X}^{n} + {a}_{1}{X}^{n - 1} + \cdots + {a}_{n} \in \mathbb{K}\left\lbrack X\right\rbrack \) (où \( \mathbb{K} \) est un corps commutatif quelconque), tel que \( {a}_{0} \neq 0 \) et \( n \geq 1 \) . On se propose de résoudre l’équation \( F\left( x\right) = 0 \) . Quitte à diviser le polynôme \( F \) par \( {a}_{0} \) , on peut supposer \( {a}_{0} = 1 \) . Enfin, en effectuant le changement de variable \( x = z - {a}_{1}/n \) , on se ramène au cas où le coefficient de \( {X}^{n - 1} \) est nul. Finalement, on est amené à résoudre une équation de la forme

> Let \( F = {a}_{0}{X}^{n} + {a}_{1}{X}^{n - 1} + \cdots + {a}_{n} \in \mathbb{K}\left\lbrack X\right\rbrack \) be a polynomial (where \( \mathbb{K} \) is any commutative field), such that \( {a}_{0} \neq 0 \) and \( n \geq 1 \) . We propose to solve the equation \( F\left( x\right) = 0 \) . By dividing the polynomial \( F \) by \( {a}_{0} \) , we can assume \( {a}_{0} = 1 \) . Finally, by performing the change of variable \( x = z - {a}_{1}/n \) , we reduce it to the case where the coefficient of \( {X}^{n - 1} \) is zero. Ultimately, we are led to solve an equation of the form

\[
{z}^{n} + {a}_{2}{z}^{n - 2} + \cdots  + {a}_{n - 1}z + {a}_{n} = 0.
\]

Dans la suite, le corps de base \( \mathbb{K} \) est le corps des nombres complexes \( \mathbb{C} \) .

> In the following, the base field \( \mathbb{K} \) is the field of complex numbers \( \mathbb{C} \) .
