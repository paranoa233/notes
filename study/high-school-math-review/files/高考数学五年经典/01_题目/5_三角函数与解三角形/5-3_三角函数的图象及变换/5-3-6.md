## 题目

A. \( f\left( x\right)  =  - 2\cos {2x} \)

C. \( f\left( x\right)  = \frac{1}{2}\cos \left( {\frac{2}{3}x - \frac{5\pi }{6}}\right) \)

D. \( f\left( x\right)  = 2\sin \left( {\frac{2}{3}x - \frac{\pi }{6}}\right) \)
