## 题目

7.(2023 天津南开统考二模)设 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 为等比数列, \( \left\{  {b}_{n}\right\} \) 为公差不为零的等差数列,且 \( {a}_{1} = {b}_{3} = \) 3, \( {a}_{2} = {b}_{9},{a}_{3} = {b}_{27} \) .

(1)求 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 和 \( \left\{  {b}_{n}\right\} \) 的通项公式；

(2)记 \( \left\{  {a}_{n}\right\} \) 的前 \( n \) 项和为 \( {S}_{n},\left\{  {b}_{n}\right\} \) 的前 \( n \) 项和为 \( {T}_{n} \) ,求证: \( \frac{{T}_{n}}{{S}_{n}} \leq  \frac{1}{3} \) ;

(3) 记 \( {c}_{n} = \left\{  \begin{array}{l} \frac{{a}_{\frac{n + 1}{2}}}{{b}_{n} + 2}, n\text{ 为奇数, } \\   - \frac{{a}_{\frac{n}{2}}}{\left( {{b}_{n} - 1}\right) \left( {{b}_{n} + 1}\right) }, n\text{ 为偶数, } \end{array}\right. \)
