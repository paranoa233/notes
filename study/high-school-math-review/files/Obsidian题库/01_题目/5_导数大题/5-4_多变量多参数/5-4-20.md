(2)求证: \( f\left( x\right)  < {\mathrm{e}}^{-x} + \frac{1}{{x}^{2}} - \frac{2}{x} - 1 \) ;

(3)若 \( p > 0, q > 0 \) 且 \( {pq} > 1 \) ，求证: \( f\left( p\right)  + f\left( q\right)  <  - 4 \) .
