## 题目

1. 二元柯西不等式: \( \left( {{a}^{2} + {b}^{2}}\right) \left( {{c}^{2} + {d}^{2}}\right)  \geq  {\left( ac + bd\right) }^{2} \) ,当且仅当 \( {ad} = {bc} \) 时,等号成立.

证明:

① 多元柯西不等式: \( \left( {{a}_{1}^{2} + {a}_{2}^{2} + \cdots  + {a}_{n}^{2}}\right) \left( {{b}_{1}^{2} + {b}_{2}^{2} + \cdots  + {b}_{n}^{2}}\right)  \geq  \left( {{a}_{1}{b}_{1} + {a}_{2}{b}_{2} + \cdots  + }\right. \; {\left. {a}_{n}{b}_{n}\right) }^{2}\left( {\mathrm{n} \geq  3,\mathrm{n} \in  \mathbf{N}}\right) \) ,当且仅当 \( \frac{{a}_{1}}{{b}_{1}} = \frac{{a}_{2}}{{b}_{2}} = \cdots  = \frac{{a}_{n}}{{b}_{n}} \) 时,等号成立

② 当 \( a, b > 0 \) 时， \( \left( {a + b}\right) \left( {\frac{1}{a} + \frac{1}{b}}\right)  \geq  4 \) 成立.

(2004 湖南高考)设 \( \mathrm{a} > 0,\mathrm{\;b} > 0 \) ，则以下不等式中不恒成立的是( )

A. \( \left( {a + b}\right) \left( {\frac{1}{a} + \frac{1}{b}}\right)  \geq  4 \) B. \( {a}^{3} + {b}^{3} \geq  {2a}{b}^{2} \)

C. \( {a}^{2} + {b}^{2} + 2 \geq  {2a} + {2b} \) D. \( \sqrt{\left| a - b\right| } \geq  \sqrt{a} - \sqrt{b} \)
