## 题目

6. (2024 河北二模)若 \( \bigtriangleup {ABC} \) 内一点 \( P \) 满足 \( \angle {PAB} = \angle {PBC} = \angle {PCA} = \theta \) ，则称点 \( P \) 为 \( \bigtriangleup {ABC} \) 的布洛卡点, \( \theta \) 为 \( \bigtriangleup {ABC} \) 的布洛卡角. 如图,已知 \( \bigtriangleup {ABC} \) 中, \( {BC} = a \) , \( {AC} = b,{AB} = c \) ,点 \( P \) 为 \( \bigtriangleup {ABC} \) 的布洛卡点, \( \theta \) 为 \( \bigtriangleup {ABC} \) 的布洛卡角.

![[05_附件/images/558_637_410_370_356_0.jpg]]

(1)若 \( b = c \) ，且满足 \( \frac{PB}{PA} = \sqrt{3} \) ，求 \( \angle {ABC} \) 的大小.

(2)若 \( \bigtriangleup  {ABC} \) 为锐角三角形.

(i) 证明: \( \frac{1}{\tan \theta } = \frac{1}{\tan \angle {BAC}} + \frac{1}{\tan \angle {ABC}} + \frac{1}{\tan \angle {ACB}} \) .

(ii) 若 \( {PB} \) 平分 \( \angle {ABC} \) ，证明: \( {b}^{2} = {ac} \) .
