## 题目

2. \( \min ,\max \) 函数

(1)定义:

\( \min \{ a, b\}  = a, b \) 两数中的较小者 \( = \left\{  \begin{array}{l} a, a \leq  b, \\  b, a > b. \end{array}\right. \)

\( \max \{ a, b\}  = a, b \) 两数中的较大者 \( = \left\{  \begin{array}{l} b, a \leq  b, \\  a, a > b. \end{array}\right. \)

因此, \( \min ,\max \) 本质上是分段函数.

(2)例: \( \min \{ \pi ,\mathrm{e}\}  = \) ___， \( \max \{ {x}^{2},{2x} - 1\}  = \) ___.

(3)画出下列函数的图像.

① \( f\left( x\right)  = \min \left\{  {{\mathrm{e}}^{x}, x + 2}\right\} \) ；

② \( g\left( x\right)  = \max \{ \sin {2x},\left| x\right| \} \) .

(4)重要的性质

① \( \min \{ a, b\}  \leq  a,\min \{ a, b\}  \leq  b,\max \{ a, b\}  \geq  a,\max \{ a, b\}  \geq  b \) . 用一句话说明它们成立:

② \( \min \{ a, b\}  \leq  \frac{a + b}{2} \leq  \max \{ a, b\} \) .

用一句话说明它们成立:

③ \( \min \{ a, b\}  + \max \{ a, b\}  = a + b,\max \{ a, b\}  - \min \{ a, b\}  = \left| {b - a}\right| \) . 用一句话说明它们成立:
