A. \( \lbrack 2 - 2\sqrt{2},0) \) B. \( ( - \infty ,2 - 2\sqrt{2}\rbrack \)

C. \( ( - \infty ,2 + 2\sqrt{2}\rbrack \) D. \( (0,2 + 2\sqrt{2}\rbrack \)
